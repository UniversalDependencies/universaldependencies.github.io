INTJ: interjection

Definition

An interjection is a word that is used most often as an exclamation or part of an exclamation.

Examples
  • ja “yes”, nei, “no”
  • hei “hi”, hallo “hello”, heisan “hi there”
  • å “oh”
  • ok “okay”
  • piip “peep”
