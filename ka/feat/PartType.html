PartType: particle type

Values: Int Inf Neg Emp

The PartType feature typically applies to particles, pronouns, adpositions, nouns, proper nouns adverbs, adjectives, numerals.

Int: question particle

Examples

  • ნუთუ ‘whether’, იქნებ ‘maybe’ etc.

Inf: infinitive particle

Examples

  • რომელიმე ‘any’, ვინმე ‘anyone’ etc.

Neg: negative particle

Examples

  • არ ‘not’, ვერ ‘not’ etc.

Emp: emphasis particle

Examples

  • აკი ‘but’, მაშ ‘surely’ etc.
