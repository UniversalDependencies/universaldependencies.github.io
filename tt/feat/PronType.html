PronType: pronominal type

This feature typically applies to pronouns, determiners, pronominal numerals (quantifiers) and pronominal adverbs.

Prs: personal

Examples

  • мин “I”
  • син “you (sg.)”
  • сез “you (pl.)”

Rcp: reciprocal pronoun

This value covers reciprocal pronoun бер-бере and its inflected forms.

Examples

  • Без бер-беребезгә бик нык ышанабыз “We believe in each other very firmly”
  • Алар бер-берсенә кунакка йөрешәләр “They visit each other”

Int: interrogative pronoun, determiner, numeral or adverb

Examples:

  • кем “who”
  • нәрсә “what”
  • ничә “how many”
  • кемнеке “whose”

Dem: demonstrative pronoun

Examples:

  • бу “this”
  • шул “that/this”
  • ул “that”

Ind: indefinite pronoun

Examples:

  • кемдер “someone”
  • нәрсәдер “something”
  • берәү “someone”

Neg: negative determiner or pronoun

Examples:

  • беркем, һичкем “no one”
  • бернәрсә, һичнәрсә “nothing”

Tot: collective determiner or pronoun

Examples:

  • һәр кеше “every person”
