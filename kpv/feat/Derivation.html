Derivation: derivation

(Please note: this part of the documentation is not yet completed.)

Language-specific feature identifying the form of derivation applied to a word.

Al: derivation with suffix -ал

Produces verbs with meaning of irregular frequency.

Examples

  • [kpv] ветлыны: ветлалӧ “go/walk : walk around from time to time”

An: derivation with suffix -ан

Produces adnominal deverbal noun modifier / noun.

Examples

  • [kpv] кутан “holding”
  • [kpv] сёян “food”
  • [kpv] чужан “birth”

Ana: derivation with suffix -ана

Produces adnominal deverbal adjective modifier / adverb.

Examples

  • [kpv] кутана “holding”

Anin: derivation with suffix -анін

Produces deverbal noun indicating place where activity of the verb is performed

Examples

  • [kpv] сёянін “place for eating”
  • [kpv] вузасянін “place of retail, store”

CompMod: derivation with suffix -кодь

Produces meaning like added to adjectival heads.

Examples

  • [kpv] паськыдкодь “rather extensive”

Ig: derivation with suffix -иг/-іг

Produces converb.

Examples

  • [kpv] вундігӧн “in cutting”

Igdyrji: derivation with suffix -игдырйи/-ігдырйи

Produces converb.

Examples

  • [kpv] горзігдырйи “while shouting”

Igkost: derivation with suffix -игкост/-ігкост

Produces converb.

Examples

  • [kpv] чӧвтігкостаныс “in between keeping silent”

Igkosta: derivation with suffix -игкоста/-ігкоста

Produces converb.

Examples

  • [kpv] _ но ворсны велӧдчигкостаыс_ “but while learning to play in the meantime”

Igkosti: derivation with suffix -игкості/-ігкості

Produces converb, which may be a variant of Igkost.

Examples

  • [kpv] чукӧртчигкості “while gathering”

Igmoz: derivation with suffix -игмоз/-ігмоз

Produces converb.

Examples

  • [kpv] лӧсьӧдігмоз “while preparing”

Igtyr: derivation with suffix -игтыр/-ігтыр

Produces converb.

Examples

  • [kpv] чатрасигтыр “TODO”

Igtyrji: derivation with suffix -игтырйи/-ігтырйи

Produces converb.

Examples

  • [kpv] горзігтырйи “shouting each and everyone”

Ik: derivation with suffix -ик

Produces diminutive.

Examples

  • [kpv] векньыдик “quite thin”

Ko: derivation with suffix -кӧ

Produces indefinite from interrogative.

Examples

  • [kpv] кодкӧ “somebody”
  • [kpv] кыдзкӧ “somehow”
  • [kpv] кутшӧмкӧ “some kind of”

La: derivation with suffix -ла

Produces spatial nouns denoting area/place and is inadvertantly in a spatial case.

Examples

  • [kpv] быдла-: быдлаын “in every place, everywhere”
  • [kpv] кыкла-: кыклаын “in two places”
  • [kpv] важла-: важлаын “in the old place”

LocMod: derivation with suffix -са

Produces meaning in added to adjectival heads adnominal phrases, contrast Inessive case. Added to both singular and plural stems.

Examples

  • [kpv] карса олысьяс “urban dwellers”

NomAg: derivation with suffix -ысь

Produces nomen agensis (non-temporal).

Examples

  • [kpv] сулалысь “one standing”

Oma: derivation with suffix -ӧма

Produces proprietive-like adjectival derivation of deverbal noun used in elliptic constructions.

Examples

  • [kpv] быдмӧма “having grown”

Omon: derivation with suffix -ӧмӧн

Produces converb.

Examples

  • [kpv] кутӧмӧн “holding”

PrivMod: derivation with suffix -тӧм

Produces adnominal modifiers (without an X).

Examples

  • [kpv] кепысьтӧм “without a glove”

ProprietiveMod: derivation with suffix -а/-я

Produces adverbs and adjectives (having an X).

Examples

  • [kpv] кепыся “with a glove”

Sjuro: derivation with suffix -сюрӧ

Produces indefinite from interrogative.

Examples

  • [kpv] кодсюрӧ “somebody”
  • [kpv] кыдзсюрӧ “somehow”
  • [kpv] кутшӧмсюрӧ “some kind of”

TempMod: derivation with suffix -ся

Produces adnominal modifiers (in the time of X).

Examples

  • [kpv] июль тӧлысся “from the month of June”

VAbe: derivation with suffix -тӧг

Produces converbs.

Examples

  • [kpv] повтӧг “without fearing”

Voc: derivation with suffix -ӧ

Produces vocative with nouns.

Examples

  • [kpv] нылӧ “hey girl”

VTer: derivation with suffix -тӧдз

Produces converbs.

Examples

  • [kpv] петтӧдз “until it comes out”
