advcl:pred: optional depictive

Beginning with release 2.17, optional depictives (secondary predication) are annotated with the dedicated relation subtype advcl:pred instead of plain advcl. The current implementation assumes that all instances of the Atv relation (verbal attribute) in PADT will be converted to advcl:pred, although some of them may need the xcomp relation instead.
