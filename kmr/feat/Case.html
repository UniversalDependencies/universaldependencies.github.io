Case: case

Values: Acc Con Nom Voc

Nom: nominative

Examples

  • sal “year, years”

Acc: oblique

Examples

  • salê “year”
  • salan “years”

Con: construct

This case marks possession on the possessed noun, not on the possessor, hence it is not the genitive. The construct suffix is sometimes also called ezafe.

Examples

  • sala 1940’ê “year of 1940”
  • salên “years of”

Voc: vocative

Examples

  • salê “year”
  • saline “years”
