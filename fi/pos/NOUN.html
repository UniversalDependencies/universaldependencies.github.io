NOUN: noun

Nouns inflect for case and number and denote things such as people, places, things, animals and ideas.

Proper nouns are not annotated as NOUN but rather PROPN.

Examples

  • [fi] tyttö “girl”
  • [fi] kissa “cat”
  • [fi] puu “tree”
