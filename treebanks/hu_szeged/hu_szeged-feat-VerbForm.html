Treebank Statistics: UD_Hungarian-Szeged: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv, PartFut, PartPast, PartPres are language-specific. It occurs with 6 different values: Conv, Fin, Inf, PartFut, PartPast, PartPres.

5082 tokens (12%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 2963 types (22%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 1923 lemmas (22%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 4 part-of-speech tags: VERB (3663; 9% instances), ADJ (1149; 3% instances), AUX (189; 0% instances), ADV (81; 0% instances).
