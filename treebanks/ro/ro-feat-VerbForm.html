Treebank Statistics: UD_Romanian: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fin, Ger, Inf, Part.

27225 tokens (12%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 7205 types (23%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 1892 lemmas (11%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (23164; 11% instances), AUX (4061; 2% instances).

23164 VERB tokens (100% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of VerbForm.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and VerbForm co-occurred: Gender=EMPTY (15562; 67%), Number=Sing (11901; 51%).

  • Ger (1005; 4% of non-empty VerbForm): având, începând, ținând, făcând, fiind, lăsând, urmând, înregistrând, putând, reprezentând
  • Inf (2109; 9% of non-empty VerbForm): putea, trebui, avea, face, fi, aduce, lua, asigura, permite, vedea
  • Part (7602; 33% of non-empty VerbForm): avut, prevăzute, fost, făcut, spus, putut, rupt, murit, dat, devenit
