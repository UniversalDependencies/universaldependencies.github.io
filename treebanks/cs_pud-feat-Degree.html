Treebank Statistics: UD_Czech-PUD: Features: Degree

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Cmp, Pos, Sup.

2172 tokens (12%) have a non-empty value of Degree. 1638 types (22%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Degree. 1060 lemmas (20%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Degree. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: ADJ (1883; 10% instances), ADV (289; 2% instances).
