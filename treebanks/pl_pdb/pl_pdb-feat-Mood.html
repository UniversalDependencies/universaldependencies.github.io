Treebank Statistics: UD_Polish-PDB: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Imp, Ind.

38306 tokens (11%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 11602 types (19%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 4335 lemmas (16%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (32717; 9% instances), AUX (5589; 2% instances).

VERB

32717 VERB tokens (83% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Mood.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Mood co-occurred: VerbForm=Fin (32717; 100%), Voice=Act (31910; 98%), Number=Sing (23930; 73%), Animacy=EMPTY (23519; 72%), Aspect=Imp (21255; 65%), Gender=EMPTY (18507; 57%).
