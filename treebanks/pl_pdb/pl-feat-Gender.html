Treebank Statistics: UD_Polish: Features: Gender

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fem, Masc, Neut.

42428 tokens (51%) have a non-empty value of Gender. 21647 types (92%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Gender. 12129 lemmas (90%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Gender. The feature is used with 8 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (20764; 25% instances), ADJ (7896; 9% instances), VERB (4968; 6% instances), PROPN (2959; 4% instances), PRON (2658; 3% instances), DET (1975; 2% instances), NUM (741; 1% instances), AUX (467; 1% instances).
