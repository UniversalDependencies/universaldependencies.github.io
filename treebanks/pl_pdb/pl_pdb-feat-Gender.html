Treebank Statistics: UD_Polish-PDB: Features: Gender

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fem, Masc, Neut.

171723 tokens (49%) have a non-empty value of Gender. 55766 types (92%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Gender. 25364 lemmas (92%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Gender.
