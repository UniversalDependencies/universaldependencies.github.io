Treebank Statistics: UD_Polish-PDB: Features: Polarity

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Neg, Pos.

14720 tokens (4%) have a non-empty value of Polarity. 6649 types (11%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Polarity. 2871 lemmas (10%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Polarity. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: ADJ (6764; 2% instances), PART (4043; 1% instances), NOUN (3913; 1% instances).
