Treebank Statistics: UD_Polish-PDB: Features: Polarity

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Neg, Pos.

14631 tokens (4%) have a non-empty value of Polarity. 6648 types (11%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Polarity. 2872 lemmas (10%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Polarity. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: ADJ (6770; 2% instances), PART (3957; 1% instances), NOUN (3904; 1% instances).
