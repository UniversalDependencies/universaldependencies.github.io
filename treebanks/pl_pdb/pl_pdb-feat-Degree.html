Treebank Statistics: UD_Polish-PDB: Features: Degree

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Cmp, Pos, Sup.

34819 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Degree. 13146 types (22%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Degree. 5650 lemmas (20%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Degree. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: ADJ (28668; 8% instances), ADV (6151; 2% instances).

ADJ

28668 ADJ tokens (80% of all ADJ tokens) have a non-empty value of Degree.
