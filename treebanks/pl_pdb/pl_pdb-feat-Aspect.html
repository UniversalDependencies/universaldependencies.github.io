Treebank Statistics: UD_Polish-PDB: Features: Aspect

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Imp, Perf.

56858 tokens (16%) have a non-empty value of Aspect. 20852 types (35%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. 5834 lemmas (21%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. The feature is used with 4 part-of-speech tags: VERB (38912; 11% instances), AUX (7272; 2% instances), ADJ (6770; 2% instances), NOUN (3904; 1% instances).
