Treebank Statistics: UD_Polish-PDB: Features: Voice

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Act, Pass.

49689 tokens (14%) have a non-empty value of Voice. 18591 types (31%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. 5539 lemmas (20%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (38720; 11% instances), ADJ (6764; 2% instances), AUX (4205; 1% instances).

VERB

38720 VERB tokens (98% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Voice.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Voice co-occurred: VerbForm=Fin (31910; 82%), Mood=Ind (31324; 81%), Animacy=EMPTY (29522; 76%), Aspect=Imp (24723; 64%), Gender=EMPTY (24510; 63%), Number=Sing (23833; 62%), Person=EMPTY (21020; 54%).

VERB tokens may have the following values of Voice:

  • Act (38720; 100% of non-empty Voice): ma, może, stoi, jest, mówi, siedzi, miał, należy, powiedział, mam
  • EMPTY (807): można, trzeba, będzie, wiadomo, widać, warto, oto, wolno, będą, słychać

Voice seems to be lexical feature of VERB. 100% lemmas (4885) occur only with one value of Voice.
