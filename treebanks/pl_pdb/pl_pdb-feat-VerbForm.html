Treebank Statistics: UD_Polish-PDB: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv, Vnoun are language-specific. It occurs with 5 different values: Conv, Fin, Inf, Part, Vnoun.

56390 tokens (16%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 20886 types (35%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 5860 lemmas (21%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 4 part-of-speech tags: VERB (39744; 11% instances), ADJ (6789; 2% instances), AUX (5947; 2% instances), NOUN (3910; 1% instances).
