Treebank Statistics: UD_Czech-PDTC: Features: Gender

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fem, Masc, Neut. Some words have combined values of the feature; 3 combinations have been observed: Fem|Masc, Fem|Neut, Masc|Neut.

This is a layered feature with the following layers: Gender, Gender[psor].

318217 tokens (46%) have a non-empty value of Gender. 68174 types (88%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Gender. 29426 lemmas (78%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Gender. The feature is used with 9 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (153526; 22% instances), ADJ (73814; 11% instances), VERB (27589; 4% instances), DET (25773; 4% instances), PROPN (24377; 4% instances), AUX (6366; 1% instances), NUM (4106; 1% instances), PRON (2655; 0% instances), SYM (11; 0% instances).
