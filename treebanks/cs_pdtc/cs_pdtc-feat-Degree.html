Treebank Statistics: UD_Czech-PDTC: Features: Degree

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Cmp, Pos, Sup.

79310 tokens (11%) have a non-empty value of Degree. 23134 types (30%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Degree. 8537 lemmas (23%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Degree. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: ADJ (69108; 10% instances), ADV (10202; 1% instances).
