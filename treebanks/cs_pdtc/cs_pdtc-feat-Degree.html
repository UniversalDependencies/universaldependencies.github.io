Treebank Statistics: UD_Czech-PDTC: Features: Degree

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Cmp, Pos, Sup.

386601 tokens (11%) have a non-empty value of Degree. 57750 types (31%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Degree. 16841 lemmas (20%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Degree. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: ADJ (336415; 10% instances), ADV (50186; 1% instances).
