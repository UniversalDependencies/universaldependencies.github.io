Treebank Statistics: UD_Czech-PDTC: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

398654 tokens (12%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 34789 types (19%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 8076 lemmas (10%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (268298; 8% instances), AUX (120218; 3% instances), ADJ (10138; 0% instances).
