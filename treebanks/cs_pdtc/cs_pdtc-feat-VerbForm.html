Treebank Statistics: UD_Czech-PDTC: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv, Vnoun are language-specific. It occurs with 5 different values: Conv, Fin, Inf, Part, Vnoun.

113881 tokens (16%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 24477 types (32%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 8297 lemmas (22%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm.
