Treebank Statistics: UD_Kyrgyz-KTMU: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

1423 tokens (19%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 873 types (26%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 485 lemmas (23%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (1407; 19% instances), NOUN (16; 0% instances).
