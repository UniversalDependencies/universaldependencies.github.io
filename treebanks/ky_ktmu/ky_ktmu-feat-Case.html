Treebank Statistics: UD_Kyrgyz-KTMU: Features: Case

This feature is universal but the values Equ are language-specific. It occurs with 8 different values: Abl, Acc, Dat, Equ, Gen, Ins, Loc, Nom. Some words have combined values of the feature; 7 combinations have been observed: Abl|Gen, Acc|Dat, Acc|Gen, Acc|Ins, Acc|Loc, Dat|Gen, Gen|Loc.

3813 tokens (51%) have a non-empty value of Case. 2338 types (70%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 1601 lemmas (75%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. The feature is used with 10 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (2633; 35% instances), PROPN (667; 9% instances), VERB (215; 3% instances), PRON (166; 2% instances), ADJ (82; 1% instances), ADV (22; 0% instances), NUM (11; 0% instances), CCONJ (9; 0% instances), ADP (7; 0% instances), DET (1; 0% instances).
