Treebank Statistics: UD_Kyrgyz-KTMU: Features: Case

This feature is universal but the values Equ are language-specific. It occurs with 8 different values: Abl, Acc, Dat, Equ, Gen, Ins, Loc, Nom. Some words have combined values of the feature; 7 combinations have been observed: Abl|Gen, Acc|Dat, Acc|Gen, Acc|Ins, Acc|Loc, Dat|Gen, Gen|Loc.

12847 tokens (54%) have a non-empty value of Case. 5070 types (77%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 2988 lemmas (81%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. The feature is used with 10 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (8652; 37% instances), PROPN (2820; 12% instances), VERB (847; 4% instances), PRON (220; 1% instances), ADJ (199; 1% instances), NUM (49; 0% instances), ADV (34; 0% instances), ADP (12; 0% instances), CCONJ (12; 0% instances), DET (2; 0% instances).
