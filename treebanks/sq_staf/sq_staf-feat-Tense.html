Treebank Statistics: UD_Albanian-STAF: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Past, Pres.

444 tokens (12%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 288 types (24%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 193 lemmas (20%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (337; 9% instances), AUX (107; 3% instances).
