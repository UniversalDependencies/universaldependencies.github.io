Treebank Statistics: UD_Albanian-STAF: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 5 different values: Abl, Acc, Dat, Gen, Nom.

1285 tokens (36%) have a non-empty value of Case. 705 types (58%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 561 lemmas (57%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case.
