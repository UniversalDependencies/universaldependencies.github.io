Treebank Statistics: UD_Polish-LFG: Features: Degree

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Cmp, Pos, Sup.

10373 tokens (8%) have a non-empty value of Degree. 5405 types (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Degree. 2918 lemmas (19%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Degree. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: ADJ (7332; 6% instances), ADV (3041; 2% instances).
