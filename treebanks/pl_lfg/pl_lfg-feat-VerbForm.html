Treebank Statistics: UD_Polish-LFG: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv, Vnoun are language-specific. It occurs with 5 different values: Conv, Fin, Inf, Part, Vnoun.

25473 tokens (19%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 11624 types (39%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 3875 lemmas (25%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 4 part-of-speech tags: VERB (21592; 16% instances), AUX (2126; 2% instances), ADJ (1193; 1% instances), NOUN (562; 0% instances).
