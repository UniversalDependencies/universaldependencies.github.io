Treebank Statistics: UD_Polish-LFG: Features: Aspect

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Imp, Perf.

26265 tokens (20%) have a non-empty value of Aspect. 11595 types (39%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. 3856 lemmas (25%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. The feature is used with 4 part-of-speech tags: VERB (20761; 16% instances), AUX (3749; 3% instances), ADJ (1193; 1% instances), NOUN (562; 0% instances).
