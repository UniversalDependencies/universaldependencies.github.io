Treebank Statistics: UD_Marathi-UFAL: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 10 different values: Abl, Abs, Acc, Com, Dat, Erg, Ins, Loc, Nom, Voc.

1426 tokens (37%) have a non-empty value of Case. 605 types (55%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 454 lemmas (65%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case.
