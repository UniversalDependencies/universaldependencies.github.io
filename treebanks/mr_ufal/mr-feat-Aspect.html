Treebank Statistics: UD_Marathi: Features: Aspect

This feature is universal but the values Hab, Prosp are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Hab, Imp, Perf, Prosp.

574 tokens (15%) have a non-empty value of Aspect. 252 types (27%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. 117 lemmas (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (541; 14% instances), AUX (33; 1% instances).

541 VERB tokens (77% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Aspect.
