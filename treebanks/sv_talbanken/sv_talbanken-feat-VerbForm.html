Treebank Statistics: UD_Swedish-Talbanken: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Stem are language-specific. It occurs with 5 different values: Fin, Inf, Part, Stem, Sup.

15716 tokens (16%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 3473 types (23%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 1702 lemmas (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (9871; 10% instances), AUX (4281; 4% instances), ADJ (1564; 2% instances).
