Treebank Statistics: UD_English-GENTLE: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Past, Pres.

1504 tokens (8%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 618 types (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 419 lemmas (13%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (1021; 6% instances), AUX (483; 3% instances).
