Treebank Statistics: UD_Croatian: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Imp, Past, Pres.

25746 tokens (13%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 4615 types (13%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 1898 lemmas (10%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (13670; 7% instances), AUX (11921; 6% instances), ADV (155; 0% instances).

13670 VERB tokens (79% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Tense.
