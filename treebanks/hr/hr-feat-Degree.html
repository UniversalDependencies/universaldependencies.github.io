Treebank Statistics: UD_Croatian: Features: Degree

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Cmp, Pos, Sup.

30417 tokens (15%) have a non-empty value of Degree. 11422 types (32%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Degree. 5091 lemmas (27%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Degree. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: ADJ (21922; 11% instances), ADV (8495; 4% instances).
