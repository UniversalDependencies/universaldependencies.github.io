Treebank Statistics: UD_Albanian-TSA: Features: Voice

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Act, Pass.

95 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Voice. 66 types (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. 55 lemmas (13%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (67; 7% instances), AUX (28; 3% instances).

VERB

67 VERB tokens (79% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Voice.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Voice co-occurred: Aspect=Imp (63; 94%), VerbForm=EMPTY (61; 91%), Person=3 (58; 87%), Mood=Ind (56; 84%), Tense=Pres (49; 73%).

VERB tokens may have the following values of Voice:

  • Act (50; 75% of non-empty Voice): dashuruar, ka, kanë, kishte, Dëgjojmë, dëmtojnë, ekziston, fillojnë, formon, identifikojnë
  • Pass (17; 25% of non-empty Voice): mendohet, formuan, intensifikuar, karakterizohet, kthehet, kuptohet, mirren, njihet, nënshtruan, përcaktohet
  • EMPTY (18): mund, Kam, arritur, bartur, duhet, krijuar, mbijetuar, mundësuar, ndryshuar, pastruar
