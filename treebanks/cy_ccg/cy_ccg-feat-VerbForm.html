Treebank Statistics: UD_Welsh-CCG: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Vnoun are language-specific. It occurs with 2 different values: Fin, Vnoun.

9193 tokens (17%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 1877 types (25%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 818 lemmas (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm.
