Treebank Statistics: UD_Welsh-CCG: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values FinRel, Vnoun are language-specific. It occurs with 3 different values: Fin, FinRel, Vnoun.

8840 tokens (17%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 1833 types (25%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 800 lemmas (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (4768; 9% instances), VERB (2985; 6% instances), AUX (1087; 2% instances).

NOUN

4768 NOUN tokens (30% of all NOUN tokens) have a non-empty value of VerbForm.

The most frequent other feature values with which NOUN and VerbForm co-occurred: Number=Sing (4768; 100%), Gender=EMPTY (4760; 100%), Mutation=EMPTY (3298; 69%).

NOUN tokens may have the following values of VerbForm:

  • Vnoun (4768; 100% of non-empty VerbForm): bod, cael, fod, gael, mynd, dod, wneud, ddod, fynd, gwneud
  • EMPTY (11226): ôl, iaith, Gymraeg, ysgol, nifer, gwaith, gyfer, cyngor, mwyn, eisteddfod

VerbForm seems to be lexical feature of NOUN. 100% lemmas (744) occur only with one value of VerbForm.
