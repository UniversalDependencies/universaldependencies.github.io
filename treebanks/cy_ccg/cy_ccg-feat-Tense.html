Treebank Statistics: UD_Welsh-CCG: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 5 different values: Fut, Imp, Past, Pqp, Pres.

2699 tokens (7%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 538 types (9%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 189 lemmas (5%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (2037; 6% instances), AUX (662; 2% instances).
