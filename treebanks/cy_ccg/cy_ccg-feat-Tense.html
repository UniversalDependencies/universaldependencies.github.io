Treebank Statistics: UD_Welsh-CCG: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 5 different values: Fut, Imp, Past, Pqp, Pres.

3084 tokens (7%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 610 types (10%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 209 lemmas (5%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (2326; 6% instances), AUX (758; 2% instances).
