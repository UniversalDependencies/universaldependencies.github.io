Treebank Statistics: UD_Spanish-PUD: Features: Voice

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Act, Pass.

1911 tokens (8%) have a non-empty value of Voice. 925 types (16%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. 54 lemmas (2%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (1263; 5% instances), AUX (648; 3% instances).

VERB

1263 VERB tokens (58% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Voice.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Voice co-occurred: Gender=EMPTY (1263; 100%), VerbForm=Fin (1263; 100%), Person=3 (1200; 95%), Mood=Ind (1164; 92%), Number=Sing (921; 73%), Aspect=Imp (753; 60%), Tense=Past (702; 56%).

VERB tokens may have the following values of Voice:

  • Act (1177; 93% of non-empty Voice): dijo, es, tiene, hay, está, hace, tienen, afirmó, convirtió, dice
  • Pass (86; 7% of non-empty Voice): conoce, aplicaría, conocen, construyó, cree, dijo, espera, incluyen, lanzó, llevó
  • EMPTY (926): hacer, tener, debido, ver, establecer, hecho, ayudar, dado, dejado, dejar

Voice seems to be lexical feature of VERB. 92% lemmas (49) occur only with one value of Voice.

AUX

648 AUX tokens (91% of all AUX tokens) have a non-empty value of Voice.

The most frequent other feature values with which AUX and Voice co-occurred: VerbForm=Fin (648; 100%), Person=3 (616; 95%), Mood=Ind (578; 89%), Number=Sing (471; 73%), Aspect=Imp (385; 59%), Tense=Past (346; 53%).

AUX tokens may have the following values of Voice:

  • Act (523; 81% of non-empty Voice): es, ha, había, está, era, fue, son, han, puede, estaba
  • Pass (125; 19% of non-empty Voice): fue, fueron, ha, había, puede, son, habían, pueden, era, eran
  • EMPTY (62): ser, sido, haber, siendo, estar, estado, hecho, poder, Habiendo, escrito
