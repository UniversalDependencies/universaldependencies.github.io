Treebank Statistics: UD_Finnish: Features: PartForm

This feature is language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Agt, Neg, Past, Pres.

7831 tokens (4%) have a non-empty value of PartForm. 3862 types (7%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PartForm. 1258 lemmas (5%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PartForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (7309; 4% instances), AUX (522; 0% instances).
