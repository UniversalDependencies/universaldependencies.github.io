Treebank Statistics: UD_Komi_Zyrian-Lattice: Features: Case

This feature is universal but the values Apr, Car, Egr, Prl are language-specific. It occurs with 16 different values: Abl, Acc, Apr, Car, Com, Dat, Egr, Ela, Gen, Ill, Ine, Ins, Nom, Prl, Ter, Tra.

1124 tokens (46%) have a non-empty value of Case. 788 types (65%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 569 lemmas (63%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. The feature is used with 9 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (545; 22% instances), ADJ (183; 7% instances), PRON (161; 7% instances), ADP (77; 3% instances), VERB (49; 2% instances), ADV (41; 2% instances), PROPN (37; 2% instances), DET (18; 1% instances), NUM (13; 1% instances).
