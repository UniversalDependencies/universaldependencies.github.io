Treebank Statistics: UD_Komi_Zyrian-Lattice: Features: Case

This feature is universal but the values Apr, Car, Egr, Prl are language-specific. It occurs with 16 different values: Abl, Acc, Apr, Car, Com, Dat, Egr, Ela, Gen, Ill, Ine, Ins, Nom, Prl, Ter, Tra.

1007 tokens (46%) have a non-empty value of Case. 716 types (66%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 515 lemmas (63%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. The feature is used with 9 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (483; 22% instances), ADJ (162; 7% instances), PRON (142; 7% instances), ADP (68; 3% instances), VERB (57; 3% instances), ADV (35; 2% instances), PROPN (32; 1% instances), DET (17; 1% instances), NUM (11; 1% instances).
