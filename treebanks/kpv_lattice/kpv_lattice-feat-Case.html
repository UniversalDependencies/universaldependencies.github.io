Treebank Statistics: UD_Komi_Zyrian-Lattice: Features: Case

This feature is universal but the values Apr, Car, Cns, Comp, Egr, Prl are language-specific. It occurs with 18 different values: Abl, Acc, Apr, Car, Cns, Com, Comp, Dat, Egr, Ela, Gen, Ill, Ine, Ins, Nom, Prl, Ter, Tra.

2282 tokens (46%) have a non-empty value of Case. 1559 types (66%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 1084 lemmas (64%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. The feature is used with 11 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (1278; 26% instances), ADJ (310; 6% instances), PRON (277; 6% instances), ADP (145; 3% instances), VERB (89; 2% instances), ADV (82; 2% instances), PROPN (39; 1% instances), NUM (36; 1% instances), DET (23; 0% instances), SCONJ (2; 0% instances), INTJ (1; 0% instances).
