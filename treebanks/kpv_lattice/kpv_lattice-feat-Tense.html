Treebank Statistics: UD_Komi_Zyrian-Lattice: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

1080 tokens (13%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 731 types (21%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 507 lemmas (22%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (842; 11% instances), AUX (238; 3% instances).
