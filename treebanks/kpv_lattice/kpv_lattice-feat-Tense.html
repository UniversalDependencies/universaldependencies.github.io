Treebank Statistics: UD_Komi_Zyrian-Lattice: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

1088 tokens (13%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 735 types (21%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 509 lemmas (22%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (848; 10% instances), AUX (240; 3% instances).
