Description

UD Komi-Zyrian Lattice is a treebank of written standard Komi-Zyrian. The treebank contains continuous texts which are identifiable through the id’s.

The written language data is all from openly available sources. One of the larger segments come from Lev Uspenskiy’s book Нёль боевӧй случай (URN http://urn.fi/URN:NBN:fi-fe2014102045428), which is released in Public Domain, and Ivan Belyx’s short story which was added into Komi Nebögain online library by the author in 2013.
