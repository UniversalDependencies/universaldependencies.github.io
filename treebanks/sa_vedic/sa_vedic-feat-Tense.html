Treebank Statistics: UD_Sanskrit-Vedic: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Past, Pqp, Pres.

37688 tokens (18%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 11840 types (33%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 2737 lemmas (21%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (35863; 17% instances), AUX (1825; 1% instances).
