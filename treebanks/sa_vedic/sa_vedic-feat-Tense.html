Treebank Statistics: UD_Sanskrit-Vedic: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Past, Pqp, Pres.

5319 tokens (20%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 2418 types (32%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 839 lemmas (25%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (5018; 19% instances), AUX (301; 1% instances).
