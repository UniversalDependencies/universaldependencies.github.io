Treebank Statistics: UD_Sanskrit-Vedic: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv, Gdv are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Conv, Gdv, Inf, Part.

12492 tokens (6%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 5005 types (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 1854 lemmas (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (12399; 6% instances), AUX (93; 0% instances).
