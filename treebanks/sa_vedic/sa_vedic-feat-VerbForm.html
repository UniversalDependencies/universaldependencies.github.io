Treebank Statistics: UD_Sanskrit-Vedic: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv, Gdv are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Conv, Gdv, Inf, Part.

1312 tokens (5%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 754 types (10%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 410 lemmas (12%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (1289; 5% instances), AUX (23; 0% instances).
