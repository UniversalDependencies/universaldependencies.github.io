Treebank Statistics: UD_Tamil-MWTT: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

408 tokens (16%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 176 types (21%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 81 lemmas (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (342; 13% instances), AUX (66; 3% instances).
