Treebank Statistics: UD_Romanian-RRT: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 5 different values: Fut, Imp, Past, Pqp, Pres.

17723 tokens (8%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 4361 types (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 1539 lemmas (9%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (14308; 7% instances), AUX (3405; 2% instances), PART (10; 0% instances).
