Treebank Statistics: UD_Romanian-RRT: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Imp, Ind, Sub.

17532 tokens (8%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 3985 types (13%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 1428 lemmas (8%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (12448; 6% instances), AUX (2674; 1% instances), PART (2410; 1% instances).

12448 VERB tokens (54% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Mood.
