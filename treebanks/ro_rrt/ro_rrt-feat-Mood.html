Treebank Statistics: UD_Romanian-RRT: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Imp, Ind, Sub.

17473 tokens (8%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 3939 types (12%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 1411 lemmas (8%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (12280; 6% instances), AUX (2784; 1% instances), PART (2409; 1% instances).

VERB

12280 VERB tokens (53% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Mood.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Mood co-occurred: Gender=EMPTY (12280; 100%), VerbForm=Fin (12280; 100%), Person=3 (10779; 88%), Tense=Pres (8960; 73%).

VERB tokens may have the following values of Mood:

  • Sub (1714; 14% of non-empty Mood): facă, aibă, ia, dea, poată, aplice, vadă, spună, stabilească, prevadă
  • EMPTY (10719): avut, prevăzute, putea, făcut, trebui, având, avea, face, spus, putut
