Treebank Statistics: UD_Russian-Poetry: Features: PronType

This feature is universal but the values Emp, Exc are language-specific. It occurs with 10 different values: Dem, Emp, Exc, Ind, Int, Neg, Prs, Rcp, Rel, Tot.

6389 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of PronType. 573 types (3%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. 137 lemmas (1%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: PRON (3531; 6% instances), DET (1808; 3% instances), ADV (1050; 2% instances).
