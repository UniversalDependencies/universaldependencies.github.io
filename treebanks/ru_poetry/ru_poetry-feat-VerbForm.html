Treebank Statistics: UD_Russian-Poetry: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Conv, Fin, Inf, Part.

8287 tokens (13%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 5889 types (33%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 2787 lemmas (28%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (8051; 13% instances), AUX (236; 0% instances).

VERB

8051 VERB tokens (98% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of VerbForm.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and VerbForm co-occurred: Voice=Act (6267; 78%), Gender=EMPTY (5788; 72%), Mood=Ind (5273; 65%), Number=Sing (5018; 62%), Aspect=Imp (4442; 55%), Person=EMPTY (4369; 54%).

  • Fin (5758; 72% of non-empty VerbForm): знаю, может, стоит, есть, люблю, вижу, могу, поет, хочу, прости
  • Inf (800; 10% of non-empty VerbForm): быть, жить, любить, идти, найти, сказать, слушать, знать, петь, умереть
  • Part (962; 12% of non-empty VerbForm): дано, сказано, звенящий, приказано, рожденный, сверкающих, дремлющим, занят, знавшие, лишен
  • EMPTY (183): нет, надо, можно, пора, нельзя, нету, жаль, некуда, лень, незачем
