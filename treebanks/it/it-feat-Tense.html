Treebank Statistics: UD_Italian: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Imp, Past, Pres.

28634 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 6341 types (24%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 1891 lemmas (10%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (18227; 6% instances), AUX (10406; 4% instances), NOUN (1; 0% instances).
