Treebank Statistics: UD_Upper_Sorbian: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Cnd, Imp, Ind.

893 tokens (8%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 407 types (10%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 263 lemmas (9%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (612; 6% instances), AUX (281; 3% instances).
