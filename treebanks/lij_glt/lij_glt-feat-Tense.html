Treebank Statistics: UD_Ligurian-GLT: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Imp, Past, Pres.

1080 tokens (16%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 509 types (33%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 250 lemmas (21%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (762; 11% instances), AUX (318; 5% instances).
