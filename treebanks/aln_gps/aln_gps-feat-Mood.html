Treebank Statistics: UD_Gheg-GPS: Features: Mood

This feature is universal but the values Adm are language-specific. It occurs with 3 different values: Adm, Ind, Sub.

2457 tokens (15%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 739 types (29%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 190 lemmas (19%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (1708; 11% instances), AUX (749; 5% instances).
