Treebank Statistics: UD_Gheg-GPS: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fin, Inf, Part.

3430 tokens (21%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 966 types (38%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 225 lemmas (23%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm.
