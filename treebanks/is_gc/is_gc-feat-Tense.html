Treebank Statistics: UD_Icelandic-GC: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Past, Pres.

11008 tokens (11%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 1692 types (8%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 748 lemmas (5%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (7345; 7% instances), AUX (3662; 4% instances), ADV (1; 0% instances).
