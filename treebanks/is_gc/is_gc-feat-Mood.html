Treebank Statistics: UD_Icelandic-GC: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Imp, Ind, Sub.

11067 tokens (11%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 1735 types (9%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 760 lemmas (6%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (7402; 7% instances), AUX (3664; 4% instances), ADV (1; 0% instances).
