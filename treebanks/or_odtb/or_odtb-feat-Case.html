Treebank Statistics: UD_Odia-ODTB: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 5 different values: Acc, Gen, Ins, Loc, Nom.

2064 tokens (35%) have a non-empty value of Case. 1250 types (49%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 366 lemmas (45%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case.
