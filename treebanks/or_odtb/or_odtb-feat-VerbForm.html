Treebank Statistics: UD_Odia-ODTB: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv, Vnoun are language-specific. It occurs with 6 different values: Conv, Fin, Ger, Inf, Part, Vnoun.

610 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 333 types (13%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 112 lemmas (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm.
