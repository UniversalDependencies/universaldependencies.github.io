Treebank Statistics: UD_Persian-PerDT: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

47499 tokens (9%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 2726 types (8%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 461 lemmas (2%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (35801; 7% instances), AUX (11698; 2% instances).

35801 VERB tokens (69% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Tense.
