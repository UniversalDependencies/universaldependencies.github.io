Treebank Statistics: UD_Galician-TreeGal: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fin, Ger, Inf, Part.

2983 tokens (12%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 1652 types (31%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 692 lemmas (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (2341; 9% instances), AUX (642; 3% instances).

2341 VERB tokens (100% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of VerbForm.
