Treebank Statistics: UD_Portuguese-Porttinari: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fin, Ger, Inf, Part.

22959 tokens (14%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 6484 types (34%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 2039 lemmas (16%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (17104; 10% instances), AUX (4806; 3% instances), ADJ (1049; 1% instances).
