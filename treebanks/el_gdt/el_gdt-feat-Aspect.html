Treebank Statistics: UD_Greek-GDT: Features: Aspect

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Imp, Perf.

6932 tokens (11%) have a non-empty value of Aspect. 2995 types (26%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. 954 lemmas (15%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (5867; 9% instances), AUX (1065; 2% instances).

VERB

5867 VERB tokens (100% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Aspect.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Aspect co-occurred: VerbForm=Fin (5400; 92%), Mood=Ind (5380; 92%), Person=3 (4277; 73%), Voice=Act (4073; 69%), Number=Sing (3316; 57%).

VERB tokens may have the following values of Aspect:

  • Imp (2897; 49% of non-empty Aspect): μπορεί, αφορά, έχει, αποτελεί, έχουν, υπάρχει, ήθελα, βρίσκεται, πρόκειται, πιστεύω
  • Perf (2970; 51% of non-empty Aspect): δήλωσε, γίνει, ανέφερε, έγινε, πω, ανακοίνωσε, τεθεί, αποτελέσει, γίνουν, επεσήμανε
