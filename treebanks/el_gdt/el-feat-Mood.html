Treebank Statistics: UD_Greek: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Imp, Ind.

6465 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 2707 types (24%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 881 lemmas (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (5915; 9% instances), AUX (550; 1% instances).

VERB

5915 VERB tokens (93% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Mood.
