Treebank Statistics: UD_Spanish-AnCora: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Cnd, Imp, Ind, Sub.

43152 tokens (8%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 7600 types (20%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 1917 lemmas (7%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (30197; 5% instances), AUX (12955; 2% instances).
