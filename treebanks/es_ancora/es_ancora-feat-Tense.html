Treebank Statistics: UD_Spanish-AnCora: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Imp, Past, Pres.

47239 tokens (8%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 8550 types (22%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 2044 lemmas (8%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (34216; 6% instances), AUX (13023; 2% instances).
