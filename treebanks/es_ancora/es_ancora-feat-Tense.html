Treebank Statistics: UD_Spanish-AnCora: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Imp, Past, Pres.

47203 tokens (8%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 8551 types (22%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 2040 lemmas (8%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (35144; 6% instances), AUX (12059; 2% instances).
