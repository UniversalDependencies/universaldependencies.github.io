Treebank Statistics: UD_Spanish-AnCora: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fin, Ger, Inf, Part.

68312 tokens (12%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 13004 types (33%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 3749 lemmas (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm.
