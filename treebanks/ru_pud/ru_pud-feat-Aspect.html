Treebank Statistics: UD_Russian-PUD: Features: Aspect

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Imp, Perf.

2336 tokens (12%) have a non-empty value of Aspect. 1525 types (20%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. 875 lemmas (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (2099; 11% instances), AUX (237; 1% instances).

VERB

2099 VERB tokens (99% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Aspect.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Aspect co-occurred: Person=EMPTY (1536; 73%), Voice=Act (1413; 67%), VerbForm=Fin (1352; 64%), Mood=Ind (1349; 64%), Gender=EMPTY (1288; 61%), Number=Sing (1174; 56%), Tense=Past (1080; 51%).

VERB tokens may have the following values of Aspect:

  • Perf (1064; 51% of non-empty Aspect): сказал, стало, заявил, стал, сделать, сказала, получили, получить, привести, принял
