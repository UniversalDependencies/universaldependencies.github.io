Treebank Statistics: UD_Russian-PUD: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 6 different values: Acc, Dat, Gen, Ins, Loc, Nom.

9077 tokens (47%) have a non-empty value of Case. 5737 types (75%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 3921 lemmas (76%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case.
