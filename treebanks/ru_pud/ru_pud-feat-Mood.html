Treebank Statistics: UD_Russian-PUD: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Cnd, Imp, Ind.

1627 tokens (8%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 901 types (12%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 609 lemmas (12%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (1352; 7% instances), AUX (275; 1% instances).
