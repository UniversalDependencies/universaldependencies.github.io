Treebank Statistics: UD_Ottoman_Turkish-BOUN: Features: Case

This feature is universal but the values Equ are language-specific. It occurs with 8 different values: Abl, Acc, Dat, Equ, Gen, Ins, Loc, Nom.

4108 tokens (46%) have a non-empty value of Case. 3093 types (71%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 1963 lemmas (75%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. The feature is used with 7 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (3144; 36% instances), VERB (293; 3% instances), PROPN (266; 3% instances), PRON (205; 2% instances), ADJ (181; 2% instances), AUX (11; 0% instances), NUM (8; 0% instances).
