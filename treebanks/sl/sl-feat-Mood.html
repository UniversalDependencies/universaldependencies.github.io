Treebank Statistics: UD_Slovenian: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Cnd, Imp, Ind.

15403 tokens (11%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 2704 types (9%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 1393 lemmas (8%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: AUX (9190; 7% instances), VERB (6213; 4% instances).
