Treebank Statistics: UD_Slovenian: Features: Degree

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Cmp, Pos, Sup.

20992 tokens (15%) have a non-empty value of Degree. 9576 types (30%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Degree. 4694 lemmas (28%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Degree. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: ADJ (14379; 10% instances), ADV (6613; 5% instances).
