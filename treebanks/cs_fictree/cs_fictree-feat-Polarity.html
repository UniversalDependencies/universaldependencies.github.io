Treebank Statistics: UD_Czech-FicTree: Features: Polarity

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Neg, Pos.

43681 tokens (26%) have a non-empty value of Polarity. 17042 types (63%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Polarity. 7160 lemmas (52%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Polarity.
