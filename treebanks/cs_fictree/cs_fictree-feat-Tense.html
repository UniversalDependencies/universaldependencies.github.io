Treebank Statistics: UD_Czech-FicTree: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

26787 tokens (16%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 9131 types (34%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 3482 lemmas (25%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (21337; 13% instances), AUX (5187; 3% instances), ADJ (263; 0% instances).
