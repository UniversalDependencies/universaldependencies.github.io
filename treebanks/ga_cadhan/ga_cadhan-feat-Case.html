This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Dat, Gen, Nom, Voc.

1037 tokens (27%) have a non-empty value of Case. 797 types (55%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 608 lemmas (67%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case.
