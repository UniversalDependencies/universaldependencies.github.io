Treebank Statistics: UD_Irish-Cadhan: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Dat, Gen, Nom, Voc.

1386 tokens (29%) have a non-empty value of Case. 1053 types (57%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 755 lemmas (68%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. The feature is used with 4 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (1023; 21% instances), PROPN (190; 4% instances), ADJ (103; 2% instances), DET (70; 1% instances).
