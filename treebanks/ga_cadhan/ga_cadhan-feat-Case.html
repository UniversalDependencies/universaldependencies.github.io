Treebank Statistics: UD_Irish-Cadhan: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Dat, Gen, Nom, Voc.

1379 tokens (29%) have a non-empty value of Case. 1053 types (57%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 757 lemmas (68%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. The feature is used with 4 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (1016; 21% instances), PROPN (186; 4% instances), ADJ (110; 2% instances), DET (67; 1% instances).
