Treebank Statistics: UD_Irish-Cadhan: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

482 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 280 types (15%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 102 lemmas (9%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (369; 8% instances), AUX (82; 2% instances), PART (31; 1% instances).
