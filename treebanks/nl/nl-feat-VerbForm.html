Treebank Statistics: UD_Dutch: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fin, Inf, Part.

31471 tokens (15%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 5068 types (19%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 2428 lemmas (11%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (20567; 10% instances), AUX (10904; 5% instances).
