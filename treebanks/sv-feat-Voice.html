Treebank Statistics: UD_Swedish: Features: Voice

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Act, Pass.

13792 tokens (14%) have a non-empty value of Voice. 2601 types (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. 1189 lemmas (12%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (9606; 10% instances), AUX (4186; 4% instances).
