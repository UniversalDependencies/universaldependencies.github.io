Treebank Statistics: UD_Spanish: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Imp, Past, Pres.

32627 tokens (8%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 6474 types (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 2592 lemmas (7%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (27390; 6% instances), AUX (5237; 1% instances).
