Treebank Statistics: UD_Estonian-EWT: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv are language-specific. It occurs with 5 different values: Conv, Fin, Inf, Part, Sup.

12493 tokens (18%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 3849 types (24%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 1379 lemmas (15%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm.
