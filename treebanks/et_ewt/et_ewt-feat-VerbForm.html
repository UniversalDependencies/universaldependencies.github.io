Treebank Statistics: UD_Estonian-EWT: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv are language-specific. It occurs with 5 different values: Conv, Fin, Inf, Part, Sup.

7873 tokens (18%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 2749 types (24%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 1082 lemmas (16%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 4 part-of-speech tags: VERB (5283; 12% instances), AUX (2175; 5% instances), ADJ (404; 1% instances), NOUN (11; 0% instances).
