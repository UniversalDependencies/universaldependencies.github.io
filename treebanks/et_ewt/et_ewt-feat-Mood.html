Treebank Statistics: UD_Estonian-EWT: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Cnd, Imp, Ind, Qot.

8777 tokens (13%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 2322 types (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 740 lemmas (8%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (5428; 8% instances), AUX (3349; 5% instances).
