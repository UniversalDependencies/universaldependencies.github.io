Treebank Statistics: UD_Estonian-EWT: Features: Voice

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Act, Pass.

6807 tokens (16%) have a non-empty value of Voice. 2358 types (21%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. 982 lemmas (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice.
