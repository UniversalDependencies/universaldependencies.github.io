Treebank Statistics: UD_Estonian-EWT: Features: Voice

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Act, Pass.

14186 tokens (16%) have a non-empty value of Voice. 4041 types (20%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. 1525 lemmas (13%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. The feature is used with 4 part-of-speech tags: VERB (8700; 10% instances), AUX (4596; 5% instances), ADJ (836; 1% instances), NOUN (54; 0% instances).
