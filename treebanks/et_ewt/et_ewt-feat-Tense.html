Treebank Statistics: UD_Estonian-EWT: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Past, Pres.

6396 tokens (15%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 2143 types (19%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 916 lemmas (13%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense.
