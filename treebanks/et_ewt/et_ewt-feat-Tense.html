Treebank Statistics: UD_Estonian-EWT: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Past, Pres.

4144 tokens (15%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 1663 types (20%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 755 lemmas (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 4 part-of-speech tags: VERB (2485; 9% instances), AUX (1340; 5% instances), ADJ (309; 1% instances), NOUN (10; 0% instances).
