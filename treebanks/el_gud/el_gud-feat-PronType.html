Treebank Statistics: UD_Greek-GUD: Features: PronType

This feature is universal but the values Emp are language-specific. It occurs with 9 different values: Art, Dem, Emp, Ind, Int, Neg, Prs, Rel, Tot.

6007 tokens (24%) have a non-empty value of PronType. 243 types (5%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. 64 lemmas (2%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. The feature is used with 4 part-of-speech tags: DET (3790; 15% instances), PRON (2016; 8% instances), ADV (161; 1% instances), SCONJ (40; 0% instances).
