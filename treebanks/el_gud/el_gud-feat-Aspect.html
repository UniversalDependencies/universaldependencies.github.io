Treebank Statistics: UD_Greek-GUD: Features: Aspect

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Imp, Perf.

3994 tokens (16%) have a non-empty value of Aspect. 1892 types (42%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. 611 lemmas (23%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (3770; 15% instances), AUX (224; 1% instances).

3770 VERB tokens (98% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Aspect.
