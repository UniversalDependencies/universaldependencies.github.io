Treebank Statistics: UD_Greek-GUD: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Imp, Ind.

4181 tokens (16%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 1860 types (41%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 598 lemmas (22%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (3674; 14% instances), AUX (507; 2% instances).

VERB

3674 VERB tokens (95% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Mood.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Mood co-occurred: VerbForm=Fin (3664; 100%), Voice=Act (3220; 88%), Number=Sing (2736; 74%), Aspect=Imp (2336; 64%), Person=3 (2084; 57%), Tense=Pres (1997; 54%).

  • EMPTY (175): γελώντας, περάσει, φύγει, έρθει, ακούσει, ευχαριστημένος, κάνει, φτάσει, αρχίσει, αφήσει
