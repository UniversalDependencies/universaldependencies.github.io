Treebank Statistics: UD_Greek-GUD: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Imp, Ind.

4235 tokens (17%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 1879 types (41%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 603 lemmas (22%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (3910; 15% instances), AUX (325; 1% instances).
