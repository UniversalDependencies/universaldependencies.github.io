Treebank Statistics: UD_Persian-Seraji: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

14699 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 1339 types (9%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 159 lemmas (2%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (10320; 7% instances), AUX (4379; 3% instances).

10320 VERB tokens (82% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Tense.
