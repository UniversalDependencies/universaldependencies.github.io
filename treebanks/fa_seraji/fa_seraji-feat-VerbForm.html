Treebank Statistics: UD_Persian-Seraji: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fin, Inf, Part.

5813 tokens (4%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 844 types (6%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 303 lemmas (3%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (4250; 3% instances), AUX (1324; 1% instances), ADJ (239; 0% instances).
