Treebank Statistics: UD_Finnish-TDT: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fin, Inf, Part.

39458 tokens (20%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 11422 types (21%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 2496 lemmas (10%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (27794; 14% instances), AUX (11664; 6% instances).
