Treebank Statistics: UD_Irish: Features: PronType

This feature is universal but the values Emp are language-specific. It occurs with 7 different values: Art, Dem, Emp, Ind, Int, Prs, Rel.

3302 tokens (14%) have a non-empty value of PronType. 118 types (2%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. 54 lemmas (1%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. The feature is used with 8 part-of-speech tags: DET (1836; 8% instances), PART (611; 3% instances), ADP (453; 2% instances), PRON (215; 1% instances), VERB (95; 0% instances), AUX (55; 0% instances), X (27; 0% instances), ADV (10; 0% instances).
