Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish-Kenet: Features: Voice

This feature is universal but the values CauPass, Rfl are language-specific. It occurs with 5 different values: Cau, CauPass, Pass, Rcp, Rfl.

1964 tokens (1%) have a non-empty value of Voice. 1340 types (3%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. 433 lemmas (3%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. The feature is used with 1 part-of-speech tags: VERB (1964; 1% instances).
