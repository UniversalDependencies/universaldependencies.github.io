Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish-Kenet: Features: Polarity

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Neg, Pos.

18715 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Polarity. 8578 types (19%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Polarity. 1730 lemmas (11%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Polarity. The feature is used with 1 part-of-speech tags: VERB (18715; 10% instances).
