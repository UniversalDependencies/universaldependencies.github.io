Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish-Kenet: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv, Vnoun are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Conv, Fin, Part, Vnoun.

16251 tokens (9%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 7189 types (16%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 1937 lemmas (13%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (16194; 9% instances), AUX (57; 0% instances).
