Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish-Kenet: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv, Vnoun are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Conv, Fin, Part, Vnoun.

21650 tokens (12%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 10105 types (22%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 2739 lemmas (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (21495; 12% instances), AUX (155; 0% instances).
