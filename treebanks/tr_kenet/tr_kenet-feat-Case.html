Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish-Kenet: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 7 different values: Abl, Acc, Dat, Gen, Ins, Loc, Nom.

74080 tokens (41%) have a non-empty value of Case. 30753 types (68%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 10960 lemmas (70%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. The feature is used with 7 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (58540; 33% instances), VERB (5884; 3% instances), PRON (5144; 3% instances), PROPN (2524; 1% instances), ADJ (1613; 1% instances), NUM (257; 0% instances), AUX (118; 0% instances).
