Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish-Kenet: Features: Aspect

This feature is universal but the values Hab, Rapid are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Hab, Perf, Prog, Rapid.

18029 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Aspect. 8094 types (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. 2068 lemmas (13%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (17968; 10% instances), AUX (61; 0% instances).
