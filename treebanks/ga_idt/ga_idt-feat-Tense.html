Treebank Statistics: UD_Irish-IDT: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

9960 tokens (9%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 1800 types (12%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 451 lemmas (5%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 5 part-of-speech tags: VERB (8114; 7% instances), AUX (1530; 1% instances), PART (298; 0% instances), SCONJ (17; 0% instances), ADV (1; 0% instances).
