Treebank Statistics: UD_Irish-IDT: Features: PronType

This feature is universal but the values Emp are language-specific. It occurs with 7 different values: Art, Dem, Emp, Ind, Int, Prs, Rel.

3306 tokens (14%) have a non-empty value of PronType. 120 types (2%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. 57 lemmas (1%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. The feature is used with 8 part-of-speech tags: DET (1862; 8% instances), PART (612; 3% instances), ADP (454; 2% instances), PRON (215; 1% instances), VERB (95; 0% instances), AUX (54; 0% instances), ADV (11; 0% instances), X (3; 0% instances).
