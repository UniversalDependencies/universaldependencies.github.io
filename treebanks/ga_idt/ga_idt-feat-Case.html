Treebank Statistics: UD_Irish-IDT: Features: Case

This feature is universal but the values NomAcc are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Dat, Gen, NomAcc, Voc.

38191 tokens (33%) have a non-empty value of Case. 10894 types (73%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 5955 lemmas (67%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. The feature is used with 4 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (28668; 25% instances), PROPN (3759; 3% instances), ADJ (3432; 3% instances), DET (2332; 2% instances).
