Treebank Statistics: UD_Punjabi-Rang: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fin, Inf, Part.

259 tokens (14%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 164 types (23%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 137 lemmas (21%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (196; 10% instances), AUX (63; 3% instances).
