Treebank Statistics: UD_Estonian-EDT: Features: Voice

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Act, Pass.

65605 tokens (15%) have a non-empty value of Voice. 12828 types (16%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. 4614 lemmas (11%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice.
