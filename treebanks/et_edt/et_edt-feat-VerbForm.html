Treebank Statistics: UD_Estonian-EDT: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv are language-specific. It occurs with 6 different values: Conv, Fin, Ger, Inf, Part, Sup.

74792 tokens (17%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 14438 types (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 4855 lemmas (12%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm.
