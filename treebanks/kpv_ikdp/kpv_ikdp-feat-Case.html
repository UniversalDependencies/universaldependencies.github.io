Treebank Statistics: UD_Komi_Zyrian-IKDP: Features: Case

This feature is universal but the values Egr, Prl are language-specific. It occurs with 13 different values: Acc, Com, Dat, Egr, Ela, Gen, Ill, Ine, Ins, Loc, Nom, Prl, Ter.

406 tokens (38%) have a non-empty value of Case. 293 types (59%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 241 lemmas (56%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. The feature is used with 8 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (210; 20% instances), PRON (69; 7% instances), ADJ (43; 4% instances), NUM (30; 3% instances), ADP (21; 2% instances), PROPN (19; 2% instances), ADV (8; 1% instances), DET (6; 1% instances).
