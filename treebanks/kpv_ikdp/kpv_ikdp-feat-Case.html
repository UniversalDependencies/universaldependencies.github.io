Treebank Statistics: UD_Komi_Zyrian-IKDP: Features: Case

This feature is universal but the values Egr, Prl are language-specific. It occurs with 11 different values: Acc, Com, Dat, Egr, Gen, Ill, Ine, Ins, Nom, Prl, Ter.

326 tokens (33%) have a non-empty value of Case. 242 types (51%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 207 lemmas (49%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. The feature is used with 8 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (169; 17% instances), ADJ (41; 4% instances), PRON (39; 4% instances), NUM (24; 2% instances), ADP (21; 2% instances), PROPN (17; 2% instances), DET (8; 1% instances), ADV (7; 1% instances).
