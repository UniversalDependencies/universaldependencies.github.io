Treebank Statistics: UD_Komi_Zyrian-IKDP: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Conv, Fin, Inf, Part.

194 tokens (15%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 148 types (24%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 98 lemmas (20%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (158; 12% instances), AUX (36; 3% instances).
