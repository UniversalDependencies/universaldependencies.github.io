Treebank Statistics: UD_Czech-PUD: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

2052 tokens (11%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 1110 types (15%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 675 lemmas (13%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (1460; 8% instances), AUX (529; 3% instances), ADJ (63; 0% instances).
