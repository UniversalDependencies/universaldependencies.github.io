Treebank Statistics: UD_Komi_Permyak-UH: Features: Case

This feature is universal but the values Apr, Car, Cmp, Egr, Prl are language-specific. It occurs with 14 different values: Acc, Apr, Car, Cmp, Com, Dat, Egr, Ela, Gen, Ill, Ine, Ins, Nom, Prl.

302 tokens (36%) have a non-empty value of Case. 224 types (49%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 173 lemmas (46%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case.
