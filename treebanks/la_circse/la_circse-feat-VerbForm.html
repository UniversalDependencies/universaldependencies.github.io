Treebank Statistics: UD_Latin-CIRCSE: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Gdv are language-specific. It occurs with 6 different values: Fin, Gdv, Ger, Inf, Part, Sup.

4239 tokens (22%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 2832 types (36%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 1093 lemmas (30%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 4 part-of-speech tags: VERB (3944; 21% instances), AUX (262; 1% instances), ADJ (20; 0% instances), NOUN (13; 0% instances).
