Treebank Statistics: UD_Latin-CIRCSE: Features: PronType

This feature is universal but the values Con are language-specific. It occurs with 8 different values: Con, Dem, Ind, Int, Neg, Prs, Rel, Tot.

2164 tokens (11%) have a non-empty value of PronType. 331 types (4%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. 114 lemmas (3%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. The feature is used with 5 part-of-speech tags: DET (1065; 6% instances), PRON (713; 4% instances), ADV (212; 1% instances), SCONJ (168; 1% instances), PART (6; 0% instances).
