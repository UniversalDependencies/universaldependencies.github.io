Treebank Statistics: UD_Latin-CIRCSE: Features: PronType

This feature is universal but the values Con are language-specific. It occurs with 8 different values: Con, Dem, Ind, Int, Neg, Prs, Rel, Tot.

3340 tokens (11%) have a non-empty value of PronType. 355 types (3%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. 113 lemmas (3%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. The feature is used with 5 part-of-speech tags: DET (1641; 6% instances), PRON (1204; 4% instances), ADV (268; 1% instances), SCONJ (214; 1% instances), PART (13; 0% instances).
