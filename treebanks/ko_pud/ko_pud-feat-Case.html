Treebank Statistics: UD_Korean-PUD: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Acc, Gen, Nom.

3187 tokens (19%) have a non-empty value of Case. 2070 types (25%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 1617 lemmas (62%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. The feature is used with 8 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (2260; 14% instances), PROPN (440; 3% instances), PRON (309; 2% instances), PART (166; 1% instances), ADJ (5; 0% instances), NUM (5; 0% instances), AUX (1; 0% instances), DET (1; 0% instances).
