Treebank Statistics: UD_Korean-PUD: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Fut, Past.

673 tokens (4%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 361 types (4%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 24 lemmas (1%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense.
