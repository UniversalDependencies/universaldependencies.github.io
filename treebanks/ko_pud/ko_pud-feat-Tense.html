Treebank Statistics: UD_Korean-PUD: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Fut, Past.

673 tokens (4%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 361 types (4%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 26 lemmas (1%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 4 part-of-speech tags: VERB (447; 3% instances), AUX (150; 1% instances), ADJ (57; 0% instances), PART (19; 0% instances).
