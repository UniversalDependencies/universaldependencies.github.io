Treebank Statistics: UD_Italian: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Cnd, Imp, Ind, Sub.

20353 tokens (7%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 3701 types (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 1288 lemmas (7%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (10314; 4% instances), AUX (10039; 3% instances).
