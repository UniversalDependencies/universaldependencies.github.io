Treebank Statistics: UD_Italian-ISDT: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Imp, Past, Pres.

29904 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 6392 types (23%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 1884 lemmas (10%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (18877; 6% instances), AUX (11025; 4% instances), NOUN (2; 0% instances).
