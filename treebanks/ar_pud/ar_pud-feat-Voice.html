Treebank Statistics: UD_Arabic-PUD: Features: Voice

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Act, Pass.

2162 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Voice. 1196 types (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. 751 lemmas (16%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice.
