Treebank Statistics: UD_Arabic-PUD: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

1981 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 1063 types (16%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 635 lemmas (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 4 part-of-speech tags: VERB (1780; 9% instances), AUX (145; 1% instances), NOUN (49; 0% instances), ADJ (7; 0% instances).
