This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

1981 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 1063 types (16%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 667 lemmas (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense.
