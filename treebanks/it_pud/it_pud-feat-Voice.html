Treebank Statistics: UD_Italian-PUD: Features: Voice

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Act, Pass.

2467 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Voice. 1215 types (20%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. 575 lemmas (12%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (1546; 7% instances), AUX (921; 4% instances).

VERB

1546 VERB tokens (75% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Voice.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Voice co-occurred: Gender=EMPTY (1379; 89%), Aspect=EMPTY (1141; 74%), Person=3 (858; 55%), Mood=Ind (844; 55%).

VERB tokens may have the following values of Voice:

  • Act (1408; 91% of non-empty Voice): avere, far, ha, fare, afferma, partire, portare, aveva, iniziò, sono
  • Pass (138; 9% of non-empty Voice): diretto, inclusi, pubblicato, accusato, causato, dato, fatto, fondato, guidati, impiegato
  • EMPTY (508): affermato, detto, fatto, avuto, portato, deciso, raggiunto, considerate, diventata, messo
