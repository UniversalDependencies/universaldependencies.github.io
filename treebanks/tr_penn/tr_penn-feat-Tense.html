Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish-Penn: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Past, Pqp, Pres.

20137 tokens (11%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 6010 types (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 947 lemmas (6%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (19860; 11% instances), AUX (277; 0% instances).
