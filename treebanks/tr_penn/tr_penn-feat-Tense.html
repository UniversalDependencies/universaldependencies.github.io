Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish-Penn: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

16141 tokens (9%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 5113 types (15%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 1487 lemmas (10%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (15985; 9% instances), AUX (156; 0% instances).

15985 VERB tokens (100% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Tense.
