Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish-Penn: Features: Voice

This feature is universal but the values CauPass, Rfl are language-specific. It occurs with 5 different values: Cau, CauPass, Pass, Rcp, Rfl.

2119 tokens (1%) have a non-empty value of Voice. 1072 types (3%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. 414 lemmas (3%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. The feature is used with 1 part-of-speech tags: VERB (2119; 1% instances).
