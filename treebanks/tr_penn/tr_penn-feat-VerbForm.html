Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish-Penn: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv, Vnoun are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Conv, Fin, Part, Vnoun.

7164 tokens (8%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 2555 types (12%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 793 lemmas (9%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (7133; 8% instances), AUX (31; 0% instances).

7133 VERB tokens (85% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of VerbForm.
