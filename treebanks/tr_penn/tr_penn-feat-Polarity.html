Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish-Penn: Features: Polarity

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Neg, Pos.

26342 tokens (14%) have a non-empty value of Polarity. 8430 types (24%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Polarity. 1118 lemmas (8%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Polarity. The feature is used with 5 part-of-speech tags: VERB (25718; 14% instances), AUX (424; 0% instances), ADJ (112; 0% instances), NOUN (52; 0% instances), ADV (36; 0% instances).
