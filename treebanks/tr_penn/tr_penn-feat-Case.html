Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish-Penn: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 7 different values: Abl, Acc, Dat, Gen, Ins, Loc, Nom.

37508 tokens (43%) have a non-empty value of Case. 14900 types (72%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 6956 lemmas (76%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case.
