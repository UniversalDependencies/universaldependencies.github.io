Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish-Penn: Features: Aspect

This feature is universal but the values Hab, Prosp are language-specific. It occurs with 5 different values: Hab, Imp, Perf, Prog, Prosp.

18179 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Aspect. 5177 types (15%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. 1227 lemmas (8%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect.
