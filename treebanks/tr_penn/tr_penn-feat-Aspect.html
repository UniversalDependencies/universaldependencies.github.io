Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish-Penn: Features: Aspect

This feature is universal but the values Hab are language-specific. It occurs with 3 different values: Hab, Perf, Prog.

7423 tokens (8%) have a non-empty value of Aspect. 2711 types (13%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. 822 lemmas (9%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (7392; 8% instances), AUX (31; 0% instances).

VERB

7392 VERB tokens (88% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Aspect.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Aspect co-occurred: VerbForm=Fin (7050; 95%), Person=3 (6966; 94%), Number=Sing (6861; 93%), Mood=Ind (6457; 87%), Polarity=Pos (6241; 84%), Voice=EMPTY (6165; 83%), Tense=Past (4067; 55%).
