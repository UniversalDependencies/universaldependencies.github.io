Treebank Statistics: UD_Ancient_Greek-PROIEL: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Past, Pqp, Pres.

43598 tokens (20%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 16925 types (52%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 2862 lemmas (31%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (39663; 19% instances), AUX (3935; 2% instances).
