Treebank Statistics: UD_Ancient_Greek-PROIEL: Features: Voice

This feature is universal but the values Mid are language-specific. It occurs with 3 different values: Act, Mid, Pass. Some words have combined values of the feature; 1 combinations have been observed: Mid|Pass.

43598 tokens (20%) have a non-empty value of Voice. 16925 types (52%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. 2862 lemmas (31%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (39663; 19% instances), AUX (3935; 2% instances).
