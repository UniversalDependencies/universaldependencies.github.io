Treebank Statistics: UD_Czech-CAC: Features: Polarity

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Neg, Pos.

136115 tokens (28%) have a non-empty value of Polarity. 36252 types (58%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Polarity. 13095 lemmas (46%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Polarity. The feature is used with 4 part-of-speech tags: ADJ (72708; 15% instances), VERB (38748; 8% instances), AUX (14049; 3% instances), ADV (10610; 2% instances).
