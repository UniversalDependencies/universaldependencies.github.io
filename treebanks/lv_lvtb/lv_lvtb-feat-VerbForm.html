Treebank Statistics: UD_Latvian-LVTB: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Conv, Fin, Inf, Part.

45729 tokens (18%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 16247 types (35%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 4079 lemmas (19%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (38953; 15% instances), AUX (6724; 3% instances), ADJ (52; 0% instances).
