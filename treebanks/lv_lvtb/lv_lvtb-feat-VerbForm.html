Treebank Statistics: UD_Latvian-LVTB: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Conv, Fin, Inf, Part.

58264 tokens (18%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 18931 types (35%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 4347 lemmas (19%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (49273; 15% instances), AUX (8918; 3% instances), ADJ (73; 0% instances).
