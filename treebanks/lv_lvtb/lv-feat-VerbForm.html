Treebank Statistics: UD_Latvian: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Conv, Fin, Inf, Part.

15884 tokens (18%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 7133 types (33%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 2833 lemmas (24%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (15840; 17% instances), ADJ (44; 0% instances).
