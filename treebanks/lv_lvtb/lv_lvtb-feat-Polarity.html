Treebank Statistics: UD_Latvian-LVTB: Features: Polarity

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Neg, Pos.

15285 tokens (14%) have a non-empty value of Polarity. 5495 types (22%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Polarity. 2552 lemmas (19%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Polarity. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (12337; 11% instances), AUX (2948; 3% instances).

12337 VERB tokens (76% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Polarity.
