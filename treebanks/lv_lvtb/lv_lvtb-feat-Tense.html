Treebank Statistics: UD_Latvian-LVTB: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

35243 tokens (14%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 12239 types (26%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 3609 lemmas (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (28998; 11% instances), AUX (6245; 2% instances).
