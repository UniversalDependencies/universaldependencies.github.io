Treebank Statistics: UD_Latvian-LVTB: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

36904 tokens (14%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 12602 types (26%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 3683 lemmas (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (30365; 11% instances), AUX (6539; 2% instances).
