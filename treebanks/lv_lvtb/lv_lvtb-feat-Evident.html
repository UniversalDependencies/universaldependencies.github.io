Treebank Statistics: UD_Latvian-LVTB: Features: Evident

This feature is universal but the values Fh are language-specific. It occurs with 2 different values: Fh, Nfh.

28036 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Evident. 6650 types (13%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Evident. 2795 lemmas (13%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Evident.
