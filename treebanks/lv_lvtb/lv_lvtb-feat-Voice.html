Treebank Statistics: UD_Latvian-LVTB: Features: Voice

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Act, Pass.

13655 tokens (12%) have a non-empty value of Voice. 5008 types (20%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. 2371 lemmas (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (10736; 10% instances), AUX (2863; 3% instances), ADJ (56; 0% instances).
