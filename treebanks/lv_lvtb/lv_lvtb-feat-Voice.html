Treebank Statistics: UD_Latvian-LVTB: Features: Voice

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Act, Pass.

30860 tokens (15%) have a non-empty value of Voice. 11662 types (28%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. 3450 lemmas (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (25121; 12% instances), AUX (5621; 3% instances), ADJ (118; 0% instances).
