Treebank Statistics: UD_Serbian: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

8939 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 1377 types (8%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 812 lemmas (9%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: AUX (5162; 6% instances), VERB (3777; 4% instances).

5162 AUX tokens (93% of all AUX tokens) have a non-empty value of Tense.

The most frequent other feature values with which AUX and Tense co-occurred: Person=3 (5016; 97%), Number=Sing (3954; 77%).
