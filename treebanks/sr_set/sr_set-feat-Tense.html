Treebank Statistics: UD_Serbian-SET: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

14096 tokens (14%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 2981 types (16%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 1241 lemmas (13%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (7761; 8% instances), AUX (6054; 6% instances), ADV (281; 0% instances).

7761 VERB tokens (92% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Tense.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Tense co-occurred: Number=Sing (5363; 69%), Gender=EMPTY (4409; 57%), Mood=Ind (4409; 57%), VerbForm=Fin (4409; 57%), Voice=EMPTY (4409; 57%), Person=3 (4023; 52%).

  • Past (3353; 43% of non-empty Tense): rekao, izjavio, trebalo, rekla, dodao, mogla, saopštila, sastao, dobila, pozvao
  • Pres (4308; 56% of non-empty Tense): kaže, ima, može, treba, mora, mogu, navodi, postoji, kažu, očekuje
