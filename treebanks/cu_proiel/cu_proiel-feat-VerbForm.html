Treebank Statistics: UD_Old_Church_Slavonic-PROIEL: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values PartRes are language-specific. It occurs with 5 different values: Fin, Inf, Part, PartRes, Sup.

14778 tokens (26%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 4719 types (48%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 1109 lemmas (37%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (12698; 22% instances), AUX (2080; 4% instances).
