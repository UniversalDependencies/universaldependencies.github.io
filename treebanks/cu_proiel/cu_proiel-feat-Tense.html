Treebank Statistics: UD_Old_Church_Slavonic-PROIEL: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

14713 tokens (26%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 4696 types (48%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 1108 lemmas (37%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (12633; 22% instances), AUX (2080; 4% instances).
