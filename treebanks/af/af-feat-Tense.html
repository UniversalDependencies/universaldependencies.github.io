Treebank Statistics: UD_Afrikaans: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Past, Pres.

7591 tokens (15%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 1030 types (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 797 lemmas (15%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (4317; 9% instances), AUX (3274; 7% instances).
