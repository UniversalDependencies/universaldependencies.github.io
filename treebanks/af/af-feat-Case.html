Treebank Statistics: UD_Afrikaans: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Acc, Nom. Some words have combined values of the feature; 1 combinations have been observed: Acc|Nom.

4642 tokens (9%) have a non-empty value of Case. 1005 types (16%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 791 lemmas (15%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: ADJ (3172; 6% instances), PRON (1470; 3% instances).
