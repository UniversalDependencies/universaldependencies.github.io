Treebank Statistics: UD_Catalan: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Imp, Past, Pres.

45009 tokens (8%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 5572 types (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 1565 lemmas (7%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (24105; 5% instances), AUX (20904; 4% instances).

24105 VERB tokens (60% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Tense.
