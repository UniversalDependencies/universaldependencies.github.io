Treebank Statistics: UD_Catalan-AnCora: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fin, Ger, Inf, Part.

69537 tokens (13%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 9145 types (28%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 2936 lemmas (13%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (41894; 8% instances), AUX (22054; 4% instances), ADJ (5589; 1% instances).
