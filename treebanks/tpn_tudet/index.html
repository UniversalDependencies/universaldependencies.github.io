Description

UD_Tupinamba-TuDeT is a collection of annotated sentences in Tupinambá. All known sources in this language are being annotated: cathecisms, letters, poems, theater plays, and grammars (sixteenth and seventeenth century). Sentence annotation and documentation by Fabrício Ferraz Gerardi.

UD_Tupinamba-TuDeT is a collection of annotated sentences in Tupinambá. All known sources in this language are being annotated: cathecisms, letters, poems, theater plays, and grammars (sixteenth and seventeenth century). It is part of TuLaR, Tupían Language Resources. The project is work-in-progress and the treebank is being updated on a regular basis.
