Treebank Statistics: UD_Hindi: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Conv, Fin, Inf, Part.

52634 tokens (15%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 1966 types (10%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 559 lemmas (4%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 10 part-of-speech tags: VERB (27978; 8% instances), AUX (24639; 7% instances), ADP (5; 0% instances), PROPN (4; 0% instances), NOUN (3; 0% instances), ADJ (1; 0% instances), ADV (1; 0% instances), PRON (1; 0% instances), PUNCT (1; 0% instances), X (1; 0% instances).
