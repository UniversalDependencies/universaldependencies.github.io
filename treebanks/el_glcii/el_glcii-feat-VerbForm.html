Treebank Statistics: UD_Greek-GLCII: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Conv, Fin, Inf, Part.

1543 tokens (16%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 831 types (30%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 415 lemmas (21%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (1277; 13% instances), AUX (266; 3% instances).

VERB

1277 VERB tokens (100% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of VerbForm.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and VerbForm co-occurred: Mood=Ind (1199; 94%), Voice=Act (1078; 84%), Aspect=Imp (809; 63%), Tense=Pres (705; 55%), Number=Sing (684; 54%).
