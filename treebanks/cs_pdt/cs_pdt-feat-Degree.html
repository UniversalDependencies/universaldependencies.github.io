Treebank Statistics: UD_Czech-PDT: Features: Degree

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Cmp, Pos, Sup.

191344 tokens (13%) have a non-empty value of Degree. 39570 types (31%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Degree. 13745 lemmas (24%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Degree.
