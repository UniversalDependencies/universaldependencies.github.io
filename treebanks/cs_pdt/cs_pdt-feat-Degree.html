Treebank Statistics: UD_Czech-PDT: Features: Degree

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Cmp, Pos, Sup.

41146 tokens (12%) have a non-empty value of Degree. 15044 types (28%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Degree. 6252 lemmas (23%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Degree. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: ADJ (35543; 11% instances), ADV (5603; 2% instances).
