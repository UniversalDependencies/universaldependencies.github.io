Treebank Statistics: UD_Czech-PDT: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

145235 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 21497 types (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 5999 lemmas (10%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (110182; 7% instances), AUX (30555; 2% instances), ADJ (4498; 0% instances).
