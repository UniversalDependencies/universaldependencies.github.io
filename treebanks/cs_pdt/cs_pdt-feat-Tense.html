Treebank Statistics: UD_Czech-PDT: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

32629 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 8986 types (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 3233 lemmas (12%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (23528; 7% instances), AUX (8170; 2% instances), ADJ (931; 0% instances).
