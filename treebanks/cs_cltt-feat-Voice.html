Treebank Statistics: UD_Czech-CLTT: Features: Voice

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Act, Pass.

2587 tokens (7%) have a non-empty value of Voice. 642 types (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. 389 lemmas (15%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (1542; 4% instances), ADJ (548; 2% instances), AUX (497; 1% instances).
