Treebank Statistics: UD_Portuguese-Bosque: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fin, Ger, Inf, Part.

25750 tokens (11%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 7600 types (29%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 1949 lemmas (11%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm.
