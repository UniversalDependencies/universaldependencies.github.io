Treebank Statistics: UD_Portuguese-Bosque: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 5 different values: Fut, Imp, Past, Pqp, Pres.

15141 tokens (7%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 3872 types (15%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 1279 lemmas (7%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (11239; 5% instances), AUX (3902; 2% instances).
