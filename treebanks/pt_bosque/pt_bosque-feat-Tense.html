Treebank Statistics: UD_Portuguese-Bosque: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 5 different values: Fut, Imp, Past, Pqp, Pres.

15126 tokens (7%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 3870 types (15%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 1285 lemmas (7%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (11244; 5% instances), AUX (3882; 2% instances).
