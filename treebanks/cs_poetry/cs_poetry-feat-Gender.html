Treebank Statistics: UD_Czech-Poetry: Features: Gender

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fem, Masc, Neut.

2747 tokens (44%) have a non-empty value of Gender. 2009 types (75%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Gender. 1380 lemmas (72%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Gender.
