Treebank Statistics: UD_Old_East_Slavic-Birchbark: Features: Voice

This feature is universal but the values Mid are language-specific. It occurs with 3 different values: Act, Mid, Pass.

2947 tokens (11%) have a non-empty value of Voice. 2136 types (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. 677 lemmas (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (2578; 9% instances), AUX (369; 1% instances).

VERB

2578 VERB tokens (98% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Voice.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Voice co-occurred: Number=Sing (1931; 75%), Mood=EMPTY (1799; 70%), Gender=EMPTY (1752; 68%), VerbForm=Fin (1476; 57%).
