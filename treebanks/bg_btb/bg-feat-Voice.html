Treebank Statistics: UD_Bulgarian: Features: Voice

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Act, Pass.

22666 tokens (15%) have a non-empty value of Voice. 7792 types (30%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. 2932 lemmas (20%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (16904; 11% instances), AUX (4276; 3% instances), ADJ (1486; 1% instances).
