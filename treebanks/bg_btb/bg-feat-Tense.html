Treebank Statistics: UD_Bulgarian: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Imp, Past, Pres.

20450 tokens (13%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 6412 types (24%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 2685 lemmas (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (15835; 10% instances), AUX (4094; 3% instances), ADJ (521; 0% instances).
