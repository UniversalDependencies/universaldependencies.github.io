Treebank Statistics: UD_Polish-MPDT: Features: Aspect

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Imp, Perf.

7233 tokens (15%) have a non-empty value of Aspect. 4327 types (32%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. 1977 lemmas (26%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. The feature is used with 4 part-of-speech tags: VERB (5136; 11% instances), AUX (881; 2% instances), ADJ (832; 2% instances), NOUN (384; 1% instances).
