Treebank Statistics: UD_Polish-MPDT: Features: Degree

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Cmp, Pos, Sup.

5343 tokens (11%) have a non-empty value of Degree. 3413 types (25%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Degree. 1882 lemmas (25%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Degree. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: ADJ (4478; 9% instances), ADV (865; 2% instances).
