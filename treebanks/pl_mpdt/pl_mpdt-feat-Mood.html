Treebank Statistics: UD_Polish-MPDT: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Imp, Ind.

4618 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 2435 types (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 1314 lemmas (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (4020; 9% instances), AUX (598; 1% instances).

VERB

4020 VERB tokens (77% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Mood.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Mood co-occurred: VerbForm=Fin (4020; 100%), Voice=Act (3970; 99%), Number=Sing (2812; 70%), Aspect=Imp (2575; 64%), Gender=EMPTY (2215; 55%).

VERB tokens may have the following values of Mood:

  • Imp (201; 5% of non-empty Mood): powiedz, weźmi, dajmy, zmiłuj, czyń, daj, przydź, Idź, bójcie, czyńcie
  • Ind (3819; 95% of non-empty Mood): ma, jest, może, miał, mają, masz, trzeba, było, mieli, można
  • EMPTY (1214): mieć, mówić, czynić, mając, być, dać, mówiąc, chcąc, uczynić, widzieć
