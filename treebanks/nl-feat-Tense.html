Treebank Statistics: UD_Dutch: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Past, Pres.

25605 tokens (12%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 4250 types (16%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 2778 lemmas (13%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 4 part-of-speech tags: VERB (16042; 8% instances), AUX (9435; 5% instances), X (127; 0% instances), PUNCT (1; 0% instances).
