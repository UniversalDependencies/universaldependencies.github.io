Treebank Statistics: UD_Uyghur-UDT: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Past, Pres.

2071 tokens (5%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 557 types (5%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 222 lemmas (8%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (1683; 4% instances), AUX (386; 1% instances), PART (2; 0% instances).
