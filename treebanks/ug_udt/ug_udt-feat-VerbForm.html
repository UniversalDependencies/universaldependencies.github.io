Treebank Statistics: UD_Uyghur-UDT: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv, Cov are language-specific. It occurs with 5 different values: Conv, Cov, Fin, Ger, Part.

4432 tokens (11%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 1137 types (9%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 369 lemmas (13%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (3859; 10% instances), AUX (571; 1% instances), PART (2; 0% instances).
