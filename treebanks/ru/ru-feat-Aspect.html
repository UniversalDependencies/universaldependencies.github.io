Treebank Statistics: UD_Russian: Features: Aspect

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Imp, Perf.

9311 tokens (9%) have a non-empty value of Aspect. 4837 types (16%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. 2105 lemmas (11%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (8336; 8% instances), AUX (975; 1% instances).
