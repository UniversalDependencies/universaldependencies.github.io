Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish-GB: Features: Case

This feature is universal but the values Equ are language-specific. It occurs with 8 different values: Abl, Acc, Dat, Equ, Gen, Ins, Loc, Nom.

6491 tokens (38%) have a non-empty value of Case. 2938 types (58%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 1229 lemmas (60%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case.
