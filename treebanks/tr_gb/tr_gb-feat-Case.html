Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish-GB: Features: Case

This feature is universal but the values Equ are language-specific. It occurs with 8 different values: Abl, Acc, Dat, Equ, Gen, Ins, Loc, Nom.

6783 tokens (39%) have a non-empty value of Case. 3052 types (60%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 1278 lemmas (62%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. The feature is used with 8 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (4621; 27% instances), PRON (1070; 6% instances), VERB (499; 3% instances), PROPN (392; 2% instances), ADJ (72; 0% instances), NUM (72; 0% instances), AUX (51; 0% instances), ADP (6; 0% instances).
