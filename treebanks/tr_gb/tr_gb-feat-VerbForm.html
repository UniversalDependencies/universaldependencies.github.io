Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish-GB: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv, Vnoun are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Conv, Fin, Part, Vnoun.

3638 tokens (21%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 2138 types (42%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 474 lemmas (23%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (3085; 18% instances), AUX (553; 3% instances).

3085 VERB tokens (100% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of VerbForm.
