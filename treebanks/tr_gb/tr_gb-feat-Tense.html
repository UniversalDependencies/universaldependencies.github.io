Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish-GB: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Past, Pqp, Pres. Some words have combined values of the feature; 1 combinations have been observed: Fut|Past.

3189 tokens (19%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 1834 types (36%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 394 lemmas (19%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (2642; 15% instances), AUX (535; 3% instances), ADJ (12; 0% instances).
