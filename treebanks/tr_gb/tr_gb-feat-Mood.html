Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish-GB: Features: Mood

This feature is universal but the values CndGen, CndGenPot, CndPot, Gen, GenNec, GenPot, GenPotPot, PotPot are language-specific. It occurs with 14 different values: Cnd, CndGen, CndGenPot, CndPot, Gen, GenNec, GenPot, GenPotPot, Imp, Ind, Nec, Opt, Pot, PotPot.

3079 tokens (18%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 1779 types (35%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 450 lemmas (22%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (2564; 15% instances), AUX (503; 3% instances), ADJ (12; 0% instances).
