Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish-GB: Features: Aspect

This feature is universal but the values Dur, Hab, Prosp, Rapid are language-specific. It occurs with 7 different values: Dur, Hab, Imp, Perf, Prog, Prosp, Rapid.

2197 tokens (13%) have a non-empty value of Aspect. 1309 types (26%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. 271 lemmas (13%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (2061; 12% instances), AUX (136; 1% instances).
