Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish-GB: Features: Number

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Plur, Sing.

This is a layered feature with the following layers: Number, Number[psor].

10505 tokens (62%) have a non-empty value of Number. 4595 types (91%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Number. 1505 lemmas (73%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Number.
