Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish-GB: Features: Number

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Plur, Sing.

This is a layered feature with the following layers: Number, Number[psor].

9766 tokens (57%) have a non-empty value of Number. 4650 types (91%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Number. 1517 lemmas (74%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Number. The feature is used with 8 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (4510; 26% instances), VERB (2596; 15% instances), PRON (1070; 6% instances), PROPN (914; 5% instances), AUX (578; 3% instances), NUM (78; 0% instances), ADP (13; 0% instances), ADJ (7; 0% instances).
