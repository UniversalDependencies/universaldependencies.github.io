Treebank Statistics: UD_Arabic: Features: Gender

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Fem, Masc.

66056 tokens (23%) have a non-empty value of Gender. 9183 types (35%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Gender. 3386 lemmas (20%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Gender.
