Treebank Statistics: UD_Lithuanian-ALKSNIS: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

8020 tokens (11%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 4417 types (25%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 1882 lemmas (22%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (7482; 11% instances), AUX (538; 1% instances).
