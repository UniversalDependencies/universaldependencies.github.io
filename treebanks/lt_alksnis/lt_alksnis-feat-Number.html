Treebank Statistics: UD_Lithuanian-ALKSNIS: Features: Number

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Dual, Plur, Sing.

38135 tokens (54%) have a non-empty value of Number. 16208 types (91%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Number. 6919 lemmas (80%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Number.
