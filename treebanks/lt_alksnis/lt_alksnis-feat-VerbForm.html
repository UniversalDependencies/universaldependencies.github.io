Treebank Statistics: UD_Lithuanian-ALKSNIS: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv are language-specific. It occurs with 5 different values: Conv, Fin, Ger, Inf, Part.

10863 tokens (16%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 5744 types (32%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 2294 lemmas (27%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (10177; 15% instances), AUX (684; 1% instances), ADV (2; 0% instances).
