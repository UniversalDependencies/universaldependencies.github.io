Treebank Statistics: UD_Slovak: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

15974 tokens (15%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 5956 types (23%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 2419 lemmas (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (12499; 12% instances), AUX (3470; 3% instances), ADJ (5; 0% instances).
