Treebank Statistics: UD_French-Rhapsodie: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fin, Inf, Part.

5744 tokens (13%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 1365 types (30%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 606 lemmas (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (4083; 9% instances), AUX (1659; 4% instances), ADJ (2; 0% instances).
