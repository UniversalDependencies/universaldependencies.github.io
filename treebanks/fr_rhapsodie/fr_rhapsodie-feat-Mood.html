Treebank Statistics: UD_French-Rhapsodie: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Cnd, Imp, Ind, Sub.

4067 tokens (9%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 711 types (16%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 351 lemmas (10%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (2518; 6% instances), AUX (1549; 4% instances).
