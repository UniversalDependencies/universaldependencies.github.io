Treebank Statistics: UD_Latin-ITTB: Features: PronType

This feature is universal but the values Con are language-specific. It occurs with 8 different values: Art, Con, Dem, Ind, Int, Neg, Prs, Rel.

58164 tokens (13%) have a non-empty value of PronType. 454 types (2%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. 83 lemmas (1%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. The feature is used with 6 part-of-speech tags: DET (27074; 6% instances), PRON (22591; 5% instances), SCONJ (5249; 1% instances), ADV (3248; 1% instances), ADP (1; 0% instances), CCONJ (1; 0% instances).
