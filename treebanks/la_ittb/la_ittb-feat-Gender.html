Treebank Statistics: UD_Latin-ITTB: Features: Gender

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fem, Masc, Neut.

192875 tokens (43%) have a non-empty value of Gender. 13359 types (68%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Gender. 4233 lemmas (74%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Gender.
