Treebank Statistics: UD_Latin-ITTB: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Gdv are language-specific. It occurs with 5 different values: Fin, Gdv, Ger, Inf, Part.

83627 tokens (19%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 9622 types (49%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 1430 lemmas (26%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm.
