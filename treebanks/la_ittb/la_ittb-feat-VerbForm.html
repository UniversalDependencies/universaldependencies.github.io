Treebank Statistics: UD_Latin-ITTB: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fin, Inf, Part.

83730 tokens (19%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 9622 types (49%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 1444 lemmas (25%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 7 part-of-speech tags: VERB (59766; 13% instances), AUX (23590; 5% instances), CCONJ (205; 0% instances), ADJ (58; 0% instances), NOUN (57; 0% instances), SCONJ (42; 0% instances), ADV (12; 0% instances).
