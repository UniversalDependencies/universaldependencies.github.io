Treebank Statistics: UD_Latin-ITTB: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Gdv are language-specific. It occurs with 5 different values: Fin, Gdv, Ger, Inf, Part.

53813 tokens (18%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 6067 types (46%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 1056 lemmas (29%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (42090; 14% instances), AUX (11723; 4% instances).
