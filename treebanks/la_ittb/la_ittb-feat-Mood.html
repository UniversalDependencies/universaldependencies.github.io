Treebank Statistics: UD_Latin-ITTB: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Imp, Ind, Sub.

36918 tokens (13%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 3050 types (23%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 848 lemmas (23%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (26482; 9% instances), AUX (10436; 4% instances).
