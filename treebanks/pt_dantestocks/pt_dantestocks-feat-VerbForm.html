Treebank Statistics: UD_Portuguese-DANTEStocks: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fin, Ger, Inf, Part.

7958 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 2140 types (20%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 698 lemmas (8%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm.
