Treebank Statistics: UD_Portuguese-DANTEStocks: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Cnd, Imp, Ind, Sub.

5725 tokens (7%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 1380 types (13%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 488 lemmas (6%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (4486; 6% instances), AUX (1239; 2% instances).
