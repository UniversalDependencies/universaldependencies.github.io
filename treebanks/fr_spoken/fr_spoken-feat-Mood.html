Treebank Statistics: UD_French-Spoken: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Cnd, Imp, Ind, Sub.

3250 tokens (9%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 408 types (9%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 233 lemmas (7%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (1728; 5% instances), AUX (1522; 4% instances).
