Treebank Statistics: UD_French-Spoken: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Imp, Past, Pres.

3865 tokens (11%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 694 types (15%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 344 lemmas (10%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (2296; 7% instances), AUX (1569; 4% instances).

VERB

2296 VERB tokens (55% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Tense.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Tense co-occurred: Number=Sing (1798; 78%), Gender=EMPTY (1779; 77%), VerbForm=Fin (1728; 75%), Mood=Ind (1603; 70%), Person=3 (1204; 52%).

VERB tokens may have the following values of Tense:

  • Fut (27; 1% of non-empty Tense): aura, serez, pourra, sera, acceptera, aurai, changera, devra, dira, faudra
  • Imp (220; 10% of non-empty Tense): avait, avais, était, avaient, pouvait, étais, connaissais, faisait, allait, avions
  • Past (519; 23% of non-empty Tense): eu, pu, travaillé, vu, arrivé, vécu, choisi, partis, pris, commencé
  • Pres (1530; 67% of non-empty Tense): a, est, va, faut, ai, peut, sais, vais, vois, veux
  • EMPTY (1864): dire, dit, fait, faire, allez, voilà, crois, pense, avoir, aller
