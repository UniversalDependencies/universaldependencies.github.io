Treebank Statistics: UD_Ancient_Greek-PTNK: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Imp, Ind, Opt, Sub. Some words have combined values of the feature; 2 combinations have been observed: Imp|Ind, Ind|Sub.

4912 tokens (12%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 1851 types (35%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 637 lemmas (29%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (4408; 11% instances), AUX (504; 1% instances).
