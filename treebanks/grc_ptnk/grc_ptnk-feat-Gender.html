Treebank Statistics: UD_Ancient_Greek-PTNK: Features: Gender

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fem, Masc, Neut. Some words have combined values of the feature; 3 combinations have been observed: Fem|Masc, Fem|Masc|Neut, Masc|Neut.

18417 tokens (47%) have a non-empty value of Gender. 2920 types (55%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Gender. 1407 lemmas (63%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Gender.
