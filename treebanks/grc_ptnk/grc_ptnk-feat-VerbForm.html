Treebank Statistics: UD_Ancient_Greek-PTNK: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fin, Inf, Part.

5860 tokens (15%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 2368 types (45%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 711 lemmas (32%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (5325; 13% instances), AUX (535; 1% instances).
