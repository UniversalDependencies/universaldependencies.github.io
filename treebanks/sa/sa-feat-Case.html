Treebank Statistics: UD_Sanskrit: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 8 different values: Abl, Acc, Dat, Gen, Ins, Loc, Nom, Voc.

949 tokens (55%) have a non-empty value of Case. 686 types (70%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 538 lemmas (73%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case.
