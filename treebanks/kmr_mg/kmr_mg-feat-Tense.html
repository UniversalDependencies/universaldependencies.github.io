Treebank Statistics: UD_Kurmanji-MG: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Past, Pqp, Pres.

1325 tokens (13%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 504 types (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 149 lemmas (8%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (970; 9% instances), AUX (336; 3% instances), PART (19; 0% instances).
