This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Imp, Ind, Opt, Sub.

1289 tokens (13%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 500 types (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 145 lemmas (8%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (962; 10% instances), AUX (327; 3% instances).
