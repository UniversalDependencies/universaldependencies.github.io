Treebank Statistics: UD_Kurmanji-MG: Features: Case

This feature is universal but the values Con, Obl are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Con, Nom, Obl, Voc.

3910 tokens (38%) have a non-empty value of Case. 1887 types (68%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 1267 lemmas (70%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. The feature is used with 7 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (2622; 26% instances), PRON (634; 6% instances), PROPN (441; 4% instances), DET (104; 1% instances), NUM (97; 1% instances), ADJ (11; 0% instances), X (1; 0% instances).
