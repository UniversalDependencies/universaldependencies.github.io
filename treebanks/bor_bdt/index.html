Description

UD_Bororo-BDT is a collection of annotated sentences in Bororo. All known sources in this language are being annotated: grammar examples, myths, and other sources. Sentence annotation and documentation by Fabrício Ferraz Gerardi and Carolina C. Aragon.

UD_Bororo-BDT is a collection of annotated sentences in Bororo. All known sources in this language are being annotated: grammar examples, myths, and other sources. The project is work-in-progress and the treebank is being updated on a regular basis.
