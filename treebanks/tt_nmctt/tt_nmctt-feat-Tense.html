Treebank Statistics: UD_Tatar-NMCTT: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

185 tokens (8%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 131 types (11%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 83 lemmas (10%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (163; 7% instances), AUX (22; 1% instances).
