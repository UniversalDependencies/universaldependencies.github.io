Treebank Statistics: UD_Tatar-NMCTT: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv, NomAg, Vnoun are language-specific. It occurs with 6 different values: Conv, Fin, Inf, NomAg, Part, Vnoun.

370 tokens (16%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 281 types (23%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 134 lemmas (16%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (342; 15% instances), AUX (28; 1% instances).
