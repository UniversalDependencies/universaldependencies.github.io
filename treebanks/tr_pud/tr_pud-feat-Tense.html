Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish-PUD: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Past, Pqp, Pres.

1929 tokens (11%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 1221 types (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 558 lemmas (13%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (1651; 10% instances), AUX (278; 2% instances).

1651 VERB tokens (97% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Tense.
