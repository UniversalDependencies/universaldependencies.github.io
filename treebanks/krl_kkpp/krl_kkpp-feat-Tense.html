Treebank Statistics: UD_Karelian-KKPP: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Past, Pres.

391 tokens (13%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 229 types (16%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 135 lemmas (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (276; 9% instances), AUX (114; 4% instances), ADV (1; 0% instances).
