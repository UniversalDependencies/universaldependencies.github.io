Treebank Statistics: UD_Karelian-KKPP: Features: Voice

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Act, Pass.

418 tokens (14%) have a non-empty value of Voice. 249 types (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. 143 lemmas (15%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. The feature is used with 5 part-of-speech tags: VERB (290; 9% instances), AUX (115; 4% instances), ADP (7; 0% instances), CCONJ (5; 0% instances), ADV (1; 0% instances).
