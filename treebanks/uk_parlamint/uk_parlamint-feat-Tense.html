Treebank Statistics: UD_Ukrainian-ParlaMint: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

4059 tokens (8%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 1421 types (16%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 790 lemmas (16%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (3647; 7% instances), AUX (403; 1% instances), ADJ (9; 0% instances).
