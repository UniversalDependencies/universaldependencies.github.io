Treebank Statistics: UD_Ukrainian-ParlaMint: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Conv, Fin, Inf, Part.

13961 tokens (13%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 4251 types (29%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 1905 lemmas (25%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (12025; 11% instances), ADJ (994; 1% instances), AUX (942; 1% instances).
