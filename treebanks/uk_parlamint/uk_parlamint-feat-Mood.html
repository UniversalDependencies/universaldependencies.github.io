Treebank Statistics: UD_Ukrainian-ParlaMint: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Cnd, Imp, Ind.

4639 tokens (9%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 1507 types (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 801 lemmas (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (4165; 8% instances), AUX (474; 1% instances).
