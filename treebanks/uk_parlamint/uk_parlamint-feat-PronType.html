Treebank Statistics: UD_Ukrainian-ParlaMint: Features: PronType

This feature is universal but the values Emp are language-specific. It occurs with 9 different values: Dem, Emp, Ind, Int, Neg, Prs, Rcp, Rel, Tot.

11658 tokens (11%) have a non-empty value of PronType. 465 types (3%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. 114 lemmas (2%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. The feature is used with 4 part-of-speech tags: PRON (6009; 6% instances), DET (4269; 4% instances), ADV (1249; 1% instances), SCONJ (131; 0% instances).
