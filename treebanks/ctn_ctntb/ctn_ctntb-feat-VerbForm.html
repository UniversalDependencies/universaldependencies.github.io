Treebank Statistics: UD_Chintang-CTNTB: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Conv, Fin, Inf, Part.

3341 tokens (23%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 2336 types (53%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 578 lemmas (35%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (3323; 23% instances), AUX (18; 0% instances).
