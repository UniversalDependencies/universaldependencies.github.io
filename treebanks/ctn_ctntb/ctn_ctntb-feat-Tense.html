Treebank Statistics: UD_Chintang-CTNTB: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Past, Pres.

2375 tokens (16%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 1705 types (39%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 426 lemmas (25%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (2361; 16% instances), AUX (14; 0% instances).
