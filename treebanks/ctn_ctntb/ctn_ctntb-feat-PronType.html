Treebank Statistics: UD_Chintang-CTNTB: Features: PronType

This feature is universal. It occurs with 6 different values: Dem, Ind, Int, Prs, Rel, Tot.

1579 tokens (11%) have a non-empty value of PronType. 379 types (9%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. 41 lemmas (2%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType.
