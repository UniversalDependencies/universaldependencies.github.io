Treebank Statistics: UD_Chintang-CTNTB: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Imp, Ind, Opt, Sub.

2581 tokens (18%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 1873 types (43%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 471 lemmas (28%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (2567; 18% instances), AUX (14; 0% instances).
