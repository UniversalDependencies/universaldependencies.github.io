Treebank Statistics: UD_Danish: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Past, Pres.

12434 tokens (12%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 2418 types (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 1316 lemmas (10%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (7853; 8% instances), AUX (4581; 5% instances).
