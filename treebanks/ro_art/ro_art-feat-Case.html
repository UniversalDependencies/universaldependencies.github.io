Treebank Statistics: UD_Romanian-ArT: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 5 different values: Acc, Dat, Gen, Nom, Voc. Some words have combined values of the feature; 2 combinations have been observed: Acc|Nom, Dat|Gen.

198 tokens (35%) have a non-empty value of Case. 135 types (45%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 92 lemmas (41%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. The feature is used with 6 part-of-speech tags: PRON (71; 12% instances), NOUN (63; 11% instances), ADP (41; 7% instances), DET (12; 2% instances), ADJ (6; 1% instances), NUM (5; 1% instances).
