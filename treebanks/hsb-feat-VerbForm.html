Treebank Statistics: UD_Upper_Sorbian: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Conv, Fin, Inf, Part.

1251 tokens (12%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 710 types (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 472 lemmas (16%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (780; 7% instances), AUX (285; 3% instances), ADJ (186; 2% instances).

VERB

780 VERB tokens (100% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of VerbForm.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and VerbForm co-occurred: Mood=Ind (599; 77%), Person=3 (566; 73%), Number=Sing (419; 54%), Tense=Pres (414; 53%).

  • Part (48; 6% of non-empty VerbForm): přewzali, wužiwali, započał, změnili, dodźeržała, eksistowali, ilustrował, kontrolowali, ležo, mał
