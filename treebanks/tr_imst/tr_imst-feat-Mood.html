Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish-IMST: Features: Mood

This feature is universal but the values CndPot, DesPot, Gen, GenNec, GenNecPot, GenPot, NecPot, Prs are language-specific. It occurs with 15 different values: Cnd, CndPot, Des, DesPot, Gen, GenNec, GenNecPot, GenPot, Imp, Ind, Nec, NecPot, Opt, Pot, Prs.

12036 tokens (21%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 6521 types (38%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 828 lemmas (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (10995; 19% instances), AUX (1041; 2% instances).
