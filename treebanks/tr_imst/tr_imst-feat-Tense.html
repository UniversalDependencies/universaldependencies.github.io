Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish-IMST: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Past, Pqp, Pres. Some words have combined values of the feature; 1 combinations have been observed: Fut|Past.

12035 tokens (21%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 6521 types (38%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 828 lemmas (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (10995; 19% instances), AUX (1040; 2% instances).
