Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish-IMST: Features: Case

This feature is universal but the values Equ are language-specific. It occurs with 8 different values: Abl, Acc, Dat, Equ, Gen, Ins, Loc, Nom.

25168 tokens (43%) have a non-empty value of Case. 12391 types (72%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 4684 lemmas (80%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. The feature is used with 7 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (15560; 27% instances), ADJ (2417; 4% instances), PRON (2276; 4% instances), VERB (2259; 4% instances), PROPN (2211; 4% instances), NUM (349; 1% instances), ADP (96; 0% instances).
