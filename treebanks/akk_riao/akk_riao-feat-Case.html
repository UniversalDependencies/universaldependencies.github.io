Treebank Statistics: UD_Akkadian-RIAO: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Acc, Gen, Loc, Nom.

4358 tokens (20%) have a non-empty value of Case. 722 types (26%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 525 lemmas (35%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. The feature is used with 7 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (3005; 14% instances), PROPN (765; 3% instances), ADJ (527; 2% instances), VERB (29; 0% instances), DET (16; 0% instances), NUM (14; 0% instances), PRON (2; 0% instances).
