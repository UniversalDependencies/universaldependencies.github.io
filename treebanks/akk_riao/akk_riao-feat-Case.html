Treebank Statistics: UD_Akkadian-RIAO: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Acc, Gen, Loc, Nom.

4693 tokens (20%) have a non-empty value of Case. 771 types (26%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 557 lemmas (35%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. The feature is used with 8 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (3242; 14% instances), PROPN (806; 3% instances), ADJ (583; 2% instances), VERB (29; 0% instances), DET (16; 0% instances), NUM (14; 0% instances), PRON (2; 0% instances), ADP (1; 0% instances).
