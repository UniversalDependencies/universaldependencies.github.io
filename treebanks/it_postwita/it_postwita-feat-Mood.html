Treebank Statistics: UD_Italian-PoSTWITA: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Cnd, Imp, Ind, Sub.

11142 tokens (9%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 2745 types (16%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 987 lemmas (7%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (6973; 6% instances), AUX (4169; 3% instances).
