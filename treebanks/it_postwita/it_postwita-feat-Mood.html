Treebank Statistics: UD_Italian-PoSTWITA: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Cnd, Imp, Ind, Sub.

5833 tokens (9%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 1812 types (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 717 lemmas (7%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood.
