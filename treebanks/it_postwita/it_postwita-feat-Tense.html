Treebank Statistics: UD_Italian-PoSTWITA: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Imp, Past, Pres.

12817 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 3442 types (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 1159 lemmas (7%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (8573; 7% instances), AUX (4244; 3% instances).
