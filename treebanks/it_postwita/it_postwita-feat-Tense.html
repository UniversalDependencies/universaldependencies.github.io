Treebank Statistics: UD_Italian-PoSTWITA: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Imp, Past, Pres.

12821 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 3444 types (20%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 1160 lemmas (9%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (8568; 7% instances), AUX (4253; 3% instances).
