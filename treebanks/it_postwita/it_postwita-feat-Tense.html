Treebank Statistics: UD_Italian-PoSTWITA: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Imp, Past, Pres.

6716 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 2248 types (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 860 lemmas (8%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (4496; 7% instances), AUX (2218; 3% instances), X (2; 0% instances).
