Treebank Statistics: UD_Italian-PoSTWITA: Features: PronType

This feature is universal but the values Exc are language-specific. It occurs with 9 different values: Art, Dem, Exc, Ind, Int, Neg, Prs, Rel, Tot.

22377 tokens (18%) have a non-empty value of PronType. 383 types (2%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. 88 lemmas (1%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. The feature is used with 5 part-of-speech tags: DET (14442; 12% instances), PRON (6478; 5% instances), ADV (1455; 1% instances), CCONJ (1; 0% instances), X (1; 0% instances).
