Treebank Statistics: UD_Italian-PoSTWITA: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fin, Ger, Inf, Part.

15698 tokens (13%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 4397 types (25%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 1335 lemmas (10%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (11262; 9% instances), AUX (4436; 4% instances).
