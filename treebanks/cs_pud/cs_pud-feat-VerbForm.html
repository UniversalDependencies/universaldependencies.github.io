Treebank Statistics: UD_Czech-PUD: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv, Vnoun are language-specific. It occurs with 5 different values: Conv, Fin, Inf, Part, Vnoun.

3069 tokens (16%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 1853 types (24%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 1214 lemmas (23%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm.
