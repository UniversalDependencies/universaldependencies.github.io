Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish-Atis: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

4023 tokens (9%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 233 types (12%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 63 lemmas (7%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (3591; 8% instances), AUX (432; 1% instances).
