Treebank Statistics: UD_Romanian-Nonstandard: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fin, Ger, Inf, Part.

23217 tokens (17%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 4785 types (42%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 1085 lemmas (23%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (22933; 17% instances), AUX (284; 0% instances).
