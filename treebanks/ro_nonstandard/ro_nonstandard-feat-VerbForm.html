Treebank Statistics: UD_Romanian-Nonstandard: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fin, Ger, Inf, Part.

44996 tokens (14%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 8134 types (40%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 1712 lemmas (21%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (43752; 14% instances), AUX (1244; 0% instances).
