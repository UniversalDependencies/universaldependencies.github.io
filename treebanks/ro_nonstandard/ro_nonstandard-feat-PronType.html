Treebank Statistics: UD_Romanian-Nonstandard: Features: PronType

This feature is universal but the values Emp are language-specific. It occurs with 9 different values: Art, Dem, Emp, Ind, Int, Neg, Prs, Rel, Tot. Some words have combined values of the feature; 1 combinations have been observed: Int|Rel.

35663 tokens (18%) have a non-empty value of PronType. 734 types (5%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. 100 lemmas (2%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. The feature is used with 4 part-of-speech tags: PRON (25891; 13% instances), DET (8367; 4% instances), ADV (1374; 1% instances), NUM (31; 0% instances).
