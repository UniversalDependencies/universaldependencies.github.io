Treebank Statistics: UD_Romanian-Nonstandard: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Imp, Past, Pqp, Pres.

18490 tokens (13%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 2943 types (26%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 786 lemmas (16%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (13841; 10% instances), AUX (4649; 3% instances).

13841 VERB tokens (60% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Tense.
