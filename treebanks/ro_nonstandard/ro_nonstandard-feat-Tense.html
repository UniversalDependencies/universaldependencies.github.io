Treebank Statistics: UD_Romanian-Nonstandard: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Imp, Past, Pqp, Pres.

41150 tokens (13%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 5094 types (25%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 1254 lemmas (16%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (26220; 8% instances), AUX (14930; 5% instances).
