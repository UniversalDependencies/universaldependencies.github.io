Treebank Statistics: UD_Romanian-Nonstandard: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Imp, Past, Pqp, Pres.

2846 tokens (14%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 884 types (24%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 324 lemmas (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (2111; 10% instances), AUX (735; 4% instances).

2111 VERB tokens (61% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Tense.
