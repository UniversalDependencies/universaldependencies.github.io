Treebank Statistics: UD_Romanian-Nonstandard: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Imp, Ind, Sub.

3123 tokens (15%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 1015 types (27%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 348 lemmas (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (2388; 12% instances), AUX (735; 4% instances).
