Treebank Statistics: UD_Romanian-Nonstandard: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Imp, Ind, Sub.

74238 tokens (13%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 8172 types (26%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 1800 lemmas (15%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (44294; 8% instances), AUX (29944; 5% instances).
