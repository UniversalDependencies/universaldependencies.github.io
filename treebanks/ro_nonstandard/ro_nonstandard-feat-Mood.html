Treebank Statistics: UD_Romanian-Nonstandard: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Imp, Ind, Sub.

26850 tokens (14%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 3866 types (28%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 944 lemmas (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (20297; 10% instances), AUX (6553; 3% instances).
