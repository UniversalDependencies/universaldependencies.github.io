Treebank Statistics: UD_Romanian-Nonstandard: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Imp, Ind, Sub.

59245 tokens (13%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 7163 types (27%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 1624 lemmas (16%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (36713; 8% instances), AUX (22532; 5% instances).
