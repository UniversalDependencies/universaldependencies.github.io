Treebank Statistics: UD_Romanian-Nonstandard: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 5 different values: Acc, Dat, Gen, Nom, Voc. Some words have combined values of the feature; 2 combinations have been observed: Acc|Nom, Dat|Gen.

65278 tokens (47%) have a non-empty value of Case. 7698 types (67%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 3600 lemmas (75%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. The feature is used with 8 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (20405; 15% instances), PRON (18586; 13% instances), ADP (14638; 11% instances), DET (4636; 3% instances), PROPN (4523; 3% instances), ADJ (2115; 2% instances), VERB (229; 0% instances), NUM (146; 0% instances).
