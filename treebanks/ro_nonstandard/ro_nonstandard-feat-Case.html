Treebank Statistics: UD_Romanian-Nonstandard: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 5 different values: Acc, Dat, Gen, Nom, Voc. Some words have combined values of the feature; 2 combinations have been observed: Acc|Nom, Dat|Gen.

9515 tokens (46%) have a non-empty value of Case. 2434 types (65%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 1297 lemmas (69%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. The feature is used with 8 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (3317; 16% instances), PRON (2646; 13% instances), ADP (2000; 10% instances), DET (604; 3% instances), PROPN (569; 3% instances), ADJ (324; 2% instances), VERB (39; 0% instances), NUM (16; 0% instances).
