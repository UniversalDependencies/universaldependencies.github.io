Treebank Statistics: UD_English-GUM: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Past, Pres.

24985 tokens (11%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 3412 types (19%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 1819 lemmas (13%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (15932; 7% instances), AUX (9053; 4% instances).
