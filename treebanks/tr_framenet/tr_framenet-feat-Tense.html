Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish-FrameNet: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

3339 tokens (17%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 2139 types (27%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 979 lemmas (25%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (3337; 17% instances), AUX (2; 0% instances).

VERB

3337 VERB tokens (100% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Tense.
