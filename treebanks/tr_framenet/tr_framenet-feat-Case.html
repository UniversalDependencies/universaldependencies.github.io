Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish-FrameNet: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 7 different values: Abl, Acc, Dat, Gen, Ins, Loc, Nom.

7195 tokens (37%) have a non-empty value of Case. 4805 types (61%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 2471 lemmas (63%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (6955; 36% instances), PROPN (240; 1% instances).
