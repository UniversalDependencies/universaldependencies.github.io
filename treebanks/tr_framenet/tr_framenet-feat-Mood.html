This feature is universal but the values CndGen, CndGenPot, Gen, GenNec, GenPot are language-specific. It occurs with 12 different values: Cnd, CndGen, CndGenPot, Des, Gen, GenNec, GenPot, Imp, Ind, Nec, Opt, Pot.
