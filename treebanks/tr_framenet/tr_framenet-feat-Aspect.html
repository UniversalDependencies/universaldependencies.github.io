Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish-FrameNet: Features: Aspect

This feature is universal but the values Hab are language-specific. It occurs with 3 different values: Hab, Perf, Prog.

3022 tokens (16%) have a non-empty value of Aspect. 1895 types (24%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. 912 lemmas (23%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (3021; 16% instances), AUX (1; 0% instances).
