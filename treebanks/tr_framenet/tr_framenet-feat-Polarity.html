Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish-FrameNet: Features: Polarity

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Neg, Pos.

3241 tokens (17%) have a non-empty value of Polarity. 2065 types (26%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Polarity. 915 lemmas (23%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Polarity. The feature is used with 1 part-of-speech tags: VERB (3241; 17% instances).

VERB

3241 VERB tokens (97% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Polarity.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Polarity co-occurred: VerbForm=Fin (3237; 100%), Number=Sing (2796; 86%), Mood=Ind (2666; 82%), Aspect=Perf (2420; 75%), Person=3 (2340; 72%), Tense=Past (2105; 65%).

VERB tokens may have the following values of Polarity:

  • Neg (234; 7% of non-empty Polarity): alamadı, demiyor, kalmadı, gelmez, alamıyordu, açamıyorum, başaramadı, dinlemedi, dinlemiyor, edememiş
  • Pos (3007; 93% of non-empty Polarity): etti, verdi, aldı, oldu, yaptı, bıraktı, açtı, düştü, ettim, gitti
  • EMPTY (96): yoktu, iyiydi, güzeldi, vardı, zengindi, hazırdı, karısıyım, kötüydü, uğraştı, varsa
