Treebank Statistics: UD_Czech-CLTT: Features: Degree

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Cmp, Pos, Sup.

6378 tokens (17%) have a non-empty value of Degree. 1498 types (32%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Degree. 664 lemmas (24%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Degree. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: ADJ (6075; 17% instances), ADV (285; 1% instances), DET (18; 0% instances).
