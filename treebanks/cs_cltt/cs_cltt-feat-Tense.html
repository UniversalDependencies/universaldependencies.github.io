Treebank Statistics: UD_Czech-CLTT: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

2341 tokens (7%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 501 types (11%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 291 lemmas (11%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (1507; 4% instances), AUX (547; 2% instances), ADJ (287; 1% instances).
