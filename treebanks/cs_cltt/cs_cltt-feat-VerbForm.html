Treebank Statistics: UD_Czech-CLTT: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Conv, Fin, Inf, Part.

3021 tokens (8%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 760 types (16%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 423 lemmas (15%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (1857; 5% instances), AUX (616; 2% instances), ADJ (548; 1% instances).
