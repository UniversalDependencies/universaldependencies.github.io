Treebank Statistics: UD_Greek-Messinian: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Acc, Gen, Nom, Voc.

342 tokens (38%) have a non-empty value of Case. 196 types (52%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 134 lemmas (50%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. The feature is used with 8 part-of-speech tags: DET (109; 12% instances), NOUN (95; 11% instances), PRON (74; 8% instances), ADJ (40; 4% instances), PROPN (17; 2% instances), NUM (5; 1% instances), ADP (1; 0% instances), SCONJ (1; 0% instances).
