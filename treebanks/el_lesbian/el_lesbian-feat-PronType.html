Treebank Statistics: UD_Greek-Lesbian: Features: PronType

This feature is universal. It occurs with 8 different values: Art, Dem, Ind, Int, Neg, Prs, Rel, Tot.

1673 tokens (24%) have a non-empty value of PronType. 276 types (12%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. 57 lemmas (4%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. The feature is used with 4 part-of-speech tags: DET (1058; 15% instances), PRON (553; 8% instances), ADV (54; 1% instances), SCONJ (8; 0% instances).
