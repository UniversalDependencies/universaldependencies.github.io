Treebank Statistics: UD_Greek-Lesbian: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Conv, Fin, Inf, Part.

1071 tokens (18%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 795 types (40%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 284 lemmas (26%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (973; 16% instances), AUX (98; 2% instances).
