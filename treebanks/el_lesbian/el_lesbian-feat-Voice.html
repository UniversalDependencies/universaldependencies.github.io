Treebank Statistics: UD_Greek-Lesbian: Features: Voice

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Act, Pass.

1070 tokens (18%) have a non-empty value of Voice. 795 types (40%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. 284 lemmas (26%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (973; 16% instances), AUX (97; 2% instances).

VERB

973 VERB tokens (100% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Voice.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Voice co-occurred: VerbForm=Fin (944; 97%), Mood=Ind (897; 92%), Number=Sing (736; 76%), Person=3 (578; 59%), Aspect=Imp (495; 51%).
