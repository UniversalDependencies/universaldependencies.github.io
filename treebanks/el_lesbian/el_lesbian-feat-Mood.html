Treebank Statistics: UD_Greek-Lesbian: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Imp, Ind.

1042 tokens (18%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 767 types (39%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 269 lemmas (24%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (944; 16% instances), AUX (98; 2% instances).
