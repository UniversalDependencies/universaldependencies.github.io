Treebank Statistics: UD_Greek-Lesbian: Features: Aspect

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Imp, Perf.

1026 tokens (17%) have a non-empty value of Aspect. 766 types (39%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. 269 lemmas (24%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (954; 16% instances), AUX (72; 1% instances).
