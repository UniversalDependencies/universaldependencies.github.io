Treebank Statistics: UD_Polish-PUD: Features: PronType

This feature is universal. It occurs with 7 different values: Dem, Ind, Int, Neg, Prs, Rel, Tot.

1266 tokens (7%) have a non-empty value of PronType. 177 types (2%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. 61 lemmas (1%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: PRON (639; 3% instances), DET (499; 3% instances), ADV (128; 1% instances).
