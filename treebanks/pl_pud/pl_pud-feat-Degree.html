Treebank Statistics: UD_Polish-PUD: Features: Degree

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Cmp, Pos, Sup.

2256 tokens (12%) have a non-empty value of Degree. 1603 types (21%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Degree. 1052 lemmas (21%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Degree. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: ADJ (1932; 11% instances), ADV (324; 2% instances).
