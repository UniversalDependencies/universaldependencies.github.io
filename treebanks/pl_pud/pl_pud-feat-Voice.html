Treebank Statistics: UD_Polish-PUD: Features: Voice

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Act, Pass.

2347 tokens (13%) have a non-empty value of Voice. 1582 types (21%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. 869 lemmas (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (1604; 9% instances), ADJ (412; 2% instances), AUX (331; 2% instances).

VERB

1604 VERB tokens (98% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Voice.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Voice co-occurred: VerbForm=Fin (1301; 81%), Mood=Ind (1299; 81%), Animacy=EMPTY (1163; 73%), Person=EMPTY (1070; 67%), Number=Sing (934; 58%), Aspect=Imp (874; 54%), Gender=EMPTY (837; 52%), Tense=Past (821; 51%).

  • Act (1604; 100% of non-empty Voice): może, ma, powiedział, mogą, było, jest, miał, miała, mówi, powiedziała

Voice seems to be lexical feature of VERB. 100% lemmas (705) occur only with one value of Voice.
