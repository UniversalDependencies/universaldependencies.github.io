Treebank Statistics: UD_Russian: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

8099 tokens (8%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 4034 types (13%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 1835 lemmas (10%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (7174; 7% instances), AUX (925; 1% instances).

7174 VERB tokens (85% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Tense.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Tense co-occurred: Variant=EMPTY (6228; 87%), Number=Sing (5275; 74%), Person=EMPTY (5196; 72%), Animacy=EMPTY (5113; 71%), Case=EMPTY (5113; 71%), Mood=Ind (5096; 71%), VerbForm=Fin (5096; 71%), Voice=EMPTY (3685; 51%), Aspect=Imp (3664; 51%).
