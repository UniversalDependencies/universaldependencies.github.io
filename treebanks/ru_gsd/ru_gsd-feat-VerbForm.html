Treebank Statistics: UD_Russian-GSD: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Conv, Fin, Inf, Part.

9297 tokens (9%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 4837 types (16%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 2105 lemmas (11%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (8314; 8% instances), AUX (983; 1% instances).
