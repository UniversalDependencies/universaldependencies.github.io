Treebank Statistics: UD_Russian: Features: Voice

This feature is universal but the values Mid are language-specific. It occurs with 3 different values: Act, Mid, Pass.

3794 tokens (4%) have a non-empty value of Voice. 2459 types (8%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. 1304 lemmas (7%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (3645; 4% instances), AUX (149; 0% instances).

VERB

3645 VERB tokens (43% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Voice.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Voice co-occurred: Person=EMPTY (3000; 82%), Variant=EMPTY (2699; 74%), Number=Sing (2502; 69%), Tense=Past (2444; 67%), Mood=EMPTY (2214; 61%), VerbForm=Part (2061; 57%), Aspect=Perf (2037; 56%).
