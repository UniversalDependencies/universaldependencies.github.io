Treebank Statistics: UD_Cappadocian-TueCL: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Acc, Gen, Nom, Voc.

1772 tokens (43%) have a non-empty value of Case. 515 types (52%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 405 lemmas (57%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case.
