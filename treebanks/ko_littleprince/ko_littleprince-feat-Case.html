Treebank Statistics: UD_Korean-LittlePrince: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Acc, Gen, Nom.

2739 tokens (20%) have a non-empty value of Case. 925 types (21%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 587 lemmas (35%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case.
