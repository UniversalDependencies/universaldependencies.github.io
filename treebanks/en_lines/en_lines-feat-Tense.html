Treebank Statistics: UD_English-LinES: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Past, Pres.

10240 tokens (12%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 1997 types (21%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 1250 lemmas (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (7224; 9% instances), AUX (3016; 4% instances).

VERB

7224 VERB tokens (73% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Tense.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Tense co-occurred: Voice=EMPTY (6163; 85%), VerbForm=Fin (4415; 61%), Mood=Ind (4412; 61%).
