Treebank Statistics: UD_Slovenian-SSJ: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Fut, Pres.

14240 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 2477 types (8%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 1340 lemmas (8%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: AUX (8295; 6% instances), VERB (5945; 4% instances).
