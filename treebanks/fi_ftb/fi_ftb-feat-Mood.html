Treebank Statistics: UD_Finnish-FTB: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 5 different values: Cnd, Imp, Ind, Opt, Pot.

24104 tokens (15%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 6296 types (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 1844 lemmas (9%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (20692; 13% instances), AUX (3412; 2% instances).
