Treebank Statistics: UD_Finnish-FTB: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 5 different values: Cnd, Imp, Ind, Opt, Pot.

27122 tokens (17%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 6357 types (15%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 1844 lemmas (9%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (17159; 11% instances), AUX (9963; 6% instances).
