Treebank Statistics: UD_Finnish-FTB: Features: Voice

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Act, Pass.

37297 tokens (23%) have a non-empty value of Voice. 10743 types (25%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. 2545 lemmas (12%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (26136; 16% instances), AUX (11161; 7% instances).
