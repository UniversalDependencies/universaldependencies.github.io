Treebank Statistics: UD_Finnish-FTB: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Past, Pres.

21587 tokens (14%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 5257 types (12%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 1778 lemmas (8%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (14680; 9% instances), AUX (6907; 4% instances).

VERB

14680 VERB tokens (56% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Tense.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Tense co-occurred: Case=EMPTY (14680; 100%), InfForm=EMPTY (14680; 100%), Mood=Ind (14680; 100%), PartForm=EMPTY (14680; 100%), VerbForm=Fin (14680; 100%), Voice=Act (13142; 90%), Number=Sing (10298; 70%), Person=3 (9267; 63%).

VERB tokens may have the following values of Tense:

  • Past (5974; 41% of non-empty Tense): oli, tuli, sanoi, meni, sai, ollut, teki, jäi, lähti, olivat
  • Pres (8706; 59% of non-empty Tense): on, ole, tulee, ovat, saa, sanoo, onko, tiedä, haluaa, tekee
  • EMPTY (11759): olla, ei, ollut, tehdä, mennä, tulla, olisi, lähteä, ottaa, sanoa
