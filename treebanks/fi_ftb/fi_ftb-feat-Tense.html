Treebank Statistics: UD_Finnish-FTB: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Past, Pres.

21587 tokens (14%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 5257 types (12%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 1777 lemmas (8%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (18357; 12% instances), AUX (3230; 2% instances).

VERB

18357 VERB tokens (54% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Tense.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Tense co-occurred: Case=EMPTY (18357; 100%), InfForm=EMPTY (18357; 100%), Mood=Ind (18357; 100%), PartForm=EMPTY (18357; 100%), VerbForm=Fin (18357; 100%), Voice=Act (16703; 91%), Number=Sing (13068; 71%), Person=3 (11892; 65%).

VERB tokens may have the following values of Tense:

  • Past (6793; 37% of non-empty Tense): oli, tuli, sanoi, olin, olivat, sai, ollut, piti, meni, jäi
  • Pres (11564; 63% of non-empty Tense): on, ole, voi, ovat, tulee, olen, saa, pitää, täytyy, onko
  • EMPTY (15456): ei, en, olla, olisi, eivät, ollut, eikä, pitäisi, tehdä, et
