Treebank Statistics: UD_Finnish-FTB: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Past, Pres.

21587 tokens (14%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 5257 types (12%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 1777 lemmas (8%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (15277; 10% instances), AUX (6310; 4% instances).

VERB

15277 VERB tokens (56% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Tense.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Tense co-occurred: Case=EMPTY (15277; 100%), InfForm=EMPTY (15277; 100%), Mood=Ind (15277; 100%), PartForm=EMPTY (15277; 100%), VerbForm=Fin (15277; 100%), Voice=Act (13709; 90%), Number=Sing (10707; 70%), Person=3 (9658; 63%).

VERB tokens may have the following values of Tense:

  • Past (6166; 40% of non-empty Tense): oli, tuli, sanoi, sai, meni, ollut, jäi, teki, lähti, olivat
  • Pres (9111; 60% of non-empty Tense): on, ole, tulee, saa, ovat, sanoo, onko, tiedä, kuuluu, haluaa
  • EMPTY (11917): olla, ei, ollut, tehdä, mennä, tulla, olisi, saanut, saada, ottaa
