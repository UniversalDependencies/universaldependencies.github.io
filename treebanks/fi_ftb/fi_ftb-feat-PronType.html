Treebank Statistics: UD_Finnish-FTB: Features: PronType

This feature is universal. It occurs with 6 different values: Dem, Ind, Int, Prs, Rcp, Rel.

17304 tokens (11%) have a non-empty value of PronType. 1487 types (3%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. 149 lemmas (1%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. The feature is used with 4 part-of-speech tags: PRON (11085; 7% instances), DET (3684; 2% instances), ADV (2246; 1% instances), ADJ (289; 0% instances).
