Treebank Statistics: UD_Naga-Suansu: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv, Vnoun are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Conv, Fin, Inf, Vnoun.

754 tokens (24%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 448 types (40%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 290 lemmas (36%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 6 part-of-speech tags: VERB (634; 20% instances), AUX (72; 2% instances), NOUN (31; 1% instances), ADV (9; 0% instances), ADP (6; 0% instances), PART (2; 0% instances).
