Treebank Statistics: UD_French-ParTUT: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Imp, Past, Pres.

2808 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 1060 types (26%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 468 lemmas (16%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (2045; 7% instances), AUX (763; 3% instances).

VERB

2045 VERB tokens (75% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Tense.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Tense co-occurred: Gender=EMPTY (1303; 64%), Number=Sing (1246; 61%), VerbForm=Fin (1146; 56%).

VERB tokens may have the following values of Tense:

  • Fut (69; 3% of non-empty Tense): devra, pourront, aura, auront, dirai, ferai, pourra, soutiendra, acceptera, adhéreront
  • Imp (37; 2% of non-empty Tense): avait, provenaient, recommandait, arrivait, attendait, avaient, constituaient, contenaient, contenait, couvrait
  • Past (764; 37% of non-empty Tense): dite, fait, tenu, compris, donné, mis, dit, soumis, nommés, agit
  • Pres (1175; 57% of non-empty Tense): a, peut, voudrais, doit, est, devrait, concernant, convient, ont, peuvent
  • EMPTY (691): faire, améliorer, dire, abonner, assurer, savoir, avoir, compter, utiliser, communiquer
