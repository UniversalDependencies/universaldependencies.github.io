Treebank Statistics: UD_French-ParTUT: Features: PronType

This feature is universal but the values Exc are language-specific. It occurs with 9 different values: Art, Dem, Exc, Ind, Int, Neg, Prs, Rel, Tot.

6424 tokens (22%) have a non-empty value of PronType. 163 types (4%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. 73 lemmas (2%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. The feature is used with 5 part-of-speech tags: DET (4784; 17% instances), PRON (1621; 6% instances), ADV (17; 0% instances), ADJ (1; 0% instances), ADP (1; 0% instances).
