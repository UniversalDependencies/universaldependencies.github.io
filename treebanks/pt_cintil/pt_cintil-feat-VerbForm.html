Treebank Statistics: UD_Portuguese-CINTIL: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fin, Ger, Inf, Part.

56351 tokens (12%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 10237 types (30%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 3297 lemmas (13%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (42620; 9% instances), AUX (13731; 3% instances).
