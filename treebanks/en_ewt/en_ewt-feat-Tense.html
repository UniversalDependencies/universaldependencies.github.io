Treebank Statistics: UD_English-EWT: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Past, Pres.

25079 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 2817 types (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 1509 lemmas (10%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense.
