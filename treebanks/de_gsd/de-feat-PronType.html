Treebank Statistics: UD_German: Features: PronType

This feature is universal. It occurs with 8 different values: Art, Dem, Ind, Int, Neg, Prs, Rel, Tot. Some words have combined values of the feature; 2 combinations have been observed: Dem|Rel, Int|Rel.

50970 tokens (17%) have a non-empty value of PronType. 308 types (1%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. 104 lemmas (0%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: DET (36233; 12% instances), PRON (14693; 5% instances), ADV (44; 0% instances).
