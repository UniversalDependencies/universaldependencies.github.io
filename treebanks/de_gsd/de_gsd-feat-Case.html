Treebank Statistics: UD_German-GSD: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Acc, Dat, Gen, Nom.

144622 tokens (49%) have a non-empty value of Case. 41421 types (82%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 35703 lemmas (84%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. The feature is used with 11 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (51339; 18% instances), DET (37323; 13% instances), PROPN (27308; 9% instances), ADJ (14272; 5% instances), PRON (13976; 5% instances), NUM (210; 0% instances), X (103; 0% instances), ADV (68; 0% instances), PART (9; 0% instances), SYM (9; 0% instances), ADP (5; 0% instances).
