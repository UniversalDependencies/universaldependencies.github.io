Treebank Statistics: UD_German-GSD: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Acc, Dat, Gen, Nom.

145840 tokens (50%) have a non-empty value of Case. 41851 types (83%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 36130 lemmas (85%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case.
