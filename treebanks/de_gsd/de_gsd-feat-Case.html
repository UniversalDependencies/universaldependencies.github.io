Treebank Statistics: UD_German-GSD: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Acc, Dat, Gen, Nom.

145840 tokens (50%) have a non-empty value of Case. 41851 types (83%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 36130 lemmas (85%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. The feature is used with 15 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (51387; 18% instances), DET (36392; 12% instances), PROPN (28664; 10% instances), ADJ (14566; 5% instances), PRON (14197; 5% instances), NUM (213; 0% instances), X (124; 0% instances), VERB (122; 0% instances), ADV (69; 0% instances), ADP (37; 0% instances), CCONJ (26; 0% instances), SCONJ (19; 0% instances), PART (12; 0% instances), AUX (11; 0% instances), PUNCT (1; 0% instances).
