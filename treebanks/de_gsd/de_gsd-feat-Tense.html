Treebank Statistics: UD_German-GSD: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Past, Pres.

22392 tokens (8%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 2885 types (6%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 1499 lemmas (4%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense.
