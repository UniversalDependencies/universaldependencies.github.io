Treebank Statistics: UD_French-PUD: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Imp, Past, Pres.

2819 tokens (11%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 1150 types (19%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 568 lemmas (12%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (1834; 7% instances), AUX (981; 4% instances), ADJ (4; 0% instances).

VERB

1834 VERB tokens (81% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Tense.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Tense co-occurred: Number=Sing (1337; 73%), Gender=EMPTY (996; 54%), Mood=EMPTY (934; 51%), Person=EMPTY (934; 51%), VerbForm=Part (934; 51%).

  • Imp (164; 9% of non-empty Tense): avait, était, étaient, pouvait, comprenait, pensait, avaient, considérait, faisait, allait
  • Past (928; 51% of non-empty Tense): eu, déclaré, dit, fait, commencé, indiqué, décidé, joué, reçu, utilisé
  • Pres (714; 39% of non-empty Tense): a, est, peut, pourrait, peuvent, sont, ont, pourraient, va, devrait
  • EMPTY (422): faire, avoir, voir, devenir, partir, produire, aider, créer, donner, envoyer
