Treebank Statistics: UD_Breton-KEB: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fin, Inf, Part.

1755 tokens (17%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 660 types (27%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 277 lemmas (16%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm.
