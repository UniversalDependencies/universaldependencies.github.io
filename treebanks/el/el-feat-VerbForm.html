Treebank Statistics: UD_Greek: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Conv, Fin, Inf, Part.

6935 tokens (11%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 2995 types (26%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 956 lemmas (15%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (6385; 10% instances), AUX (550; 1% instances).

VERB

6385 VERB tokens (100% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of VerbForm.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and VerbForm co-occurred: Mood=Ind (5895; 92%), Person=3 (4766; 75%), Voice=Act (4534; 71%), Number=Sing (3722; 58%), Aspect=Imp (3410; 53%).

  • Fin (5915; 93% of non-empty VerbForm): πρέπει, έχει, έχουν, μπορεί, είχε, αφορά, αποτελεί, δήλωσε, είχαν, έχουμε

  • Part (125; 2% of non-empty VerbForm): χτισμένη, περιορισμένη, χτισμένο, συμπεριλαμβανομένης, συμπεριλαμβανομένων, αποτυχημένη, αυξημένη, δομημένο, εκλεγμένος, ευνοημένων
