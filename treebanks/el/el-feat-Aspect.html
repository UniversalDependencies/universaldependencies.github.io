Treebank Statistics: UD_Greek: Features: Aspect

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Imp, Perf.

6935 tokens (11%) have a non-empty value of Aspect. 2995 types (26%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. 956 lemmas (15%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (6385; 10% instances), AUX (550; 1% instances).

VERB

6385 VERB tokens (100% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Aspect.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Aspect co-occurred: VerbForm=Fin (5915; 93%), Mood=Ind (5895; 92%), Person=3 (4766; 75%), Voice=Act (4534; 71%), Number=Sing (3722; 58%).
