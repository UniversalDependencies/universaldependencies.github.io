Treebank Statistics: UD_Old_East_Slavic-Ruthenian: Features: PronType

This feature is universal but the values Emp, Exc are language-specific. It occurs with 9 different values: Dem, Emp, Exc, Ind, Int, Neg, Prs, Rel, Tot.

15903 tokens (14%) have a non-empty value of PronType. 1151 types (6%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. 139 lemmas (2%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: DET (8096; 7% instances), PRON (7662; 7% instances), ADV (145; 0% instances).
