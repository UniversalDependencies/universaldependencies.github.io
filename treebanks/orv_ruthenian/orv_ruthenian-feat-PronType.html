Treebank Statistics: UD_Old_East_Slavic-Ruthenian: Features: PronType

This feature is universal but the values Emp, Exc are language-specific. It occurs with 9 different values: Dem, Emp, Exc, Ind, Int, Neg, Prs, Rel, Tot.

14401 tokens (15%) have a non-empty value of PronType. 847 types (5%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. 63 lemmas (1%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: DET (7376; 8% instances), PRON (7025; 7% instances).
