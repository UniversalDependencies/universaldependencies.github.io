Treebank Statistics: UD_Old_East_Slavic-Ruthenian: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 7 different values: Acc, Dat, Gen, Ins, Loc, Nom, Voc.

4475 tokens (45%) have a non-empty value of Case. 2262 types (72%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 965 lemmas (66%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case.
