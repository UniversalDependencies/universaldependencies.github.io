Treebank Statistics: UD_Old_East_Slavic-Ruthenian: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv, PartRes are language-specific. It occurs with 5 different values: Conv, Fin, Inf, Part, PartRes.

13456 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 5328 types (21%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 1739 lemmas (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (11702; 9% instances), AUX (1754; 1% instances).
