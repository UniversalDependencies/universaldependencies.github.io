Treebank Statistics: UD_Old_East_Slavic-Ruthenian: Features: Voice

This feature is universal but the values Mid are language-specific. It occurs with 3 different values: Act, Mid, Pass.

13456 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Voice. 5328 types (21%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. 1739 lemmas (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (11702; 9% instances), AUX (1754; 1% instances).

VERB

11702 VERB tokens (99% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Voice.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Voice co-occurred: Mood=EMPTY (9159; 78%), Person=EMPTY (9101; 78%), Gender=EMPTY (7964; 68%), Tense=Past (6415; 55%).

VERB tokens may have the following values of Voice:

  • Act (10186; 87% of non-empty Voice): дали, казали, мають, мают(ь), дал, маеть, держати, послали, записа(т), далъ
  • Mid (558; 5% of non-empty Voice): приточиласѧ, приточилася, въступатисѧ, приточиласе, вступатисѧ, въстꙋпатисѧ, билисѧ, виделосѧ, встꙋпатисѧ, жаловалсѧ
  • Pass (958; 8% of non-empty Voice): псан, записано, писан, дано, выписано, писаныи, писаные, писанъ, псанъ, дан
  • EMPTY (82): надобе, надобѣ, нетъ, нѣтъ, потреба, нет, нѣт, ре, -въши, встыдъ
