Treebank Statistics: UD_Old_East_Slavic-Ruthenian: Features: Voice

This feature is universal but the values Mid are language-specific. It occurs with 3 different values: Act, Mid, Pass.

9407 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Voice. 3249 types (20%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. 1027 lemmas (19%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (8080; 8% instances), AUX (1327; 1% instances).

VERB

8080 VERB tokens (99% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Voice.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Voice co-occurred: Analyt=EMPTY (7012; 87%), Person=EMPTY (6090; 75%), Mood=EMPTY (6072; 75%), Gender=EMPTY (5868; 73%).

VERB tokens may have the following values of Voice:

  • EMPTY (68): надобе, надобѣ, нетъ, нѣтъ, нет, нѣт, встыдъ, жаль, некомꙋ, нелга
