Treebank Statistics: UD_Marathi: Features: Case

This feature is universal but the values Obl are language-specific. It occurs with 10 different values: Abl, Acc, Com, Dat, Erg, Ins, Loc, Nom, Obl, Voc.

1426 tokens (37%) have a non-empty value of Case. 477 types (50%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 454 lemmas (65%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. The feature is used with 8 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (829; 22% instances), PRON (288; 7% instances), ADJ (120; 3% instances), ADP (54; 1% instances), PROPN (54; 1% instances), DET (42; 1% instances), VERB (38; 1% instances), AUX (1; 0% instances).
