Treebank Statistics: UD_Basque: Features: Aspect

This feature is universal but the values Prosp are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Imp, Perf, Prog, Prosp.

13932 tokens (11%) have a non-empty value of Aspect. 1810 types (7%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. 653 lemmas (6%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (11652; 10% instances), AUX (2280; 2% instances).
