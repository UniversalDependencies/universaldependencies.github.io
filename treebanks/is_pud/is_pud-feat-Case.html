Treebank Statistics: UD_Icelandic-PUD: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Acc, Dat, Gen, Nom.

7638 tokens (41%) have a non-empty value of Case. 4698 types (72%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 3359 lemmas (70%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. The feature is used with 11 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (4079; 22% instances), PRON (1365; 7% instances), ADJ (1150; 6% instances), PROPN (648; 3% instances), VERB (242; 1% instances), NUM (118; 1% instances), DET (22; 0% instances), ADV (10; 0% instances), SCONJ (2; 0% instances), ADP (1; 0% instances), AUX (1; 0% instances).
