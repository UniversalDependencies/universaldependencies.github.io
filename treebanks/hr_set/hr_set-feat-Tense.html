Treebank Statistics: UD_Croatian-SET: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Imp, Past, Pres.

26282 tokens (13%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 4757 types (13%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 1911 lemmas (10%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (13785; 7% instances), AUX (12025; 6% instances), ADV (472; 0% instances).

VERB

13785 VERB tokens (79% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Tense.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Tense co-occurred: Number=Sing (9213; 67%), Gender=EMPTY (7695; 56%), Mood=Ind (7695; 56%), VerbForm=Fin (7695; 56%), Voice=EMPTY (7695; 56%).

VERB tokens may have the following values of Tense:

  • Past (6097; 44% of non-empty Tense): izjavio, rekao, kazao, mogli, mogla, dodao, mogao, moglo, trebala, dobili
  • Pres (7688; 56% of non-empty Tense): može, ima, kaže, treba, mogu, nema, mora, postoji, možete, imaju
  • EMPTY (3602): imati, pomoći, raditi, pronaći, vidjeti, reći, nastaviti, dobiti, doći, morati
