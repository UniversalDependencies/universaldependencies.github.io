Treebank Statistics: UD_Italian-TWITTIRO: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fin, Ger, Inf, Part.

3933 tokens (13%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 1743 types (28%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 764 lemmas (16%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (2844; 10% instances), AUX (1089; 4% instances).

VERB

2844 VERB tokens (100% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of VerbForm.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and VerbForm co-occurred: Gender=EMPTY (2340; 82%), Number=Sing (1738; 61%), Tense=Pres (1493; 52%), Mood=Ind (1435; 50%).

VERB tokens may have the following values of VerbForm:

  • Fin (1715; 60% of non-empty VerbForm): continua, è, fa, ha, dice, va, parla, sembra, fanno, ho
  • Ger (50; 2% of non-empty VerbForm): facendo, guardando, aspettando, attrezzando, sapendo, Dimostrando, Minacciando, Portando, Producendo, Trasformando
  • Inf (574; 20% of non-empty VerbForm): fare, dire, far, avere, andare, parlare, dare, lavorare, prendere, cambiare
  • Part (505; 18% of non-empty VerbForm): fatto, detto, morto, messo, avuto, dato, letto, arrivato, capito, lasciato
  • EMPTY (2): Clicca, messa
