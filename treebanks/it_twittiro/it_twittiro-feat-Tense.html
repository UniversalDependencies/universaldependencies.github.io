Treebank Statistics: UD_Italian-TWITTIRO: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Imp, Past, Pres.

3253 tokens (11%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 1392 types (23%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 651 lemmas (13%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (2215; 7% instances), AUX (1038; 4% instances).

VERB

2215 VERB tokens (78% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Tense.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Tense co-occurred: Number=Sing (1737; 78%), Gender=EMPTY (1711; 77%), VerbForm=Fin (1709; 77%), Mood=Ind (1433; 65%), Person=3 (1214; 55%).
