Treebank Statistics: UD_Italian-TWITTIRO: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Cnd, Imp, Ind, Sub.

2736 tokens (9%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 1088 types (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 517 lemmas (11%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (1716; 6% instances), AUX (1020; 3% instances).
