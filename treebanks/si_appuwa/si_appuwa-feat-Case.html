Treebank Statistics: UD_Sinhala-Appuwa: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 7 different values: Abl, Acc, Dat, Gen, Ins, Loc, Nom.

248 tokens (37%) have a non-empty value of Case. 199 types (49%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 169 lemmas (46%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (218; 33% instances), PROPN (19; 3% instances), PRON (11; 2% instances).
