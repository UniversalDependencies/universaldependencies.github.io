Treebank Statistics: UD_Italian-Old: Features: Voice

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Act, Pass.

8888 tokens (11%) have a non-empty value of Voice. 3660 types (38%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. 1151 lemmas (22%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (8405; 10% instances), AUX (483; 1% instances).

VERB

8405 VERB tokens (73% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Voice.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Voice co-occurred: Gender=EMPTY (6906; 82%), Number=Sing (6034; 72%), VerbForm=Fin (5447; 65%), Mood=Ind (4618; 55%), Aspect=EMPTY (4602; 55%).

VERB tokens may have the following values of Voice:

  • Act (7008; 83% of non-empty Voice): disse, vidi, fa, vedi, ha, fece, veder, rispuose, dir, far
  • Pass (1397; 17% of non-empty Voice): fatto, fatta, morta, morti, rotto, detto, messo, volta, vòlto, aperta
  • EMPTY (3182): va, par, parea, convien, andar, è, vien, parlar, venir, venne
