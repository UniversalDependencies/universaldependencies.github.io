Treebank Statistics: UD_Italian-Old: Features: Voice

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Act, Pass.

13043 tokens (11%) have a non-empty value of Voice. 4790 types (39%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. 1415 lemmas (22%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (12332; 10% instances), AUX (711; 1% instances).

VERB

12332 VERB tokens (73% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Voice.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Voice co-occurred: Gender=EMPTY (10247; 83%), Number=Sing (8954; 73%), VerbForm=Fin (8013; 65%), Aspect=EMPTY (6964; 56%), Mood=Ind (6861; 56%).

VERB tokens may have the following values of Voice:

  • EMPTY (4577): va, convien, par, parea, vien, parlar, venne, parve, è, andar
