Treebank Statistics: UD_Italian-Old: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv are language-specific. It occurs with 5 different values: Conv, Fin, Ger, Inf, Part.

20119 tokens (16%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 6240 types (50%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 1737 lemmas (27%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 4 part-of-speech tags: VERB (16748; 14% instances), AUX (3369; 3% instances), ADJ (1; 0% instances), NOUN (1; 0% instances).
