Treebank Statistics: UD_Italian-Old: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv are language-specific. It occurs with 5 different values: Conv, Fin, Ger, Inf, Part.

13791 tokens (17%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 4790 types (49%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 1391 lemmas (27%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (11502; 14% instances), AUX (2288; 3% instances), ADJ (1; 0% instances).
