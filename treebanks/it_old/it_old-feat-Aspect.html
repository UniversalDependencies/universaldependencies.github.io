Treebank Statistics: UD_Italian-Old: Features: Aspect

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Imp, Perf.

8222 tokens (7%) have a non-empty value of Aspect. 2722 types (22%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. 912 lemmas (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (6864; 6% instances), AUX (1358; 1% instances).
