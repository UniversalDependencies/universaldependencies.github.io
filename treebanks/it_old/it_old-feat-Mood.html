Treebank Statistics: UD_Italian-Old: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Cnd, Imp, Ind, Sub.

5160 tokens (12%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 2098 types (33%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 790 lemmas (22%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (3998; 10% instances), AUX (1162; 3% instances).
