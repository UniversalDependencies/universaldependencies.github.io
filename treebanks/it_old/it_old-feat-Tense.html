Treebank Statistics: UD_Italian-Old: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Imp, Past, Pres.

18269 tokens (15%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 5506 types (44%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 1595 lemmas (25%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense.
