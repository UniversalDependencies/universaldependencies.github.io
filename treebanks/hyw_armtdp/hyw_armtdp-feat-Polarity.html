Treebank Statistics: UD_Western_Armenian-ArmTDP: Features: Polarity

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Neg, Pos.

6032 tokens (17%) have a non-empty value of Polarity. 2659 types (27%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Polarity. 1066 lemmas (20%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Polarity. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (4418; 12% instances), AUX (1527; 4% instances), PART (87; 0% instances).
