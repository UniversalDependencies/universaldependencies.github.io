Treebank Statistics: UD_Western_Armenian-ArmTDP: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Conv, Fin, Inf, Part.

20043 tokens (16%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 6914 types (28%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 1954 lemmas (16%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (15489; 13% instances), AUX (4554; 4% instances).
