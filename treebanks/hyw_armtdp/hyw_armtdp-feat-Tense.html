Treebank Statistics: UD_Western_Armenian-ArmTDP: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Imp, Past, Pres.

9554 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 2984 types (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 1125 lemmas (11%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (6226; 7% instances), AUX (3328; 4% instances).
