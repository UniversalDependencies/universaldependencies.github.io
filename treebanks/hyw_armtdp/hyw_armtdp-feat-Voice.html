Treebank Statistics: UD_Western_Armenian-ArmTDP: Features: Voice

This feature is universal but the values Mid are language-specific. It occurs with 5 different values: Act, Cau, Mid, Pass, Rcp.

4805 tokens (13%) have a non-empty value of Voice. 2827 types (29%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. 1089 lemmas (20%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (4789; 13% instances), AUX (16; 0% instances).

VERB

4789 VERB tokens (100% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Voice.
