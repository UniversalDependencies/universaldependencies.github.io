Treebank Statistics: UD_Western_Armenian-ArmTDP: Features: PronType

This feature is universal but the values Emp, Exc are language-specific. It occurs with 11 different values: Art, Dem, Emp, Exc, Ind, Int, Neg, Prs, Rcp, Rel, Tot.
