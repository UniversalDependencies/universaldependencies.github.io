Treebank Statistics: UD_Western_Armenian-ArmTDP: Features: Aspect

This feature is universal but the values Dur, Iter, Prosp are language-specific. It occurs with 6 different values: Dur, Imp, Iter, Perf, Prog, Prosp. Some words have combined values of the feature; 1 combinations have been observed: Dur|Iter.

6588 tokens (18%) have a non-empty value of Aspect. 2266 types (23%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. 956 lemmas (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (3864; 11% instances), AUX (2724; 8% instances).
