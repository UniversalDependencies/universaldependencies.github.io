Treebank Statistics: UD_Western_Armenian-ArmTDP: Features: Aspect

This feature is universal but the values Dur, Iter, Prosp are language-specific. It occurs with 6 different values: Dur, Imp, Iter, Perf, Prog, Prosp. Some words have combined values of the feature; 3 combinations have been observed: Dur|Iter, Iter|Perf, Iter|Prosp.
