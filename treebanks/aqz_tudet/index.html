Description

UD_Akunstu-TuDeT is a collection of annotated sentences in Akuntsú. The project is work in progress and the treebank is being updated on a regular basis.

</br> </br> </br> </br> </br>

UD_Akuntsu-TuDeT is a collection of annotated texts in Akuntsú. It is part of TuLaR, Tupían Language Resources, which contains other languages – see TuDeT. The sentences are being annotated by Carolina Aragon and Fabrício Ferraz Gerardi. Besides the works mentioned in the References, all of the annotated sentences stem from Aragon’s field work.

Acknowledgments

The development of the treebank is supported by the European Research Council (ERC) under the European Union’s Horizon 2020 research and innovation programme (Grant agreement No. 834050).
