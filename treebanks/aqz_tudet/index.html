Description

UD_Akuntsu-TuDeT is a collection of annotated texts in Akuntsú. Together with UD_Tupinamba-TuDeT and UD_Munduruku-TuDeT, UD_Akuntsu-TuDeT is part of the TuLaR project.

UD_Akuntsu-TuDeT is a collection of annotated texts in Akuntsú. Together with UD_Tupinamba-TuDeT and UD_Munduruku-TuDeT, UD_Akuntsu-TuDeT is part of the TuLaR project. The sentences are being annotated by Carolina Aragon and Fabrício Ferraz Gerardi.
