Treebank Statistics: UD_Sicilian-STB: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Imp, Past, Pres.

1562 tokens (14%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 785 types (35%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 335 lemmas (22%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (1191; 11% instances), AUX (371; 3% instances).
