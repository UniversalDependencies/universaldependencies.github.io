Treebank Statistics: UD_Sicilian-STB: Features: PronType

This feature is universal but the values Exc are language-specific. It occurs with 9 different values: Art, Dem, Exc, Ind, Int, Neg, Prs, Rel, Tot.

2656 tokens (24%) have a non-empty value of PronType. 207 types (9%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. 79 lemmas (5%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. The feature is used with 4 part-of-speech tags: DET (1293; 12% instances), PRON (1198; 11% instances), ADV (157; 1% instances), SCONJ (8; 0% instances).
