Treebank Statistics: UD_Sicilian-STB: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Cnd, Imp, Ind, Sub.

1440 tokens (13%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 699 types (31%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 305 lemmas (20%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (1077; 10% instances), AUX (363; 3% instances).
