Treebank Statistics: UD_Pashto-Sikaram: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Past, Pres.

315 tokens (8%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 92 types (9%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 50 lemmas (6%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (192; 5% instances), AUX (123; 3% instances).
