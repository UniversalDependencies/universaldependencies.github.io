Treebank Statistics: UD_Pashto-Sikaram: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

105 tokens (11%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 70 types (16%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 38 lemmas (10%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (76; 8% instances), AUX (29; 3% instances).

VERB

76 VERB tokens (84% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Tense.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Tense co-occurred: Case=EMPTY (61; 80%), VerbForm=Fin (61; 80%), Mood=Ind (59; 78%), Person=3 (54; 71%), Number=Sing (50; 66%).
