Treebank Statistics: UD_Pashto-Sikaram: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Past, Pres.

196 tokens (8%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 82 types (10%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 48 lemmas (8%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (127; 5% instances), AUX (69; 3% instances).
