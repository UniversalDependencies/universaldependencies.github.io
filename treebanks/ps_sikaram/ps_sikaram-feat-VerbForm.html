Treebank Statistics: UD_Pashto-Sikaram: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Vnoun are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Fin, Inf, Part, Vnoun.

329 tokens (13%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 151 types (19%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 81 lemmas (13%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 4 part-of-speech tags: VERB (205; 8% instances), AUX (108; 4% instances), NOUN (11; 0% instances), ADJ (5; 0% instances).
