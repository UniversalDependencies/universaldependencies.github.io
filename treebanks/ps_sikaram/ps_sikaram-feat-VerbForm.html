Treebank Statistics: UD_Pashto-Sikaram: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Vnoun are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Fin, Inf, Part, Vnoun.

536 tokens (13%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 181 types (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 96 lemmas (11%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 4 part-of-speech tags: VERB (327; 8% instances), AUX (184; 5% instances), NOUN (17; 0% instances), ADJ (8; 0% instances).
