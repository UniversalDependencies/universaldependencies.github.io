Treebank Statistics: UD_Ukrainian: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

8994 tokens (9%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 4973 types (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 2746 lemmas (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 4 part-of-speech tags: VERB (8231; 8% instances), AUX (722; 1% instances), ADJ (33; 0% instances), NOUN (8; 0% instances).
