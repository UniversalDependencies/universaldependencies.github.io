Treebank Statistics: UD_Ukrainian-IU: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Conv, Fin, Inf, Part.

15171 tokens (12%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 8470 types (27%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 4358 lemmas (24%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (12901; 11% instances), ADJ (1320; 1% instances), AUX (950; 1% instances).
