Treebank Statistics: UD_Ukrainian-IU: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Conv, Fin, Inf, Part.

15100 tokens (12%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 8436 types (27%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 4345 lemmas (24%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm.
