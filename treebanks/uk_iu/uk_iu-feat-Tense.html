Treebank Statistics: UD_Ukrainian-IU: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

10964 tokens (9%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 5741 types (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 3106 lemmas (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 4 part-of-speech tags: VERB (10056; 8% instances), AUX (862; 1% instances), ADJ (38; 0% instances), NOUN (8; 0% instances).
