Treebank Statistics: UD_Swedish-LinES: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Past, Pres.

10954 tokens (12%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 2076 types (15%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 1515 lemmas (15%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (6780; 7% instances), AUX (3541; 4% instances), ADJ (633; 1% instances).
