Treebank Statistics: UD_Old_East_Slavic-RNC: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv, PartRes are language-specific. It occurs with 5 different values: Conv, Fin, Inf, Part, PartRes.

4384 tokens (9%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 2338 types (20%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 1105 lemmas (19%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (4117; 8% instances), AUX (267; 1% instances).
