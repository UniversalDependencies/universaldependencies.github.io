Treebank Statistics: UD_Old_East_Slavic-RNC: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv, PartRes are language-specific. It occurs with 6 different values: Conv, Fin, Inf, Part, PartRes, Sup.

15895 tokens (9%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 6936 types (22%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 2280 lemmas (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (14677; 9% instances), AUX (1218; 1% instances).
