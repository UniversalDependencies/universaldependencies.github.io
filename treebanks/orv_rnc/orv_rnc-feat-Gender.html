This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fem, Masc, Neut.

83109 tokens (49%) have a non-empty value of Gender. 25346 types (81%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Gender. 10219 lemmas (82%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Gender.
