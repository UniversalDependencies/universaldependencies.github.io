Treebank Statistics: UD_Old_East_Slavic-RNC: Features: Voice

This feature is universal but the values Mid are language-specific. It occurs with 3 different values: Act, Mid, Pass.

15895 tokens (9%) have a non-empty value of Voice. 6936 types (22%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. 2280 lemmas (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (14677; 9% instances), AUX (1218; 1% instances).

14677 VERB tokens (99% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Voice.
