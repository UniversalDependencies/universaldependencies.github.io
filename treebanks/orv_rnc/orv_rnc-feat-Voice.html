Treebank Statistics: UD_Old_East_Slavic-RNC: Features: Voice

This feature is universal but the values Mid are language-specific. It occurs with 3 different values: Act, Mid, Pass.

8609 tokens (9%) have a non-empty value of Voice. 4223 types (21%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. 1672 lemmas (19%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (7962; 8% instances), AUX (647; 1% instances).

7962 VERB tokens (99% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Voice.
