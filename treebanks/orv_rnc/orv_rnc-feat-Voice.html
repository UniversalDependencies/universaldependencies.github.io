Treebank Statistics: UD_Old_East_Slavic-RNC: Features: Voice

This feature is universal but the values Mid are language-specific. It occurs with 3 different values: Act, Mid, Pass.

3415 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Voice. 2112 types (22%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. 1024 lemmas (21%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (3247; 9% instances), AUX (168; 0% instances).
