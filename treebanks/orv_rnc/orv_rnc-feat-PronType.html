Treebank Statistics: UD_Old_East_Slavic-RNC: Features: PronType

This feature is universal but the values Emp, Exc are language-specific. It occurs with 9 different values: Dem, Emp, Exc, Ind, Int, Neg, Prs, Rel, Tot.

3733 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of PronType. 586 types (6%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. 87 lemmas (2%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. The feature is used with 4 part-of-speech tags: DET (1892; 5% instances), PRON (1839; 5% instances), ADV (1; 0% instances), SCONJ (1; 0% instances).
