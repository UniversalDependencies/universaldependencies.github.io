Treebank Statistics: UD_Old_East_Slavic-RNC: Features: PronType

This feature is universal but the values Emp, Exc are language-specific. It occurs with 10 different values: Dem, Emp, Exc, Ind, Int, Neg, Prs, Rcp, Rel, Tot.

9514 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of PronType. 1025 types (5%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. 173 lemmas (2%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: DET (4523; 5% instances), PRON (4286; 4% instances), ADV (705; 1% instances).
