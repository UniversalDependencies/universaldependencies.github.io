Treebank Statistics: UD_Sanskrit: Features: PronType

This feature is universal. It occurs with 6 different values: Dem, Ind, Int, Prs, Rel, Tot.

315 tokens (18%) have a non-empty value of PronType. 112 types (11%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. 37 lemmas (5%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType.
