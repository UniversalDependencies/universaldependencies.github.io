Treebank Statistics: UD_Sanskrit: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Imp, Past, Pres.

244 tokens (14%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 195 types (20%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 139 lemmas (19%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense.
