Treebank Statistics: UD_French-GSD: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fin, Inf, Part.

44824 tokens (11%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 8267 types (19%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 2196 lemmas (7%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (31743; 8% instances), AUX (13081; 3% instances).

VERB

31743 VERB tokens (100% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of VerbForm.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and VerbForm co-occurred: Voice=EMPTY (20575; 65%), Gender=EMPTY (20574; 65%), Number=Sing (19517; 61%), Mood=EMPTY (18188; 57%), Person=EMPTY (18188; 57%).

VERB tokens may have the following values of VerbForm:

  • Fin (13555; 43% of non-empty VerbForm): a, peut, fait, trouve, devient, doit, ont, permet, agit, faut
  • Inf (5504; 17% of non-empty VerbForm): faire, partir, voir, prendre, avoir, mettre, passer, trouver, aller, dire
  • Part (12684; 40% of non-empty VerbForm): né, située, situé, eu, fait, mort, connu, nommé, née, réalisé
  • EMPTY (30): voilà, voici, Voila
