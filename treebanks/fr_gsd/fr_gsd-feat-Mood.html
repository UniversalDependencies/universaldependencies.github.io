Treebank Statistics: UD_French-GSD: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Cnd, Imp, Ind, Sub.

24878 tokens (6%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 3435 types (8%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 1351 lemmas (4%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (13701; 3% instances), AUX (11177; 3% instances).
