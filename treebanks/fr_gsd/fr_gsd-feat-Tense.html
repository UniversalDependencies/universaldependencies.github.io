Treebank Statistics: UD_French-GSD: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Imp, Past, Pres.

38688 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 7047 types (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 2014 lemmas (6%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 5 part-of-speech tags: VERB (26448; 7% instances), AUX (12237; 3% instances), ADJ (1; 0% instances), NOUN (1; 0% instances), X (1; 0% instances).
