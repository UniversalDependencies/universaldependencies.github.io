Treebank Statistics: UD_French-GSD: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Imp, Past, Pres.

38657 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 7042 types (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 2015 lemmas (6%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 4 part-of-speech tags: VERB (26422; 7% instances), AUX (12233; 3% instances), ADJ (1; 0% instances), NOUN (1; 0% instances).
