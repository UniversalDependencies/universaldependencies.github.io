Treebank Statistics: UD_French-GSD: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Imp, Past, Pres.

38654 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 7036 types (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 1997 lemmas (6%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (26239; 7% instances), AUX (12415; 3% instances).

VERB

26239 VERB tokens (83% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Tense.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Tense co-occurred: Number=Sing (19517; 74%), Voice=EMPTY (15071; 57%), Gender=EMPTY (15070; 57%), VerbForm=Fin (13555; 52%).

VERB tokens may have the following values of Tense:

  • Fut (607; 2% of non-empty Tense): aura, fera, pourra, permettra, restera, feront, deviendra, permettront, devra, faudra
  • Imp (1075; 4% of non-empty Tense): avait, devait, comptait, pouvait, faisait, allait, avaient, portait, disait, était
  • Past (12235; 47% of non-empty Tense): né, située, situé, eu, fait, mort, connu, nommé, née, réalisé
  • Pres (12322; 47% of non-empty Tense): a, peut, fait, trouve, devient, doit, ont, permet, agit, faut
  • EMPTY (5534): faire, partir, voir, prendre, avoir, mettre, passer, trouver, aller, dire
