Treebank Statistics: UD_French: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fin, Inf, Part.

45120 tokens (11%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 8289 types (19%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 2218 lemmas (7%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (32266; 8% instances), AUX (12854; 3% instances).

VERB

32266 VERB tokens (100% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of VerbForm.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and VerbForm co-occurred: Gender=EMPTY (21043; 65%), Number=Sing (20034; 62%), Mood=EMPTY (18385; 57%), Person=EMPTY (18385; 57%).

VERB tokens may have the following values of VerbForm:

  • Fin (13881; 43% of non-empty VerbForm): a, peut, fait, est, trouve, devient, doit, ont, permet, faut
  • Inf (5585; 17% of non-empty VerbForm): faire, partir, voir, prendre, avoir, mettre, passer, trouver, aller, dire
  • Part (12800; 40% of non-empty VerbForm): né, située, situé, eu, fait, mort, connu, nommé, née, réalisé
  • EMPTY (78): voilà, voici, see, Let, Pump, mining, roll, ‘s, Broken, Compte-tenu
