Treebank Statistics: UD_Malayalam-UFAL: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

328 tokens (14%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 211 types (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 156 lemmas (11%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (216; 9% instances), AUX (112; 5% instances).

VERB

216 VERB tokens (77% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Tense.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Tense co-occurred: Voice=Act (169; 78%), Aspect=EMPTY (168; 78%), Polarity=Pos (164; 76%), VerbForm=Fin (117; 54%), Mood=Ind (109; 50%).
