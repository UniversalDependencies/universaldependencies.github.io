Treebank Statistics: UD_Malayalam-UFAL: Features: Case

This feature is universal but the values Cmp are language-specific. It occurs with 12 different values: Abl, Acc, All, Ben, Cmp, Com, Dat, Gen, Ins, Loc, Nom, Voc.

1092 tokens (45%) have a non-empty value of Case. 942 types (61%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 864 lemmas (64%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case.
