Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish_German-SAGT: Features: Number

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Plur, Sing.

This is a layered feature with the following layers: Number, Number[psor].

14593 tokens (46%) have a non-empty value of Number. 4841 types (81%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Number. 2461 lemmas (72%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Number. The feature is used with 9 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (4503; 14% instances), PRON (3512; 11% instances), VERB (3248; 10% instances), AUX (1370; 4% instances), DET (954; 3% instances), PROPN (643; 2% instances), ADJ (252; 1% instances), ADP (77; 0% instances), NUM (34; 0% instances).
