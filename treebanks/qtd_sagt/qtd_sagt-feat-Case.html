Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish_German-SAGT: Features: Case

This feature is universal but the values Equ are language-specific. It occurs with 8 different values: Abl, Acc, Dat, Equ, Gen, Ins, Loc, Nom.

10475 tokens (33%) have a non-empty value of Case. 3562 types (59%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 2156 lemmas (63%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. The feature is used with 8 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (4504; 14% instances), PRON (3512; 11% instances), DET (954; 3% instances), PROPN (643; 2% instances), VERB (494; 2% instances), ADJ (256; 1% instances), ADP (78; 0% instances), NUM (34; 0% instances).
