Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish_German-SAGT: Features: Gender

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fem, Masc, Neut.

5211 tokens (14%) have a non-empty value of Gender. 1523 types (23%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Gender. 1262 lemmas (34%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Gender.
