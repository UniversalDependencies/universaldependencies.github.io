Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish_German-SAGT: Features: Mood

This feature is universal but the values Gen, GenPot, Prs are language-specific. It occurs with 11 different values: Cnd, Des, Gen, GenPot, Imp, Ind, Nec, Opt, Pot, Prs, Sub.

4692 tokens (13%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 1516 types (23%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 423 lemmas (11%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (3247; 9% instances), AUX (1445; 4% instances).
