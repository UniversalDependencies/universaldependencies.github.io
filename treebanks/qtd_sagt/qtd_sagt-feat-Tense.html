Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish_German-SAGT: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Past, Pqp, Pres.

4139 tokens (13%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 1437 types (24%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 414 lemmas (12%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (2936; 9% instances), AUX (1203; 4% instances).

2936 VERB tokens (71% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Tense.
