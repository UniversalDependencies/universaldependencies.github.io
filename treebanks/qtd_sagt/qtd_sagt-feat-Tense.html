Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish_German-SAGT: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Past, Pqp, Pres.

4886 tokens (13%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 1650 types (25%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 447 lemmas (12%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (3441; 9% instances), AUX (1445; 4% instances).

VERB

3441 VERB tokens (72% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Tense.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Tense co-occurred: Case=EMPTY (3112; 90%), Number=Sing (2885; 84%), Mood=Ind (2581; 75%), VerbForm=EMPTY (2151; 63%), Evident=Fh (1750; 51%).
