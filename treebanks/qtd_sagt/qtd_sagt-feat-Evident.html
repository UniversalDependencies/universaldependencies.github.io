Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish_German-SAGT: Features: Evident

This feature is universal but the values Fh are language-specific. It occurs with 2 different values: Fh, Nfh.

1785 tokens (6%) have a non-empty value of Evident. 903 types (15%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Evident. 210 lemmas (6%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Evident. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (1637; 5% instances), AUX (148; 0% instances).
