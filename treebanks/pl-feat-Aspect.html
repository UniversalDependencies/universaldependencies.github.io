Treebank Statistics: UD_Polish: Features: Aspect

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Imp, Perf.

14972 tokens (18%) have a non-empty value of Aspect. 7775 types (33%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. 3894 lemmas (29%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect.
