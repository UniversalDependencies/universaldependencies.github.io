Treebank Statistics: UD_Czech-CAC: Features: Polarity

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Neg, Pos.

282770 tokens (57%) have a non-empty value of Polarity. 66283 types (106%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Polarity. 27615 lemmas (97%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Polarity.
