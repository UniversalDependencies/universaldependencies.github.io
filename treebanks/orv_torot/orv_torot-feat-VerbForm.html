Treebank Statistics: UD_Old_East_Slavic-TOROT: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values PartRes are language-specific. It occurs with 5 different values: Fin, Inf, Part, PartRes, Sup.

45778 tokens (19%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 19375 types (36%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 3916 lemmas (29%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (40486; 16% instances), AUX (5292; 2% instances).
