Treebank Statistics: UD_Old_Russian-TOROT: Features: Aspect

This feature is universal but the values Res are language-specific. It occurs with 3 different values: Imp, Perf, Res.

13375 tokens (9%) have a non-empty value of Aspect. 4294 types (13%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. 1608 lemmas (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (11854; 8% instances), AUX (1521; 1% instances).
