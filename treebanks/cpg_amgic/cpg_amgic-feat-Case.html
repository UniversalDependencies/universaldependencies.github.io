Treebank Statistics: UD_Cappadocian-AMGiC: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Acc, Gen, Nom, Voc.

302 tokens (37%) have a non-empty value of Case. 169 types (42%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 135 lemmas (41%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case.
