Treebank Statistics: UD_Cappadocian-AMGiC: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Acc, Gen, Nom, Voc.

172 tokens (38%) have a non-empty value of Case. 100 types (43%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 78 lemmas (41%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case.
