Treebank Statistics: UD_Latin: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Gdv are language-specific. It occurs with 5 different values: Fin, Gdv, Ger, Inf, Part.

6159 tokens (21%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 4272 types (43%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 1893 lemmas (31%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (6154; 21% instances), X (4; 0% instances), NOUN (1; 0% instances).
