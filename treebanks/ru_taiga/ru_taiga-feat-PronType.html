Treebank Statistics: UD_Russian-Taiga: Features: PronType

This feature is universal but the values Emp, Exc are language-specific. It occurs with 10 different values: Dem, Emp, Exc, Ind, Int, Neg, Prs, Rcp, Rel, Tot.

184195 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of PronType. 1294 types (1%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. 289 lemmas (1%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: PRON (88826; 5% instances), DET (63833; 4% instances), ADV (31536; 2% instances).
