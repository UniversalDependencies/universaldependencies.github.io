Treebank Statistics: UD_Russian-Taiga: Features: PronType

This feature is universal but the values Emp, Exc are language-specific. It occurs with 10 different values: Dem, Emp, Exc, Ind, Int, Neg, Prs, Rcp, Rel, Tot.

20878 tokens (11%) have a non-empty value of PronType. 809 types (2%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. 191 lemmas (1%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: PRON (11207; 6% instances), DET (5694; 3% instances), ADV (3977; 2% instances).
