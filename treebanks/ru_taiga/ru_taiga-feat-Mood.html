Treebank Statistics: UD_Russian-Taiga: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Cnd, Imp, Ind.

18501 tokens (9%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 7148 types (19%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 3269 lemmas (16%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (16758; 9% instances), AUX (1479; 1% instances), SCONJ (264; 0% instances).

VERB

16758 VERB tokens (68% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Mood.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Mood co-occurred: VerbForm=Fin (16756; 100%), Voice=Act (13341; 80%), Gender=EMPTY (11941; 71%), Number=Sing (11700; 70%), Aspect=Imp (10739; 64%).

VERB tokens may have the following values of Mood:

  • Imp (974; 6% of non-empty Mood): здравствуйте, подскажите, помогите, скажите, посоветуйте, дайте, давай, объясните, дай, пишите
  • Ind (15784; 94% of non-empty Mood): есть, может, могу, хочу, стоит, нравится, знаю, работает, сказал, рекомендую
  • EMPTY (7979): можно, нет, надо, делать, найти, сделать, купить, сказать, быть, есть
