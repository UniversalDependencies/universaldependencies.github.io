Treebank Statistics: UD_Russian-Taiga: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Cnd, Imp, Ind.

157534 tokens (9%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 28947 types (19%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 8830 lemmas (16%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (143629; 8% instances), AUX (11965; 1% instances), SCONJ (1940; 0% instances).
