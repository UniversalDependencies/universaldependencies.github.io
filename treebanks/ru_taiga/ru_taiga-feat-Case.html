Treebank Statistics: UD_Russian-Taiga: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 8 different values: Acc, Dat, Gen, Ins, Loc, Nom, Par, Voc.

79557 tokens (40%) have a non-empty value of Case. 27446 types (72%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 13453 lemmas (67%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case.
