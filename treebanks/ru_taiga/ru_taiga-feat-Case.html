Treebank Statistics: UD_Russian-Taiga: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 8 different values: Acc, Dat, Gen, Ins, Loc, Nom, Par, Voc.

16167 tokens (42%) have a non-empty value of Case. 9213 types (69%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 5767 lemmas (64%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case.
