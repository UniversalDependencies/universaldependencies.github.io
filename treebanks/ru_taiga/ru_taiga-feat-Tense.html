Treebank Statistics: UD_Russian-Taiga: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

3765 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 2704 types (20%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 1655 lemmas (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 4 part-of-speech tags: VERB (3569; 9% instances), AUX (193; 1% instances), NOUN (2; 0% instances), PROPN (1; 0% instances).
