Treebank Statistics: UD_Lithuanian-HSE: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Cnd, Imp, Ind, Nec.

498 tokens (9%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 328 types (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 224 lemmas (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (393; 7% instances), AUX (105; 2% instances).
