Treebank Statistics: UD_Lithuanian-HSE: Features: Voice

This feature is universal but the values Necess are language-specific. It occurs with 3 different values: Act, Necess, Pass.

818 tokens (15%) have a non-empty value of Voice. 605 types (26%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. 359 lemmas (23%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (706; 13% instances), AUX (112; 2% instances).

VERB

706 VERB tokens (100% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Voice.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Voice co-occurred: Reflex=No (654; 93%), Polarity=Pos (609; 86%), Case=EMPTY (560; 79%), Definite=EMPTY (538; 76%), Gender=EMPTY (528; 75%), VerbForm=Fin (383; 54%), Number=Sing (374; 53%).

VERB tokens may have the following values of Voice:

  • Act (623; 88% of non-empty Voice): gali, turi, negali, vadinami, būti, nėra, sako, žino, analizuoja, bando
  • Necess (8; 1% of non-empty Voice): Būtina, baustinas, gerbtinų, leistinas, neleistina, nepriimtina, priimtinas, sektinų
  • Pass (75; 11% of non-empty Voice): žinoma, galima, bandoma, grįžtamasis, laikomas, paskelbta, pastebimi, skirtas, Kalbama, apibrėžimas
  • EMPTY (2): Remiantis, prašom
