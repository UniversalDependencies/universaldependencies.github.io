Treebank Statistics: UD_Lithuanian-HSE: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

618 tokens (12%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 445 types (19%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 281 lemmas (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (523; 10% instances), AUX (95; 2% instances).

VERB

523 VERB tokens (74% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Tense.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Tense co-occurred: Voice=Act (448; 86%), Polarity=Pos (445; 85%), Case=EMPTY (383; 73%), Definite=EMPTY (363; 69%), Gender=EMPTY (362; 69%), Mood=Ind (351; 67%), VerbForm=Fin (349; 67%), Number=Sing (348; 67%), Person=3 (296; 57%).
