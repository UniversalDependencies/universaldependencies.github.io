Treebank Statistics: UD_Belarusian-HSE: Features: Voice

This feature is universal but the values Mid are language-specific. It occurs with 3 different values: Act, Mid, Pass.

29567 tokens (11%) have a non-empty value of Voice. 10429 types (23%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. 4122 lemmas (15%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. The feature is used with 4 part-of-speech tags: VERB (27911; 10% instances), AUX (1651; 1% instances), NOUN (3; 0% instances), ADJ (2; 0% instances).
