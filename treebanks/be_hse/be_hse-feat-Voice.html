Treebank Statistics: UD_Belarusian-HSE: Features: Voice

This feature is universal but the values Mid are language-specific. It occurs with 3 different values: Act, Mid, Pass.

32219 tokens (11%) have a non-empty value of Voice. 11272 types (22%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. 4258 lemmas (15%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (30316; 10% instances), AUX (1902; 1% instances), NOUN (1; 0% instances).
