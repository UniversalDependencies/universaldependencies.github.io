Treebank Statistics: UD_Belarusian-HSE: Features: PronType

This feature is universal but the values Emp, Exc are language-specific. It occurs with 9 different values: Dem, Emp, Exc, Ind, Int, Neg, Prs, Rel, Tot.

16983 tokens (6%) have a non-empty value of PronType. 639 types (1%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. 122 lemmas (0%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: PRON (10302; 3% instances), DET (6681; 2% instances).
