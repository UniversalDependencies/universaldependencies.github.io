This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Cnd, Imp, Ind, Sub.

4464 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 502 types (15%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 276 lemmas (11%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (2430; 6% instances), AUX (2034; 5% instances).
