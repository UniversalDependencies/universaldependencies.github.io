Treebank Statistics: UD_Kazakh-KTB: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv, Cov are language-specific. It occurs with 5 different values: Conv, Cov, Fin, Ger, Part.

2003 tokens (19%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 1178 types (27%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 424 lemmas (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (1587; 15% instances), AUX (416; 4% instances).
