Treebank Statistics: UD_Kazakh-KTB: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

1184 tokens (11%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 627 types (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 290 lemmas (12%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (913; 9% instances), AUX (271; 3% instances).

VERB

913 VERB tokens (56% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Tense.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Tense co-occurred: Case=EMPTY (837; 92%), Voice=EMPTY (733; 80%), VerbForm=Fin (712; 78%), Mood=Ind (710; 78%), Aspect=EMPTY (633; 69%), Person=3 (626; 69%), Number=Sing (620; 68%).
