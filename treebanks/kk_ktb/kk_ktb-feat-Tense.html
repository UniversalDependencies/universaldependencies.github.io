Treebank Statistics: UD_Kazakh-KTB: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

1184 tokens (11%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 627 types (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 290 lemmas (12%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (866; 8% instances), AUX (318; 3% instances).

VERB

866 VERB tokens (55% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Tense.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Tense co-occurred: Case=EMPTY (791; 91%), Voice=EMPTY (686; 79%), VerbForm=Fin (666; 77%), Mood=Ind (664; 77%), Aspect=EMPTY (594; 69%), Person=3 (581; 67%), Number=Sing (580; 67%).
