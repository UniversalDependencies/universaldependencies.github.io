Treebank Statistics: UD_Kazakh-KTB: Features: Voice

This feature is universal but the values Coop are language-specific. It occurs with 2 different values: Coop, Pass.

266 tokens (3%) have a non-empty value of Voice. 196 types (4%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. 117 lemmas (5%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. The feature is used with 1 part-of-speech tags: VERB (266; 3% instances).
