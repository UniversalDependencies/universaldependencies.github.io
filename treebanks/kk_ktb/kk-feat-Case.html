Treebank Statistics: UD_Kazakh: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 7 different values: Abl, Acc, Dat, Gen, Ins, Loc, Nom.

4684 tokens (42%) have a non-empty value of Case. 2949 types (67%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 1601 lemmas (64%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case.
