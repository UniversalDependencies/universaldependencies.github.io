Treebank Statistics: UD_Kazakh-KTB: Features: Mood

This feature is universal but the values Vol are language-specific. It occurs with 6 different values: Cnd, Imp, Ind, Opt, Pot, Vol.

1068 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 576 types (13%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 263 lemmas (11%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (766; 7% instances), AUX (302; 3% instances).
