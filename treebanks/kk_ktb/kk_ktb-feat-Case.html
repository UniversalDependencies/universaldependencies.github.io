Treebank Statistics: UD_Kazakh-KTB: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 7 different values: Abl, Acc, Dat, Gen, Ins, Loc, Nom.

4467 tokens (42%) have a non-empty value of Case. 2920 types (67%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 1526 lemmas (63%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case.
