Treebank Statistics: UD_Kazakh: Features: PronType

This feature is universal but the values Qnt, Ref are language-specific. It occurs with 9 different values: Dem, Ind, Int, Neg, Prs, Qnt, Rcp, Ref, Tot.

771 tokens (7%) have a non-empty value of PronType. 233 types (5%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. 83 lemmas (3%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. The feature is used with 4 part-of-speech tags: PRON (517; 5% instances), DET (221; 2% instances), ADV (32; 0% instances), ADJ (1; 0% instances).
