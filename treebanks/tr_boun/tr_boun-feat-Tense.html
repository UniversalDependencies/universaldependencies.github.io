Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish-BOUN: Features: Tense

This feature is universal but the values Aor are language-specific. It occurs with 5 different values: Aor, Fut, Past, Pqp, Pres.

16302 tokens (13%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 7839 types (22%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 1288 lemmas (11%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense.
