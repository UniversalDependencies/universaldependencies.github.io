Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish-BOUN: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Past, Pqp, Pres.

15313 tokens (13%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 7145 types (20%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 1223 lemmas (11%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (14318; 12% instances), AUX (995; 1% instances).

VERB

14318 VERB tokens (69% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Tense.
