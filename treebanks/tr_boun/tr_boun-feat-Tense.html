Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish-BOUN: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

15979 tokens (13%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 6627 types (19%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 1062 lemmas (9%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (13349; 11% instances), AUX (2630; 2% instances).
