Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish-BOUN: Features: Aspect

This feature is universal but the values Hab, Prosp are language-specific. It occurs with 5 different values: Hab, Imp, Perf, Prog, Prosp.

13692 tokens (11%) have a non-empty value of Aspect. 6282 types (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. 1047 lemmas (9%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (12355; 10% instances), AUX (1337; 1% instances).
