Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish-BOUN: Features: Case

This feature is universal but the values Equ are language-specific. It occurs with 8 different values: Abl, Acc, Dat, Equ, Gen, Ins, Loc, Nom.

57149 tokens (46%) have a non-empty value of Case. 27643 types (81%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 10480 lemmas (86%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. The feature is used with 9 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (37858; 30% instances), PROPN (7757; 6% instances), VERB (6170; 5% instances), PRON (3578; 3% instances), ADJ (741; 1% instances), NUM (676; 1% instances), AUX (293; 0% instances), ADP (73; 0% instances), PART (3; 0% instances).
