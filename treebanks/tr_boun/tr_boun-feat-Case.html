Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish-BOUN: Features: Case

This feature is universal but the values Equ are language-specific. It occurs with 8 different values: Abl, Acc, Dat, Equ, Gen, Ins, Loc, Nom.

58070 tokens (47%) have a non-empty value of Case. 27824 types (79%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 9969 lemmas (89%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. The feature is used with 11 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (36318; 30% instances), VERB (6180; 5% instances), PROPN (5675; 5% instances), ADJ (5504; 4% instances), PRON (3146; 3% instances), NUM (682; 1% instances), AUX (346; 0% instances), ADP (202; 0% instances), ADV (13; 0% instances), X (3; 0% instances), PART (1; 0% instances).
