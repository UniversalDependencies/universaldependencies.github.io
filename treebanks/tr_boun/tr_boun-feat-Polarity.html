Treebank Statistics: UD_Turkish-BOUN: Features: Polarity

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Neg, Pos.

23991 tokens (19%) have a non-empty value of Polarity. 10825 types (32%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Polarity. 1408 lemmas (12%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Polarity.
