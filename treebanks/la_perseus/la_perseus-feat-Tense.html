Treebank Statistics: UD_Latin-Perseus: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Past, Pqp, Pres.

6102 tokens (21%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 4217 types (42%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 1391 lemmas (30%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (5735; 20% instances), AUX (367; 1% instances).

5735 VERB tokens (99% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Tense.
