Treebank Statistics: UD_Finnish-PUD: Features: Voice

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Act, Pass.

2769 tokens (18%) have a non-empty value of Voice. 1427 types (19%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. 616 lemmas (12%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (1998; 13% instances), AUX (771; 5% instances).

VERB

1998 VERB tokens (98% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Voice.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Voice co-occurred: InfForm=EMPTY (1630; 82%), Degree=EMPTY (1547; 77%), PartForm=EMPTY (1547; 77%), Number=Sing (1452; 73%), Case=EMPTY (1370; 69%), VerbForm=Fin (1178; 59%), Mood=Ind (1131; 57%), Person=EMPTY (1010; 51%).

VERB tokens may have the following values of Voice:

  • Act (1667; 83% of non-empty Voice): sanoi, tulee, tuli, sanoo, kertoi, alkoi, auttaa, eikä, johti, sai
  • Pass (331; 17% of non-empty Voice): rakennettiin, käytettiin, pidetään, julkaistiin, julistettiin, kerrottiin, käytetty, perustettiin, sanotaan, tehty
  • EMPTY (33): pysty, odota, ole, tiedä, auta, eroaisi, haittaa, haluaisi, häviä, kaipaa
