Treebank Statistics: UD_Finnish-PUD: Features: Voice

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Act, Pass.

2772 tokens (18%) have a non-empty value of Voice. 1424 types (19%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. 611 lemmas (12%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (1984; 13% instances), AUX (788; 5% instances).

VERB

1984 VERB tokens (98% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Voice.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Voice co-occurred: InfForm=EMPTY (1613; 81%), Degree=EMPTY (1533; 77%), PartForm=EMPTY (1533; 77%), Number=Sing (1439; 73%), Case=EMPTY (1353; 68%), VerbForm=Fin (1161; 59%), Mood=Ind (1129; 57%), Person=EMPTY (1013; 51%).
