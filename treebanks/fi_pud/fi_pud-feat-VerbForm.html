Treebank Statistics: UD_Finnish-PUD: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fin, Inf, Part.

2843 tokens (18%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 1451 types (19%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 618 lemmas (12%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (2034; 13% instances), AUX (809; 5% instances).
