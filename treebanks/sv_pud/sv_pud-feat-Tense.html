Treebank Statistics: UD_Swedish-PUD: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Past, Pres.

2157 tokens (11%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 870 types (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 680 lemmas (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (1270; 7% instances), AUX (609; 3% instances), ADJ (278; 1% instances).
