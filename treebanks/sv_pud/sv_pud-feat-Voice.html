Treebank Statistics: UD_Swedish-PUD: Features: Voice

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Act, Pass.

2692 tokens (14%) have a non-empty value of Voice. 1107 types (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. 628 lemmas (13%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (1962; 10% instances), AUX (730; 4% instances).
