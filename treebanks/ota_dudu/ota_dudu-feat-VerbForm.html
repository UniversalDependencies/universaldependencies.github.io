Treebank Statistics: UD_Ottoman_Turkish-DUDU: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv, Vnoun are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Conv, Fin, Part, Vnoun.

1767 tokens (17%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 881 types (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 303 lemmas (10%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (1747; 17% instances), AUX (20; 0% instances).

1747 VERB tokens (98% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of VerbForm.
