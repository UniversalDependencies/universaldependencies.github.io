Treebank Statistics: UD_Ottoman_Turkish-DUDU: Features: Aspect

This feature is universal but the values Hab, Prosp are language-specific. It occurs with 5 different values: Hab, Imp, Perf, Prog, Prosp.

1477 tokens (14%) have a non-empty value of Aspect. 618 types (12%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. 239 lemmas (8%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (1190; 12% instances), AUX (287; 3% instances).
