Treebank Statistics: UD_Ottoman_Turkish-DUDU: Features: Aspect

This feature is universal but the values Hab, Prosp are language-specific. It occurs with 5 different values: Hab, Imp, Perf, Prog, Prosp.

2571 tokens (15%) have a non-empty value of Aspect. 947 types (13%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. 330 lemmas (8%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (2146; 13% instances), AUX (425; 2% instances).
