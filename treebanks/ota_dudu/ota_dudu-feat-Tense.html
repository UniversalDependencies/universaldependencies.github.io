Treebank Statistics: UD_Ottoman_Turkish-DUDU: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Past, Pqp, Pres.

2701 tokens (16%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 1001 types (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 327 lemmas (8%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (2261; 13% instances), AUX (440; 3% instances).

2261 VERB tokens (70% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Tense.
