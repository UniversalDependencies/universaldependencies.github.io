Treebank Statistics: UD_Ottoman_Turkish-DUDU: Features: Case

This feature is universal but the values Equ are language-specific. It occurs with 8 different values: Abl, Acc, Dat, Equ, Gen, Ins, Loc, Nom.

5390 tokens (52%) have a non-empty value of Case. 3666 types (72%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 2358 lemmas (75%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. The feature is used with 7 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (4128; 40% instances), PROPN (600; 6% instances), PRON (346; 3% instances), VERB (149; 1% instances), ADJ (132; 1% instances), NUM (28; 0% instances), AUX (7; 0% instances).
