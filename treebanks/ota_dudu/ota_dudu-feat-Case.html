Treebank Statistics: UD_Ottoman_Turkish-DUDU: Features: Case

This feature is universal but the values Equ are language-specific. It occurs with 8 different values: Abl, Acc, Dat, Equ, Gen, Ins, Loc, Nom.

8908 tokens (52%) have a non-empty value of Case. 5276 types (72%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 3095 lemmas (76%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. The feature is used with 7 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (6583; 38% instances), PROPN (1174; 7% instances), PRON (701; 4% instances), VERB (233; 1% instances), ADJ (173; 1% instances), NUM (36; 0% instances), AUX (8; 0% instances).
