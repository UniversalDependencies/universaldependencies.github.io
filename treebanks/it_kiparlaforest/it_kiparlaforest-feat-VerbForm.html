Treebank Statistics: UD_Italian-KIParlaForest: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fin, Ger, Inf, Part.

1705 tokens (18%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 507 types (30%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 213 lemmas (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 4 part-of-speech tags: VERB (1114; 12% instances), AUX (566; 6% instances), ADJ (13; 0% instances), NOUN (12; 0% instances).
