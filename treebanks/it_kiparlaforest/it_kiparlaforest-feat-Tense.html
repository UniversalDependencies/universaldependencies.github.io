Treebank Statistics: UD_Italian-KIParlaForest: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Imp, Past, Pres.

1483 tokens (16%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 406 types (24%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 183 lemmas (15%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 4 part-of-speech tags: VERB (908; 10% instances), AUX (550; 6% instances), ADJ (13; 0% instances), NOUN (12; 0% instances).
