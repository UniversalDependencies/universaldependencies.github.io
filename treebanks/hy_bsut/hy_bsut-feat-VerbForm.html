Treebank Statistics: UD_Armenian-BSUT: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Conv, Fin, Inf, Part.

634 tokens (20%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 321 types (24%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 211 lemmas (21%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (380; 12% instances), AUX (254; 8% instances).
