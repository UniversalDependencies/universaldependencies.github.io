Treebank Statistics: UD_Armenian-BSUT: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Conv, Fin, Inf, Part.

7550 tokens (18%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 2707 types (25%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 1049 lemmas (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (4935; 12% instances), AUX (2615; 6% instances).
