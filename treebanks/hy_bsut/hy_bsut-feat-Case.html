Treebank Statistics: UD_Armenian-BSUT: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 6 different values: Abl, Dat, Gen, Ins, Loc, Nom.

1102 tokens (35%) have a non-empty value of Case. 773 types (57%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 539 lemmas (53%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. The feature is used with 6 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (738; 23% instances), PROPN (183; 6% instances), PRON (109; 3% instances), VERB (46; 1% instances), DET (25; 1% instances), ADP (1; 0% instances).
