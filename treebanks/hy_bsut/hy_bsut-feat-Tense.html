Treebank Statistics: UD_Armenian-BSUT: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Imp, Past, Pres.

3876 tokens (9%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 803 types (7%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 375 lemmas (6%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: AUX (2601; 6% instances), VERB (1275; 3% instances).
