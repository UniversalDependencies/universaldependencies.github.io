Treebank Statistics: UD_Armenian-BSUT: Features: PronType

This feature is universal but the values Emp, Exc are language-specific. It occurs with 11 different values: Art, Dem, Emp, Exc, Ind, Int, Neg, Prs, Rcp, Rel, Tot.

3331 tokens (7%) have a non-empty value of PronType. 280 types (2%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. 108 lemmas (2%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType.
