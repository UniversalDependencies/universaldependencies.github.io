Treebank Statistics: UD_Slovenian-SST: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Fut, Pres.

10297 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 1524 types (11%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 878 lemmas (12%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (5838; 6% instances), AUX (4459; 5% instances).
