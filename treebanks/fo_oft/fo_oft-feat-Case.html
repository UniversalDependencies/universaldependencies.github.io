Treebank Statistics: UD_Faroese-OFT: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Acc, Dat, Gen, Nom.

4668 tokens (47%) have a non-empty value of Case. 2696 types (86%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 1993 lemmas (83%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. The feature is used with 7 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (2472; 25% instances), PROPN (805; 8% instances), ADJ (721; 7% instances), PRON (261; 3% instances), NUM (217; 2% instances), DET (185; 2% instances), VERB (7; 0% instances).
