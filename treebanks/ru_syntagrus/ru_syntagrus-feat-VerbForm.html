Treebank Statistics: UD_Russian-SynTagRus: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Conv, Fin, Inf, Part.

184042 tokens (12%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 42821 types (30%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 9484 lemmas (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (172346; 11% instances), AUX (11695; 1% instances), ADJ (1; 0% instances).
