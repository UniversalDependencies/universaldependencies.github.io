Treebank Statistics: UD_Russian-SynTagRus: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Conv, Fin, Inf, Part.

131850 tokens (12%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 33904 types (29%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 6099 lemmas (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (123706; 11% instances), AUX (8144; 1% instances).
