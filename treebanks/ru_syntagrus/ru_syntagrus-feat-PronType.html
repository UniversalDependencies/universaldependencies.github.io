Treebank Statistics: UD_Russian-SynTagRus: Features: PronType

This feature is universal but the values Emp, Exc are language-specific. It occurs with 10 different values: Dem, Emp, Exc, Ind, Int, Neg, Prs, Rcp, Rel, Tot. Some words have combined values of the feature; 1 combinations have been observed: Int|Rel.

145889 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of PronType. 1071 types (1%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. 194 lemmas (0%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: PRON (66174; 4% instances), DET (54745; 4% instances), ADV (24970; 2% instances).
