Treebank Statistics: UD_Russian-SynTagRus: Features: Aspect

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Imp, Perf.

131845 tokens (12%) have a non-empty value of Aspect. 33902 types (29%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. 7830 lemmas (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (123700; 11% instances), AUX (8145; 1% instances).
