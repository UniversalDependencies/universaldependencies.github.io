Treebank Statistics: UD_Italian-ParTUT: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fin, Ger, Inf, Part.

6768 tokens (12%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 2783 types (33%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 975 lemmas (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (4724; 9% instances), AUX (2044; 4% instances).
