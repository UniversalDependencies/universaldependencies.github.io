Treebank Statistics: UD_Italian-ParTUT: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Imp, Past, Pres.

5218 tokens (9%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 2092 types (25%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 814 lemmas (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (3348; 6% instances), AUX (1870; 3% instances).
