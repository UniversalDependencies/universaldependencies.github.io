Treebank Statistics: UD_Italian-ParTUT: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Cnd, Imp, Ind, Sub.

3523 tokens (6%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 1194 types (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 534 lemmas (9%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (1810; 3% instances), AUX (1713; 3% instances).
