Treebank Statistics: UD_Low_Saxon-LSDC: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Acc, Dat, Gen, Nom. Some words have combined values of the feature; 1 combinations have been observed: Acc|Dat.

8264 tokens (37%) have a non-empty value of Case. 2283 types (48%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 1650 lemmas (51%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. The feature is used with 6 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (2798; 12% instances), PRON (2544; 11% instances), DET (2205; 10% instances), ADJ (672; 3% instances), PROPN (28; 0% instances), NUM (17; 0% instances).
