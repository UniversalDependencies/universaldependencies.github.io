Treebank Statistics: UD_Low_Saxon-LSDC: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Acc, Dat, Gen, Nom. Some words have combined values of the feature; 1 combinations have been observed: Acc|Dat.

918 tokens (36%) have a non-empty value of Case. 419 types (45%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 338 lemmas (47%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. The feature is used with 6 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (289; 11% instances), PRON (285; 11% instances), DET (244; 10% instances), ADJ (79; 3% instances), PROPN (18; 1% instances), NUM (3; 0% instances).
