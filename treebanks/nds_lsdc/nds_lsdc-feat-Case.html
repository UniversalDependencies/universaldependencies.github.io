Treebank Statistics: UD_Low_Saxon-LSDC: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Acc, Dat, Gen, Nom. Some words have combined values of the feature; 1 combinations have been observed: Acc|Dat.

8262 tokens (36%) have a non-empty value of Case. 2283 types (48%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 1734 lemmas (51%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. The feature is used with 6 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (2796; 12% instances), PRON (2557; 11% instances), DET (2190; 10% instances), ADJ (668; 3% instances), PROPN (34; 0% instances), NUM (17; 0% instances).
