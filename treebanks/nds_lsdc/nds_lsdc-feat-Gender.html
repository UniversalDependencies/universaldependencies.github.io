Treebank Statistics: UD_Low_Saxon-LSDC: Features: Gender

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fem, Masc, Neut. Some words have combined values of the feature; 2 combinations have been observed: Fem|Masc, Masc|Neut.
