Treebank Statistics: UD_Czech: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 7 different values: Acc, Dat, Gen, Ins, Loc, Nom, Voc.

855229 tokens (57%) have a non-empty value of Case. 105403 types (82%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 42842 lemmas (74%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case.
