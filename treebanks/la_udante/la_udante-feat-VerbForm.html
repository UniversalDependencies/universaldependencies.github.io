Treebank Statistics: UD_Latin-UDante: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fin, Inf, Part.

11284 tokens (20%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 4995 types (42%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 1696 lemmas (30%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 8 part-of-speech tags: VERB (8224; 15% instances), AUX (2200; 4% instances), NOUN (341; 1% instances), ADV (172; 0% instances), CCONJ (115; 0% instances), ADP (99; 0% instances), ADJ (97; 0% instances), SCONJ (36; 0% instances).
