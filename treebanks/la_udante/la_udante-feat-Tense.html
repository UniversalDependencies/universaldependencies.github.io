Treebank Statistics: UD_Latin-UDante: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

6641 tokens (12%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 2592 types (22%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 1101 lemmas (20%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (4797; 9% instances), AUX (1844; 3% instances).
