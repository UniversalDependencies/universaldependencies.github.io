Treebank Statistics: UD_Latin-UDante: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Past, Pqp, Pres.

8112 tokens (15%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 3201 types (27%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 1253 lemmas (22%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (5920; 11% instances), AUX (2192; 4% instances).
