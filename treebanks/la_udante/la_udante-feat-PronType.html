Treebank Statistics: UD_Latin-UDante: Features: PronType

This feature is universal but the values Con are language-specific. It occurs with 9 different values: Con, Dem, Ind, Int, Neg, Prs, Rcp, Rel, Tot.

8559 tokens (15%) have a non-empty value of PronType. 551 types (5%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. 132 lemmas (2%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType.
