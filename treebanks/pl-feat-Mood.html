Treebank Statistics: UD_Polish: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Cnd, Imp, Ind.

5858 tokens (7%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 2079 types (9%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 1200 lemmas (9%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (4135; 5% instances), AUX (1723; 2% instances).

VERB

4135 VERB tokens (38% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Mood.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Mood co-occurred: VerbForm=Fin (4135; 100%), Voice=EMPTY (4135; 100%), Gender=EMPTY (4135; 100%), Animacy=EMPTY (4135; 100%), Tense=Pres (3956; 96%), Aspect=Imp (3514; 85%), Number=Sing (3000; 73%), Person=3 (2926; 71%).
