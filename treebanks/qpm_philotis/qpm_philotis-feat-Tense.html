Treebank Statistics: UD_Pomak-Philotis: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Past, Pres.

20495 tokens (24%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 4882 types (45%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 1467 lemmas (37%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (13860; 16% instances), AUX (6635; 8% instances).
