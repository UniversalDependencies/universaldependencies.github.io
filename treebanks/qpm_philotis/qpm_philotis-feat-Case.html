Treebank Statistics: UD_Pomak-Philotis: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Acc, Gen, Nom, Voc.

11133 tokens (32%) have a non-empty value of Case. 3359 types (53%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 1685 lemmas (52%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case.
