Treebank Statistics: UD_Pomak-Philotis: Features: PronType

This feature is universal but the values Emp are language-specific. It occurs with 7 different values: Dem, Emp, Ind, Int, Prs, Rel, Tot.

5279 tokens (15%) have a non-empty value of PronType. 489 types (8%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. 103 lemmas (3%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: PRON (3382; 10% instances), DET (1361; 4% instances), ADV (536; 2% instances).
