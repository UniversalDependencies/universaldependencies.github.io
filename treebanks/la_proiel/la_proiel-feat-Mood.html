Treebank Statistics: UD_Latin-PROIEL: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Imp, Ind, Sub.

29907 tokens (17%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 8057 types (34%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 1580 lemmas (22%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (23918; 14% instances), AUX (5989; 3% instances).
