Treebank Statistics: UD_Latin-PROIEL: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Imp, Ind, Sub.

35000 tokens (17%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 9658 types (32%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 1785 lemmas (21%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (27772; 14% instances), AUX (7228; 4% instances).
