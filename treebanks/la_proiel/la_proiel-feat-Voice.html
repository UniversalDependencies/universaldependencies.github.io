Treebank Statistics: UD_Latin-PROIEL: Features: Voice

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Act, Pass.

47474 tokens (24%) have a non-empty value of Voice. 14287 types (49%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. 2167 lemmas (26%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (39629; 20% instances), AUX (7845; 4% instances).

VERB

39629 VERB tokens (97% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Voice.
