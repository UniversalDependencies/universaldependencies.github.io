Treebank Statistics: UD_Latin-PROIEL: Features: Voice

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Act, Pass.

47434 tokens (24%) have a non-empty value of Voice. 14280 types (49%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. 2189 lemmas (26%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Voice. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (39598; 20% instances), AUX (7836; 4% instances).
