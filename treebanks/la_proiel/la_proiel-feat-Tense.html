Treebank Statistics: UD_Latin-PROIEL: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Past, Pqp, Pres.

41408 tokens (24%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 12044 types (50%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 1928 lemmas (27%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (34734; 20% instances), AUX (6674; 4% instances).
