Treebank Statistics: UD_Latin-PROIEL: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Past, Pqp, Pres.

48661 tokens (24%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 14542 types (49%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 2188 lemmas (25%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (40560; 20% instances), AUX (8101; 4% instances).

40560 VERB tokens (97% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Tense.
