Treebank Statistics: UD_Latin-PROIEL: Features: PronType

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Int, Prs, Rcp, Rel.

20190 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of PronType. 202 types (1%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. 69 lemmas (1%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: PRON (18506; 9% instances), ADV (1684; 1% instances).
