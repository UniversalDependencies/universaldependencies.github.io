Treebank Statistics: UD_Latin-PROIEL: Features: PronType

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Int, Prs, Rcp, Rel.

20843 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of PronType. 206 types (1%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. 70 lemmas (1%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: PRON (19102; 9% instances), ADV (1741; 1% instances).
