Treebank Statistics: UD_Kangri-KDTB: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Conv, Fin, Inf, Part.

392 tokens (16%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 147 types (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 75 lemmas (8%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: AUX (206; 8% instances), VERB (186; 7% instances).
