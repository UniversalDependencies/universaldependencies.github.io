Treebank Statistics: UD_Kazakh: Features: Tense

This feature is universal but the values Aor, FutPlan are language-specific. It occurs with 5 different values: Aor, Fut, FutPlan, Past, Pres.

1240 tokens (11%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 638 types (15%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 312 lemmas (12%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (908; 8% instances), AUX (332; 3% instances).

VERB

908 VERB tokens (55% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Tense.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Tense co-occurred: Aspect=EMPTY (908; 100%), Case=EMPTY (833; 92%), Voice=EMPTY (723; 80%), Mood=Ind (702; 77%), VerbForm=Fin (702; 77%), Person=3 (617; 68%), Number=Sing (616; 68%).
