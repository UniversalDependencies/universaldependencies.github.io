Description

The UD_Punjabi_CS is a manually annotated treebank in Punjabi (also called Eastern Punjabi, Gurmukhi script) language. The Indo-Aryan language is prominently spoken in Punjab region of India and Pakistan with strong diasporas in Western countries like Canada, UK and USA etc. It is written from Left-to-Right with Subject-Object-Verb (SOV) word ordering.

The treebank consists of total 113 sentences, of which 94 are from the first 2 chapters of novel ‘ਛੋਟਾ ਸ਼ਹਿਜ਼ਾਦਾ’ translated from novel ‘The Little Prince’ or ‘Petit Prince’ authored by Antoine de Saint-Exupéry, and other 19 sentences from SALDEF, transcript of speeches given during a major Farmers movement against the now-repealed farm laws enacted by the Indian government, representing discourse. The data has been annotated according to Universal Dependencies guidelines.
