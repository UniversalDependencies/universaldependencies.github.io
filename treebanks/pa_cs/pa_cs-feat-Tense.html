Treebank Statistics: UD_Punjabi-CS: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

221 tokens (12%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 106 types (16%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 57 lemmas (11%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (131; 7% instances), AUX (90; 5% instances).
