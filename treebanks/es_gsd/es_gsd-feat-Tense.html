Treebank Statistics: UD_Spanish-GSD: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Imp, Past, Pres.

32699 tokens (8%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 6504 types (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 2239 lemmas (6%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (23076; 5% instances), AUX (9623; 2% instances).

23076 VERB tokens (63% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Tense.
