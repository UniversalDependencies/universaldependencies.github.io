Treebank Statistics: UD_Galician-PUD: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 5 different values: Fut, Imp, Past, Pqp, Pres.

1860 tokens (8%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 1014 types (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 508 lemmas (11%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (1464; 6% instances), AUX (396; 2% instances).
