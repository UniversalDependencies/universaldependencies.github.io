Treebank Statistics: UD_Romanian-SiMoNERo: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Acc, Dat, Gen, Nom. Some words have combined values of the feature; 1 combinations have been observed: Dat|Gen.

6576 tokens (45%) have a non-empty value of Case. 2193 types (54%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 1413 lemmas (52%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case.
