Treebank Statistics: UD_Romanian-TueCL: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Imp, Past, Pqp, Pres.

568 tokens (13%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 308 types (20%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 156 lemmas (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (423; 10% instances), AUX (145; 3% instances).
