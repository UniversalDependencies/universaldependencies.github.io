Treebank Statistics: UD_French: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Imp, Past, Pres.

38895 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 7052 types (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 2019 lemmas (6%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (26681; 7% instances), AUX (12211; 3% instances), NOUN (3; 0% instances).
