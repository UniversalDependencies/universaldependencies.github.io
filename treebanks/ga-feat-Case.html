Treebank Statistics: UD_Irish: Features: Case

This feature is universal but the values NomAcc are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Dat, Gen, NomAcc, Voc.

6948 tokens (29%) have a non-empty value of Case. 4112 types (72%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 2785 lemmas (72%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case.
