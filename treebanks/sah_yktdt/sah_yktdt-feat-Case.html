Treebank Statistics: UD_Yakut-YKTDT: Features: Case

This feature is universal but the values Cmp are language-specific. It occurs with 7 different values: Abl, Acc, Cmp, Dat, Ins, Nom, Par.

183 tokens (37%) have a non-empty value of Case. 151 types (44%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 119 lemmas (46%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. The feature is used with 4 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (135; 27% instances), PRON (31; 6% instances), PROPN (16; 3% instances), DET (1; 0% instances).
