Treebank Statistics: UD_Buryat: Features: Aspect

This feature is universal but the values Hab are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Hab, Imp, Perf, Prog.

112 tokens (1%) have a non-empty value of Aspect. 75 types (2%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. 59 lemmas (2%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (99; 1% instances), AUX (13; 0% instances).
