Treebank Statistics: UD_Buryat-BDT: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Conv, Fin, Ger, Inf.

1034 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 602 types (15%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 289 lemmas (12%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (923; 9% instances), AUX (111; 1% instances).
