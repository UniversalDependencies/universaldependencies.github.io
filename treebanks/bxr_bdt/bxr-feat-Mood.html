Treebank Statistics: UD_Buryat: Features: Mood

This feature is universal but the values Prsc are language-specific. It occurs with 5 different values: Cnd, Des, Imp, Ind, Prsc.

683 tokens (7%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 387 types (10%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 212 lemmas (9%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (622; 6% instances), AUX (61; 1% instances).

VERB

622 VERB tokens (40% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Mood.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Mood co-occurred: Number=Sing (440; 71%), VerbForm=Fin (431; 69%), Person=3 (430; 69%).

  • Prsc (2; 0% of non-empty Mood): ябаарай
  • EMPTY (944): гэжэ байгаа ажаһуудаг болонхой байдаг боложо болохо хэжэ ябаха гэхэдэ
