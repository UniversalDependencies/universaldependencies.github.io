Treebank Statistics: UD_Armenian-ArmTDP: Features: Definite

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Def, Ind.

3937 tokens (32%) have a non-empty value of Definite. 2679 types (60%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Definite. 1657 lemmas (58%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Definite.
