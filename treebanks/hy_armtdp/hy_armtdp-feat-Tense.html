Treebank Statistics: UD_Armenian-ArmTDP: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Imp, Past, Pres.

5389 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 1059 types (8%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 445 lemmas (6%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: AUX (3607; 7% instances), VERB (1782; 3% instances).
