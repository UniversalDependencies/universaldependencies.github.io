Treebank Statistics: UD_Armenian-ArmTDP: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Imp, Past, Pres.

2302 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 567 types (8%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 283 lemmas (7%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: AUX (1480; 6% instances), VERB (822; 4% instances).
