Treebank Statistics: UD_Armenian-ArmTDP: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Conv, Fin, Inf, Part.

18036 tokens (17%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 5073 types (22%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 1575 lemmas (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (11758; 11% instances), AUX (6278; 6% instances).

VERB

11758 VERB tokens (100% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of VerbForm.
