Treebank Statistics: UD_Armenian-ArmTDP: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv, Gdv are language-specific. It occurs with 5 different values: Conv, Fin, Gdv, Inf, Part.

4385 tokens (19%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 1827 types (25%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 759 lemmas (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (2878; 13% instances), AUX (1504; 7% instances), ADJ (3; 0% instances).
