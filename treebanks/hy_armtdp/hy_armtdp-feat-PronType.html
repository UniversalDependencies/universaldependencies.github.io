Treebank Statistics: UD_Armenian-ArmTDP: Features: PronType

This feature is universal but the values Emp, Exc are language-specific. It occurs with 11 different values: Art, Dem, Emp, Exc, Ind, Int, Neg, Prs, Rcp, Rel, Tot.

7269 tokens (7%) have a non-empty value of PronType. 369 types (2%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. 118 lemmas (1%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: DET (3253; 3% instances), PRON (3227; 3% instances), ADV (789; 1% instances).
