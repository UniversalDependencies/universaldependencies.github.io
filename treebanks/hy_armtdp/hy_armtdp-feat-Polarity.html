Treebank Statistics: UD_Armenian-ArmTDP: Features: Polarity

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Neg, Pos.

1756 tokens (14%) have a non-empty value of Polarity. 744 types (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Polarity. 400 lemmas (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Polarity. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (914; 8% instances), AUX (828; 7% instances), PART (14; 0% instances).
