Treebank Statistics: UD_Armenian-ArmTDP: Features: Aspect

This feature is universal but the values Dur, Iter, Prosp are language-specific. It occurs with 6 different values: Dur, Imp, Iter, Perf, Prog, Prosp. Some words have combined values of the feature; 6 combinations have been observed: Dur|Imp, Dur|Iter, Dur|Perf, Imp|Iter, Iter|Perf, Iter|Prosp.

15912 tokens (15%) have a non-empty value of Aspect. 4107 types (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. 1441 lemmas (12%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (9634; 9% instances), AUX (6278; 6% instances).
