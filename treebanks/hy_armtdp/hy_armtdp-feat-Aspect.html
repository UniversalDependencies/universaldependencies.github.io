Treebank Statistics: UD_Armenian-ArmTDP: Features: Aspect

This feature is universal but the values Dur, Iter, Prosp are language-specific. It occurs with 6 different values: Dur, Imp, Iter, Perf, Prog, Prosp. Some words have combined values of the feature; 3 combinations have been observed: Dur|Iter, Imp|Iter, Iter|Perf.

6278 tokens (17%) have a non-empty value of Aspect. 2086 types (20%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. 862 lemmas (15%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Aspect. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (3782; 10% instances), AUX (2496; 7% instances).
