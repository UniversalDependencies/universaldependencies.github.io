Treebank Statistics: UD_Slovak-SNK: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Cnd, Imp, Ind.

6687 tokens (6%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 1936 types (7%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 1038 lemmas (7%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood.
