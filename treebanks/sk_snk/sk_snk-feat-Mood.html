Treebank Statistics: UD_Slovak-SNK: Features: Mood

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Cnd, Imp, Ind.

6687 tokens (6%) have a non-empty value of Mood. 1929 types (7%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. 1034 lemmas (7%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Mood. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (3478; 3% instances), AUX (3209; 3% instances).
