Treebank Statistics: UD_Latin-LLCT: Features: Tense

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Past, Pqp, Pres.

32705 tokens (13%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 2348 types (25%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 469 lemmas (13%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: VERB (29843; 12% instances), AUX (2862; 1% instances).
