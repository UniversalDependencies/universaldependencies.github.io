Treebank Statistics: UD_Greek-Cretan: Features: PronType

This feature is universal but the values Emp are language-specific. It occurs with 9 different values: Art, Dem, Emp, Ind, Int, Neg, Prs, Rel, Tot.

1099 tokens (25%) have a non-empty value of PronType. 119 types (9%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType. 50 lemmas (5%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of PronType.
