Treebank Statistics: UD_Greek-Cretan: Features: Case

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Acc, Gen, Nom, Voc.

1958 tokens (45%) have a non-empty value of Case. 764 types (55%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case. 592 lemmas (59%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Case.
