flat:abs: clausal absolutive

Consider the following example.

1	They	_	_	_	_	0	root	_	_

Here, одне одному “one another” is acting as a single unit — an indirect object of the main predicate (like German einander) while having a predication on its own (one told another — notice that one even has a nominative case signifying a subject). A basic tree is unable to capture such secondary structure, therefore we use flat:abs here.

This phenomenon is not limited to pure reciprocals:
