nsubj:pred: subject of a secondary predicate (enhanced dependency)

nsubj:pred denotes a subject in a secondary predication and is always headed by either xcomp:pred or advcl:pred. nsubj:pred is an Enhanced dependency. It is not a part of basic syntax tree.
