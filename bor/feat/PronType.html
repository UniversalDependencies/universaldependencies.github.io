PronType

Art: Art

Art (article) marks the identifiability of the referents of the noun phrases.

Example

  • ia arigao   “A dog”

Bi: Bi

Bi (bound index) refers to a set of person index which is neither an agreement index nor an independent person index. It combines with tense, illocutionary force markers.

Example

Prs: Prs

Prs (personal) refers to an independent person index.
