NOUN: noun

Nouns inflect for case, number and possession. Nouns receive nominal morphology. Other parts of speech may be derived into nouns, such as adjectives.

Proper nouns are not annotated as NOUN but rather PROPN.

Examples

  • [kk] қыз “girl”
  • [kk] үй “house”
  • [kk] ағаш “tree”
