CCONJ: coordinating conjunction

Definition

A coordinating conjunction is a word that links words or larger constituents without syntactically subordinating one to the other and expresses a semantic relationship between them.
The following words are annotated as coordinated conjunctions CONJ in Estonian UD: aga “but”, ega “nor”, ehk “or”, elik “or”, ent “but”, ja “and”, kui “if, when”, kuid “but”, kuni “until”, ning “and”, vaid “but” või “or”. For subordinating conjunctions, see u-dep/sconj.
