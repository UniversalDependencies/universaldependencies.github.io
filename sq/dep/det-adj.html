det:adj: article of a prearticulated adjective

Certain Albanian adjectives must be accompanied by the article i/e/të/së and some authors call them prearticulated adjectives. We use the relation det:adj to attach the article to the adjective.

kultivimi i mendimeve të bukura \n cultivation of thoughts beautiful
det:adj(bukura, të)
