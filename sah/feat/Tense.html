Pres: Present

The present tense form of the third person singular is formed with the help of the affix -ар, which yields four variants of the vowel depending on the final consonant of the stem: -ар, -эр, -ор, -өр.

Examples

  • тохтуубун  “I stop”
  • көрөр  “He sees”
  • олороллор  “They sit”

Fut: Future

The future tense is formed with the help of the affix -ыах, to which the possessive affixes are then added.

Examples

  • барыаҕым  “I will go”
  • барыаҕыҥ  “You will go”
  • барыахтара  “They will go”

</tr> </table>

NearPast: Near past (categorical past)

The affixex -т, -д, -л, -н are added to the verb stems, which are followed by the personal or plural markers.

Examples

  • үлэлээтим  “I worked (recently)”
  • аһаата  “He ate (recently)”

The plural is used with the help of a complex suffix, to which the plural suffix -лар is added.

  • оонньотулар  “They played (recently)”

</tr> </table>

PastPart: Past Participle

The past participle is formed from the stem of the verb by the affix -быт, -пыт, -мыт .

Examples

  • ууга түспүтүм  “I fell into water”
  • түспүт хаар  “Fallen snow”
