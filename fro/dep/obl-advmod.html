obl:advmod: adverbial modifier confusable with an oblique dependent

The double labelling accounts for the difficulty to decide between obl and advmod relations (en and i).

TO DO: The two relations, obl and advmod, are actually functionally identical, but the former is used for nominals. The double label should thus be dissolved. If the UPOS tag is PRON, the relation should be obl. If the UPOS tag is ADV, the relation should be advmod. Most of the occurrences are currently tagged ADV.

Il en apelet e ses dux e ses cuntes
nsubj(apelet, Il)
obl:advmod(apelet, en)
obj(apelet, dux)
cc(dux, e-4)
det(dux, ses-5)
conj(dux, cuntes)
cc(cuntes, e-7)
det(cuntes, ses-8)
