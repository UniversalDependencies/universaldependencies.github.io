TokenType: TokenType

Values: Aff Clit Stem Unfinished

The morphological tokenization of the treebank involves “non-standard” units. We use this feature to identify the type of units.

Aff: affix

Token that starts or ends with a dash occupying the positions of pronoun, particle, subordinating conjunction or other, depending on the syntactic function.

Examples

  • -ja (PRON; TAM; Person Number Gender)

Clit: clitic

Token that starts or ends with a equal sign occupying the positions of pronoun, determiner, subordinating conjunction, coordinating conjunction or adposition, depending on the syntactic function

Examples

  • =i (PRON; Case Nominative; Possessive)

Stem: stem

Stem item within the word.

Examples

  • kʷiɖ “disappear”

Unfinished: unfinished

Token which is unfinished like false starts, marked by the hash sign.

Examples

  • aː# (False start DET)
