NumType: numeral type

Values: Ord

Ord: ordinal number or corresponding interrogative / relative / indefinite / demonstrative word

Used to distinguish numbers from the cardinal numerals which are ordinals stem with an added suffix.

Examples

  • areː mali -a doːr ingiːdhoːm / “Then, when he throws it a second(two + suffix) time”
