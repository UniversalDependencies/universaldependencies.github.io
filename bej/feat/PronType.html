PronType: pronominal type

Values: Dem Int Rel

This feature typically applies to pronouns, adverbs, and relative pronoun SCONJ (relativizer). .

Int: interrogative pronoun, adverb

Used to specify the interrogative quality of a pronouns or pronominal adverbs.

Examples:

  • kak “how”

Rel: relative pronoun, subordinating conjunctions

Subordinating conjunctions holding the position of relativizer are describe by this feature.

Examples:

  • bak tʔi -it =eːt ʔiːbaːb / dh =eː ti- ki “a story like that happened to me”

Dem: demonstrative determiner

Used to show the thing or person being referred to.

Examples

  • _uːn uː= tak_ “this man”
