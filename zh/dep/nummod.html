nummod: numeric modifier

A numeric modifier is a modifier that is a numeral word or phrase preceding the noun or noun phrase it modifies. Ordinal numbers such as 第一 / dìyī “first” and 第二 / dìèr “second”, however, should be treated with amod (and tagged ADJ) to conform to UD guidelines.

Numeric modifiers are always attached to the head of the noun phrase.
