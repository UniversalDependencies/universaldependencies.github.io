PUNCT: punctuation

Definition

Punctuation marks are character groups used to delimit linguistic units in printed text.

Punctuation is not taken to include logograms such as $, %, and §, which are instead tagged as SYM.

Examples

  • Period: 。
  • Comma: ， 、
  • Quotation marks: ‘ ’ “ ”「 」 『 』
  • Title marks: 《 》
