PartType: particle type

Values: Gen Inf Neg

As there are different types of particles (PART) in Afrikaans, we indicate which type they are.

Gen: genitival se

The particle se introducing an genitive. Actually the possessor is following this particle, while the possessed item is preceding it.

Examples

  • Hierdie en ander programme, insluitend die Apex-Prioriteite wat in verlede Februarie se Staatsrede geïdentifiseer is, vorm die basis van ons pogings om aan ons mandaat te voldoen en die grondwerk vir die toekoms te doen. “These and other programmes, including the Apex Priorities which have been identified in the State of the Nation of last February, are the base of our attemps to fulfil our mandate and do the groundwork for the future.”

Inf: infinitival te

The particle te introducing an infinitive.

Examples

  • _En ons hoop hierdie keer dat hernude pogings deur die internasionale gemeenskap om blywende oplossings vir hierdie konflik te vind, vrugte sal afwerp sodat die Israeliete en die Palestyne as bure vrede en sekuriteit kan geniet binne hul soewereine gebiede.- “And we hope this time that the renewed attempts by the international community to find lasting solutions for this conflict, will bear fruit in order for the Israelis and Palestinians to enjoy peace and security within their sovereign territories.”

Neg: negative particle

The negative particle nie used in double negations.

Examples

  • En dus, in die woorde van voormalige President Mandela, “durf ons nie talm nie, want ons lang pad is nog nie verby nie”. “And therefore, in the words of former President Mandela, “we don’t dare to delay, as our long path is not over yet”.”
