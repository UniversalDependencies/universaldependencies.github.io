flat: flat

We use flat for multiword placenames, two-word borrowings and miscellaneous expressions like “BBC 2” and “500 AD”. We use the language specific labels flat:name for personal names and flat:foreign for foreign-language titles, quotations, appositive explanations and longer switches into foreign languages.
