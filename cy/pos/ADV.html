ADV: adverb

Description

Adverbs typically modify verbs to indicate time, place, location or manner. In Welsh, adverbs of manner, most of which tend to correspond to the English -ly adverbs (effectively, carefully), are constructed through using the predicative particle yn in conjunction with an adjective.

Examples

  • allan “out”
  • hefyd “also”
  • yn araf “slowly”
