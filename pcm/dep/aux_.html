The auxiliaries of Naija include :

  • come
  • dey
  • don
  • fit
  • for
  • go
  • make
  • neva

Some of these auxiliaries can also be used as the main verb of a clause:

  • come
  • dey
  • fit
  • go
  • make

See more about the auxiliaries in the AUX page.
