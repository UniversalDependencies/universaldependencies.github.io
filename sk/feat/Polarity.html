Treebank Statistics (UD_Slovak)

This feature is language-specific. It occurs with 2 different values: Neg, Pos.

17095 tokens (18%) have a non-empty value of Polarity. 7281 types (32%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Polarity. 3103 lemmas (24%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Polarity. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: sk-pos/VERB (12871; 14% instances), sk-pos/AUX (3240; 3% instances), sk-pos/ADJ (984; 1% instances).
