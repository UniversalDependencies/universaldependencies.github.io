advmod:tmod: advmod with temporal modality

The advmod:tmod relation is used for adverbal modifiers with temporal modality.

А касан пяк савор, нинге школавга аф якан. \n But I’m growing so slowly, I don’t even go to school yet.
advmod:tmod(якан, нинге)
advmod:tmod in other languages: [apu] [gsw] [it] [ka] [koi] [kpv] [la] [mdf] [myv] [ro] [sms]
