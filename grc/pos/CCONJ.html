CCONJ: coordinating conjunction

Definition

Coordinating conjunctions are invariable words that connect two constituents without subordination. In Ancient Greek some conjunctions can also function as adverbs.

Examples

  • καί “and”
  • τε “and”
  • ἀλλά “but”
