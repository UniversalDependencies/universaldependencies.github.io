PossPerson: possessor’s person

Values: 1 2 3

PossPerson is possessor’s person, marked e.g. on nouns, emphatic pronouns/determiners, infinitives, or adpositions. These forms would be translated to English as possessive pronoun + noun.

The PossPerson feature captures the possessor’s person. We do not distinguish PossPerson for possessive pronouns, as they are actually identical to personal pronouns in the genitive case.

Nothe that in traditional Armenian grammars besides the definite article, possessive, demonstrative and personal articles (suffixes) are distinguished. These make a distinction between the objects, that are near to the speaker (proximal, first person), near to the addressee (second person), and far from both (third person) or are possessed to them. We do not distinguish them as separate features.

See also the relatived feature PossNumber.

1: first person possessor

Examples

  • տունս “my hous” PossPerson=1|PossNumber=Sing|Number=Sing
  • տներս “my houses” PossPerson=1|PossNumber=Sing|Number=Plur
  • գալս “my coming” PossPerson=1|PossNumber=Sing
  • օրերս “in this days” PossPerson=1|Number=Plur
  • քաղաքս “this town” PossPerson=1|Number=Sing
  • (ես) ուսուցիչս “I the teacher” or “my teacher” PossPerson=1|Number=Sing or PossPerson=1|PossNumber=Sing|Number=Sing
  • ինքս “I myself” PossPerson=1|PossNumber=Sing|Number=Sing
  • հետս “with me” PossPerson=1|PossNumber=Sing

2: second person possessor

Examples

  • տունդ “your.Sing hous” PossPerson=2|PossNumber=Sing|Number=Sing
  • տներդ “your.Sing houses” PossPerson=2|PossNumber=Sing|Number=Plur
  • գալդ “your.Sing coming” PossPerson=2|PossNumber=Sing
  • (դու) ուսուցիչդ “You.Sing the teacher” or “your.Sing teacher” PossPerson=2|Number=Sing օր PossPerson=2|PossNumber=Sing|Number=Sing
  • ինքդ “you.Sing yourself” PossPerson=2|PossNumber=Sing|Number=Sing
  • հետդ “with you.Sing” PossPerson=2|PossNumber=Sing

3: third person possessor

Examples

  • տունն(ը) “his/her/its hous” PossPerson=3|PossNumber=Sing|Number=Sing
  • տներն(ը) “his/her/its houses” PossPerson=3|PossNumber=Sing|Number=Plur
  • (նա) ուսուցիչն(ը) “He/She the teacher” or “his/her/its teacher” PossPerson=3|Number=Sing or PossPerson=3|PossNumber=Sing|Number=Sing
  • գալն(ը) “his/her/its coming” PossPerson=3|PossNumber=Sing
  • ինքն(ը) “he/she/is himself/herself/itself” PossPerson=3|PossNumber=Sing|Number=Sing
  • հետն(ը) “with him/her/it” PossPerson=3|PossNumber=Sing

(Note that first three forms are ambiguous.)
