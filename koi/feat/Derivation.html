Derivation: derivation

(Please note: this part of the documentation is not yet completed.)

Language-specific feature identifying the form of derivation applied to a word.

An: derivation with suffix -ан

Produces adnominal deverbal noun modifier / noun.

Examples

  • [koi] кутан “holding”
  • [koi] сёян “food”
  • [koi] чужан “birth”

Ana: derivation with suffix -ана

Produces adnominal deverbal adjective modifier / adverb.

Examples

  • [koi] кутана “holding”

CompMod: derivation with suffix -кодь

Produces meaning like added to adjectival heads.

Examples

  • [koi] паськыдкодь “rather extensive”

Dimin: derivation with suffix -ок

Produces diminutive derivations of nouns.

Examples

  • [koi] польӧсок “a small corner”

Ik: derivation with suffix -ик/-ік

Produces converb.

Examples

  • [koi] лӧсьӧтікӧ “while preparing”

Ko: derivation with suffix -кӧ

Produces indefinite from interrogative.

Examples

  • [koi] кодкӧ “somebody”
  • [koi] кыдзкӧ “somehow”
  • [koi] кутшӧмкӧ “some kind of”

LocMod: derivation with suffix -са

Produces meaning in added to adjectival heads adnominal phrases, contrast Inessive case. Added to both singular and plural stems.

Examples

  • [koi] карса олысьяс “urban dwellers”

NomAg: derivation with suffix -ысь

Produces nomen agensis (non-temporal).

Examples

  • [koi] сулалысь “one standing”

Omon: derivation with suffix -ӧмӧн

Produces converb.

Examples

  • [koi] кутӧмӧн “holding”

PrivMod: derivation with suffix -тӧм

Produces adnominal modifiers (without an X).

Examples

  • [koi] кепысьтӧм “without a glove”

ProprietiveMod: derivation with suffix -а/-я

Produces adverbs and adjectives (having an X).

Examples

  • [koi] кепыся “with a glove”

TempMod: derivation with suffix -ся

Produces adnominal modifiers (in the time of X).

Examples

  • [koi] июль тӧлысся “from the month of June”

VAbe: derivation with suffix -тӧг

Produces converbs.

Examples

  • [koi] повтӧг “without fearing”

VTer: derivation with suffix -тӧдз

Produces converbs.

Examples

  • [koi] петтӧдз “until it comes out”
