cc: coordinating conjunction

A coordination is the relation between an element of a conjunct and the coordinating conjunc- tion word of the conjunct. (Note: diﬀerent dependency grammars have diﬀerent treatments of coordination. We take one conjunct of a conjunction (normally the ﬁrst) as the head of the conjunction.)

Lan cao và xinh_xắn 。
nsubj(cao, Lan)
cc(cao, và)
conj(cao, xinh_xắn)
punct(cao, 。)
