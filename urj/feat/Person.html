Person: person

Description

Person is typically a feature of personal and possessive pronouns / determiners, and of verbs. On verbs it is in fact an agreement feature that marks the person of the verb’s subject. Person is further used as a layered feature for possessives, for further informations, see Person[psor] and Person[psed].

The fourth person of some Uralic grammars is Person=Impers.
