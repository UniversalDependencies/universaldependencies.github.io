Reln: relation type

This feature marks the relation type of a relational marker

Invol: Involitional

Examples

  • [ab] и-с-амха-фе-ит [Subj.3-PO.1Sg-Invol-eat-Aor] ‘I ate it inadvertently’ Dyn=Yes|Number[po]=Sing|Person[po]=1|Person[subj]=3|Reln=Invol|Tense=Aor|Trans=Yes|VerbForm=Fin

Pot: Potential

Examples

  • [ab] з-з-еил-мы-р-го.з [PO.Rel-Pot-Pv-Neg-Caus-take.Pres.NonFin] ‘who could not solve it’ Dyn=Yes|Person[lo]=Rec|Person[po]=Rel|Person[subj]=3|Polarity=Neg|Reln=Pot|Tense=Imp|Trans=No|VerbForm=NonFin|Voice=Cau

Ben: Benefactive

Examples

  • [ab] (и)-еи-зы-раз-ны [Subj.3-RO:Rec-For-agree-Cnv] ‘agreeing with each other’ Number=Sing|Person[ro]=Rec|Person[subj]=3|Reln=Ben|Tense=Pres|VerbForm=NonFin

Ins: Instrumental

Examples

  • [ab] шәы-з-л-еиҕь-у [Subj.2Pl-RO.Rel-With-better-Pres.NonFin] ‘who is better than us’ Number=Sing|Number[subj]=Plur|Person[ro]=Rel|Person[subj]=2|Reln=Ins|Tense=Pres|VerbForm=NonFin

Com: Comitative

Examples

  • [ab] с-у-ц-цо.ит [Subj.1Sg-RO.2SgM-Com-go.Pres] ‘’ Dyn=Yes|Gender[ro]=Masc|Number[ro]=Sing|Number[subj]=Sing|Person[ro]=2|Person[subj]=1|Reln=Com|Tense=Pres|Trans=No|VerbForm=Fin

Mal: Malefactive

Examples

  • [ab] и-ш-з-а-цәы-м-цо-з [Subj.3-How-Pot-RO.3SgNH-Against-Neg-go-Aor.NonFin] ‘how it/they could not go against it’ Dyn=Yes|Gender[ro]=Neut|Number[ro]=Sing|Person[ro]=3|Person[subj]=3|Polarity=Neg|Reln=Mal|RelType=Mnr|Tense=Imp|Trans=No|VerbForm=NonFin
