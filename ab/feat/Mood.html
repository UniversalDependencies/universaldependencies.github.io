Mood: mood

The feature marks the mood of the verb.

Imp: Imperative

Examples

  • [ab] у-аан-гыл(-ø) [Subj.2SgM-stop-Impv] ‘stop!’ Dyn=Yes|Gender[subj]=Masc|Mood=Imp|Number[subj]=Sing|Person[subj]=2|Trans=No|VerbForm=Fin

  • [ab] и-ҭ-га(-ø) [DO.3-raise(-Impv)] ‘raise it! (e.g. your voice)’ Dyn=Yes|Gender[lo]=Neut|Mood=Imp|Number[lo]=Sing|Person[lo]=3|Person[obj]=3|Trans=Yes|VerbForm=Fin

Prp: Purposive

Examples

  • [ab] (ø-)м-га-рц [Subj.3-Neg-rise-Purp] ‘in order not to rise’ Dyn=Yes|Mood=Prp|Person[subj]=3|Polarity=Neg|Trans=No|VerbForm=NonFin

Int: Interrogative

Examples

  • [ab] и-у-м-бо-и [DO.3-Subj.2SgM-see-Quest] ‘don’t you see it?’ Dyn=Yes|Gender[subj]=Masc|Mood=Int|Number[subj]=Sing|Person[obj]=3|Person[subj]=2|Polarity=Neg|Tense=Pres|Trans=Yes|VerbForm=NonFin

Sub: Subjunctive

Examples

  • [ab] (ø-)-б-ба-(а)аит [DO.3-Subj.2SgM-see-Sub] ‘you.Fem should see it’ Dyn=Yes|Gender[subj]=Masc|Mood=Sub|Number[subj]=Sing|Person[obj]=3|Person[subj]=2|Tense=Aor|Trans=Yes|VerbForm=Fin

Opt: Optative

Examples

  • [ab] д-хын.ҳәы-ндаз [Subj.3SgH-Pv.return-Opt] ‘may (s)he return’ Dyn=Yes|Gender[subj]=Com|Mood=Opt|Number[subj]=Sing|Person[subj]=3|Tense=Aor|Trans=No|VerbForm=NonFin

Cnd: Conditional 1

The Conditional 1 is used to form real or unreal conditions. It is different from the Conditional 1 Tense.

Examples

  • [ab] сы-ҟа-м-зар [Subj.1Sg-exist-Neg-Cnd] ‘if I would not exist’ Dyn=No|Mood=Cnd|Number[subj]=Sing|Person[subj]=1|Polarity=Neg|Tense=Pres|VerbForm=NonFin

Nec: Necessitative

Examples

  • [ab] и-ҳ-а-у-ҳәа-р.оуп [Obj.3-LO.1Pl-Pv-Subj.2SgM-tell-Nec] ‘you have to tell it to us’ Dyn=Yes|Gender[subj]=Masc|Mood=Nec|Number[lo]=Plur|Number[subj]=Sing|Person[lo]=1|Person[obj]=3|Person[subj]=2|Trans=Yes|VerbForm=Fin

Cnd2: Conditional 2

The Conditional 2 is used to form real or unreal conditions. It is different from the Conditional 2 Tense.

Examples

  • [ab] у-ҧш-уазҭ.гьы [Subj.2SgM-look-Cnd2] ‘(even) if you look’ Dyn=Yes|Gender[subj]=Masc|Mood=Cnd2|Number[subj]=Sing|Person[subj]=2|Tense=Pres|Trans=No|VerbForm=NonFin
