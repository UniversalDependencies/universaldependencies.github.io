Mood: mood

The feature marks the mood of the verb.

Imp: Imperative

Examples

  • [ab] з-къвырман-у [REL.PR-sacrifice-PRS.NFIN] ‘whose sacrifice is …’ RelType=Pr|Tense=Pres|VerbForm=NonFin

  • [ab] р-чпа-уа [3Pl.ERG-do-IPF] ‘they are making’ Number[erg]=Plur|Person[erg]=3|Tense=Pres|VerbForm=NonFin

Prp: Purposive

Examples

  • [ab] и-с-фа-п [3.OBJ-1SG.SUBJ-eat-FUT1] ‘I will eat it’ Tense=Fut1

Int: Interrogative

Examples

  • [ab] и-с-фа-п [3.OBJ-1SG.SUBJ-eat-FUT1] ‘I will eat it’ Tense=Fut1

Sub: Subjunctive

Examples

  • [ab] и-р-фа-ша [3.OBJ-3Pl.SUBJ-eat-FUT2] ‘they will eat it’ Tense=Fut2

Opt: Optative

Examples

  • [ab] с-ца-н [1Sg.SUBJ-go-PAST] ‘they will eat it’ Tense=Past

Cnd1: Conditional I

Examples

  • [ab] иҳәеит [3.OBJ-3Pl.SUBJ-eat-FUT2] ‘they will eat it’ Tense=Aor

Cnd2: Conditional II

Examples

  • [ab] иҟалаз [3.OBJ-3Pl.SUBJ-eat-FUT2] ‘they will eat it’ Tense=Imp

Conj1: Conjunctive I

Examples

  • [ab] саҳахьан [3.OBJ-3Pl.SUBJ-eat-FUT2] ‘they will eat it’ Tense=Pqp

Nec: Necessitative

Examples

  • [ab] и-ҳ-а-у-ҳәа-р.оуп [Obj.3-LO.1Pl-Pv-Subj.2SgM-tell-Necess] ‘you have to tell it to us’ Dyn=Yes|Gender[subj]=Masc|Mood=Nec|Number[lo]=Plur|Number[subj]=Sing|Person[lo]=1|Person[obj]=3|Person[subj]=2|Trans=Yes|VerbForm=Fin

Conj2: Conjunctive II

Examples

  • [ab] саҳахьан [3.OBJ-3Pl.SUBJ-eat-FUT2] ‘they will eat it’ Tense=Pqp
