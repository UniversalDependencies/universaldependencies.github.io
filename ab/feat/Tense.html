Tense: tense

The feature marks the tense of the verb.

Pres: Present

Examples

  • [ab] с-у-фо-ма [DO.1Sg-Subj.2SgM-eat-Pres.NonFin.Q] ‘do you eat me?’ Dyn=Yes|Gender[subj]=Masc|Mood=Int|Number[obj]=Sing|Number[subj]=Sing|Person[obj]=1|Person[subj]=2|Tense=Pres|Trans=Yes|VerbForm=NonFin

Fut: Future 1

Examples

  • [ab] и-с-фа-п [DO.3-Subj.1Sg-eat-Fut1] ‘I will eat it’ Dyn=Yes|Number[subj]=Sing|Person[obj]=3|Person[subj]=1|Tense=Fut|Trans=Yes|VerbForm=Fin

Fut2: Future 2

Examples

  • [ab] и-р-фа-ша [DO.Rel-Subj.3Pl-eat-Fut2.NonFin] ‘what they are going to eat’ Dyn=Yes|Number[subj]=Plur|Person[obj]=Rel|Person[subj]=3|Tense=Fut2|Trans=Yes|VerbForm=NonFin

Cnd: Conditional 1

Past equivalent to Future 1. The Cnd is used in the apodosis for contrary-to-fact condictions.

Examples

  • [ab] и-з-л-и-х-рыз [DO.3-LO.Rel-Pv-Subj.3SgM-take.out-Cnd1.NonFin] ‘which he would take out’ Dyn=Yes|Gender[subj]=Masc|Tense=Cnd|Number[subj]=Sing|Person[lo]=Rel|Person[obj]=3|Person[subj]=3|Tense=Cnd|Trans=Yes|VerbForm=NonFin

Cnd2: Conditional 2

Past equivalent to Future 2.

Examples

  • [ab] д-ца-ша.м.ызт [Subj.3SgH-go-Neg.Cnd2] ‘(s)he would not have gone’ Dyn=Yes|Gender[subj]=Com|Number[subj]=Sing|Person[subj]=3|Polarity=Neg|Tense=Cnd2|Trans=No|VerbForm=Fin

Past: Past of static verbs or Past Indefinite of dynamic verbs

Examples

Dyn=Yes Mood=Sub Person[subj]=3 Tense=Past Trans=No VerbForm=Fin
  • [ab] с-ца-н [Subj.1Sg-go-Past] ‘I went and …’ Dyn=Yes|Number[subj]=Sing|Person[subj]=1|Tense=Past|Trans=No|VerbForm=Fin

Aor: Aorist

Examples

  • [ab] и-л-ҳәе-ит [DO.3-Subj.3SgF-say-Aor] ‘she said it’ Dyn=Yes|Gender[subj]=Fem|Number[subj]=Sing|Person[obj]=3|Person[subj]=3|Tense=Aor|Trans=Yes|VerbForm=Fin

Imp: Imperfect

Examples

  • [ab] с-цо-н [Subj.1Sg-go-Imp] ‘I was going’ Dyn=Yes|Number[subj]=Sing|Person[subj]=1|Tense=Imp|Trans=No|VerbForm=Fin

Examples
