Tense: tense

The feature marks the tense of the verb.

Pres: Present

Examples

  • [ab] з-къвырман-у [REL.PR-sacrifice-PRS.NFIN] ‘whose sacrifice is …’ RelType=Pr|Tense=Pres|VerbForm=NonFin

  • [ab] р-чпа-уа [3Pl.ERG-do-IPF] ‘they are making’ Number[erg]=Plur|Person[erg]=3|Tense=Pres|VerbForm=NonFin

Fut: Future 1

Examples

  • [ab] и-с-фа-п [3.OBJ-1SG.SUBJ-eat-FUT1] ‘I will eat it’ Tense=Fut1

Fut2: Future 2

Examples

  • [ab] и-р-фа-ша [3.OBJ-3Pl.SUBJ-eat-FUT2] ‘they will eat it’ Tense=Fut2

Past: Past of static verbs or Past Indefinite of dynamic verbs

Examples

  • [ab] с-ца-н [1Sg.SUBJ-go-PAST] ‘they will eat it’ Tense=Past

Aor: Aorist

Examples

  • [ab] иҳәеит [3.OBJ-3Pl.SUBJ-eat-FUT2] ‘they will eat it’ Tense=Aor

Imp: Imperfect

Examples

  • [ab] иҟалаз [3.OBJ-3Pl.SUBJ-eat-FUT2] ‘they will eat it’ Tense=Imp

Prf: Perfect

Examples

  • [ab] с-аҳа-хье.ит [3.OBJ-3Pl.SUBJ-eat-FUT2] ‘I have heard it’ Dyn=Yes|Gender[subj]=Neut|Number[obj]=Sing|Number[subj]=Sing|Person[obj]=1|Person[subj]=3|Tense=Prf|Trans=Yes|VerbForm=Fin

Pqp: Plusquamperfect

Examples

  • [ab] с-аҳа-хьа.н [3.OBJ-3Pl.SUBJ-eat-FUT2] ‘I had heard it’ Dyn=Yes|Gender[subj]=Neut|Number[obj]=Sing|Number[subj]=Sing|Person[obj]=1|Person[subj]=3|Tense=Pqp|Trans=Yes|VerbForm=Fin
