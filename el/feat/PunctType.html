PunctType: punctuation type

Values: Brck Colo Comm Dash Elip Excl Peri Qest Quot Semi Slsh

Many tagsets have just one tag for punctuation. Others classify punctuation in more detail.

Peri: period at the end of sentence or clause

Examples

  • Γιατί?, διερωτήθηκε. “Why?, she wondered.”

Elip: ellipsis

Examples

  • Δεν ξέρω … “I don’t know …”

Qest: question mark

Examples

  • Γιατί? “Why?”

Excl: exclamation mark

Examples

  • Πρόσεχε! “Watch out!”

Quot: quotation marks (various sorts in various languages)

Examples

  • Είναι, λέει, ”σκεπτική” “She is, she says, “concerned””

Brck: bracket

Examples

  • Δημόσια Επιχείρηση Ηλεκτρισμού (ΔΕΗ) “Public Electricity Company (PEC)”

Comm: comma

Examples

  • Είναι, λέει, “σκεπτική” “She is, she says, “concerned””

Colo: colon

Examples

  • Οι εξής: “The following:”

Dash: dash, hyphen

Examples

  • άτομα ηλικίας 25-45 ετών “individuals with age between 25 and 45 years”

Slsh: slash or backslash

Examples

  • Αθήνα/Αττική “Athens/Attica”
PunctType in other languages: [el] [es] [ka] [u] [uk]
