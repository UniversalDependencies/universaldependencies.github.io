Values: Pos Neg

Modern Greek response interjection όχι / ohi “no/not” is assigned the feature-value pair Polarity=Neg. When it is used like the English particle not, e.g., φέρε τον αναπτήρα, όχι τα σπίρτα / fere ton anaptira, ohi ta spirta “fetch the lighter, not the matches”, όχι” / *ohi “no/not” is assigned the tags PART and Polarity=Neg.

Pos: positive, affirmative

Examples

  • ναι “yes”

Neg: negative

Examples

  • όχι “no/not”
  • δεν “no/not”
  • μη “no/not/non”
