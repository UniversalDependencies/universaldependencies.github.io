Stat: stative

The stative can be analysed as a verbal noun which expresses states and is not tense bound; it is built by a verbal adjective and a suffix.

Examples

  • bēdāk “I spend/spent/will spend the night”
  • bēde “he spends/spent/will spend the night”
  • dān “he/it is/was/will be strong”
  • ṣabtat “she/it is/was/will be taken”
  • šaknū “they are/were/will be placed”
