Tense: tense

Tense should only occur on verbs in French, with one of the four following values: Past, Pres, Fut or Imp.

Treebank Statistics (UD_French)

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Imp, Past, Pres.

37894 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 6941 types (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 1998 lemmas (6%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: fr-pos/VERB (25005; 6% instances), fr-pos/AUX (12886; 3% instances), fr-pos/NOUN (3; 0% instances).

VERB

25005 fr-pos/VERB tokens (82% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Tense.

The most frequent other feature values with which VERB and Tense co-occurred: Number=Sing (18751; 75%), Gender=EMPTY (14092; 56%), VerbForm=Fin (12563; 50%).

VERB tokens may have the following values of Tense:

  • Fut (568; 2% of non-empty Tense): fera, aura, sera, feront, permettra, restera, deviendra, permettront, verra, faudra
  • Imp (985; 4% of non-empty Tense): avait, était, comptait, faisait, avaient, portait, étaient, disait, existait, comprenait
  • Past (12001; 48% of non-empty Tense): né, située, fait, situé, eu, mort, connu, nommé, née, réalisé
  • Pres (11451; 46% of non-empty Tense): a, fait, est, trouve, devient, ont, permet, agit, reste, prend
  • EMPTY (5511): faire, partir, voir, prendre, avoir, passer, mettre, trouver, donner, devenir
