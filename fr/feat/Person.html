Person: person

Person is marked for verbs and pronouns in French, as first (1), second (2), or third (3) person.

Treebank Statistics (UD_French)

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: 1, 2, 3.

37556 tokens (9%) have a non-empty value of Person. 3513 types (8%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Person. 1374 lemmas (4%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Person. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: fr-pos/VERB (17457; 4% instances), fr-pos/PRON (12572; 3% instances), fr-pos/AUX (7527; 2% instances).
