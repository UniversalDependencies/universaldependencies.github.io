Variant: non-standard morphological form

Values: Long Short

Long: long form

Variant=Long distinguishes long forms of infinitives and negative imperatives in nonstandard Romanian.

Examples

  • nu vă teamereți de ceia ce omoară trupul “do not fear those that kill the body” (old long form)
  • nu vă temeți de cei ce omoară trupul “do not fear those that kill the body” (newer form)

Short: short form

Clitics are often written in a contracted form, with some characters omitted, and attached via a hyphen to a neighboring word. Such form is annotated as Variant=Short. Note that this is not the same as Strength=Weak, although the two features interact.

Examples

  • sine “oneself” Case=Acc|Strength=Strong (strong form of the reflexive pronoun)
  • se “oneself” Case=Acc|Strength=Weak (weak but full form of the reflexive pronoun)
  • s-, -se, se- “oneself” Case=Acc|Strength=Weak|Variant=Short
