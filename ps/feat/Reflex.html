Reflex: reflexive

Values: Yes

Boolean feature of personal pronouns or determiners. It tells whether the word is reflexive, i.e. refers to the subject of its clause.

Yes: it is possessive

Note that there is no No value. If the word is not reflexive, the Reflex feature will just not be mentioned in the FEAT column. (Which means that empty value has the No meaning.)

These words are reflexive in Pashto:

  • personal pronoun ځان żân (indeclinable)
  • personal possessive determiner خپل xpël (declined like common adjectives in agreement with the possessed object)
