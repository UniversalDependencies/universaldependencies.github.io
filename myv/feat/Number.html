Number: number

Number is a feature of nouns, pronouns and numbers as well as of verbs and adjectives that mark agreement with them.

Sing: singular number

Examples

  • [myv] чи “day”
  • [myv] сон “he/she”
  • [myv] тёжа “thousand”
  • [myv] сёрмады “writes”
  • [myv] стака “difficult”

Plur: plural number

Examples

  • [myv] чить “days”
  • [myv] сынь “they”
  • [myv] тёжат “thousands”
  • [myv] сёрмадыть “write”
  • [myv] стакат “difficult”
