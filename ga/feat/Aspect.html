Aspect: aspect

Aspect is a feature that specifies duration of the action in time, whether the action has been completed etc.

Hab: habitual aspect

The action takes place habitually (daily, weekly, annually etc) or is a usual occurence

Examples

  • Bíonn an seoladh poist céanna ag na vótóirí uilig “Each voter (usually) has the same postal address”
  • Glas a bhíonn na caora ar dtús “The sheep are green at first”
  • Bíonn contúirt bhreise ag baint leis an iomarca meáchain “There are (can be) additional risks linked to overweight”

Imp: imperfect aspect

The action took / takes / will take some time span and there is no information whether and when it was / will be completed.

Examples

  • Chuiridís oilithrigh amach go dtí Compostela “They used to send pilgrims out to Compostela”
  • De ghnáth bhíodh an teach i Sráid Sheáin Mhic Dhiarmada go deas te “Usually, the house in Sean MacDermott St. used to be nice and warm”
  • Bhíodh colún rialta ag Máire dar theideal ‘An Fáinne’ ar an Irish Independent “Mary used to have a regular column in the Irish Independent called `The Ring’”
