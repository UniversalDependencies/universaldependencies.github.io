Degree: degree of comparison

Degree is relevant for adjectives and some adverbs in Hungarian.

Treebank Statistics (UD_Hungarian)

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Cmp, Pos, Sup.

3808 tokens (12%) have a non-empty value of Degree. 1742 types (16%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Degree. 1533 lemmas (22%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Degree. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: hu-pos/ADJ (3608; 11% instances), hu-pos/ADV (200; 1% instances).
