Tense: tense

This feature is used for verbs and auxiliaries, with the values Past and Pres.

Treebank Statistics (UD_Hungarian)

This feature is universal. It occurs with 2 different values: Past, Pres.

2635 tokens (8%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 1624 types (15%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 918 lemmas (13%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense.
