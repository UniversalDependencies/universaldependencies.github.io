Treebank Statistics (UD_Belarusian)

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fut, Past, Pres.

626 tokens (9%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 424 types (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 304 lemmas (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: be-pos/VERB (593; 9% instances), be-pos/AUX (33; 0% instances).
