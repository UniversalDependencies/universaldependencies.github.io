Tokenization

The low-level tokenization of the Belarusian UD treebank generally adopts the RNC standard.

  • In general, tokens are delimited by whitespace. The regexp [╨Ρ-z╨Ρ-╤Π╨Β╤Σ╨μ╤η╤Ψ-]+ usually corresponds to one token.
  • Punctuation (recognized by the corresponding Unicode property) that is conventionally written adjacent to the preceding or following word is separated during tokenization.
  • Each punctuation mark is treated as a single token, e.g. the following sequence: )έΑζ, - becomes four tokens, ) , έΑζ, ,, and -έΑε. Exceptions are conventional multi-character punctuation marks: έΑΥ , έΑο , ?! , etc., and emojis and smileys: :) , ^_^, etc.
  • Conventional non-cyrillic multi-character terms are tokenized as single tokens: ┬░╨κ, ╨║╨╝2.

Some special cases worth mentioning:

  • Numerical expressions including decimal numbers, such as 245, 3,14, are treated as single tokens.
  • Time expressions like 20:55 are splitted into separate tokens (in this case, three { 20 , : , 55 }).
  • Dates like 20.04.2012 are splitted into separate tokens (in this case, five { 20 , . , 04 , . , 2012 }).
  • Special symbols before and after numerical expressions, as in $500 , 2,67% , +27┬░╨κ , are tokenised separately (so, the tokens are { $ , 500 } , { 2,67 , % } , { + , 27 , ┬░╨κ }).
  • Numerical expressions with hyphen and cyrillic endings (e.g. 1-╤Μ╨╣ έΑε1stέΑζ, 3-╨╝ έΑε3rd.InsέΑζ) as well as adjectives and other non-numerals which contain digits (e.g. 79-╨│╨░╨┤╨╛╨▓╤Μ έΑε79 year oldέΑζ, 500-╨│╨╛╨┤╨┤╨╖╨╡ έΑε500th anniversaryέΑζ) are treated as single tokens.
  • Other words with hyphen are treated as single tokens, except for the cases then the first part is inflected. Examples: { ╨╖-╨╖╨░ } έΑεbecause ofέΑζ, { ╨╖╤Π╨╗╤Σ╨╜╨░-╤Ι╤Ξ╤Α╤Μ╤Ζ } έΑεgreen-grayέΑζ, { ╨κ╨░╨╜╨║╤Γ-╨θ╨╡╤Η╤Π╤Α╨▒╤Δ╤Α╨│ } έΑεSt. PetersburgέΑζ, but { ╨ι╨╛╤Β╤Γ╨╛╨▓╨╡ , - , ╨╜╨░ , - , ╨Φ╨╛╨╜╤Δ} έΑε(in) Rostov on DonέΑζ.
  • Abbreviations are treated as single tokens, whitespaces split the abbreviations.
  • Abbreviations marked by a period, as in ╤Β╤Γ╤Α. έΑεp. (page)έΑζ, ╨θ. έΑεP. (for Peter)έΑζ, are treated as single tokens. If the period overlaps with the end of sentence period then it is written once as a separate token (denoting end-of-sentence), e.g. { 1914 , ╨│ , . } έΑεyear 1914έΑζ.
  • Abbreviations can not contain a period inside, i.e. the patterns like ╤Ψ ╤Γ.╨┤. έΑεand so onέΑζ, ╨┤╨░ ╤Γ.╨┐. έΑεand so forthέΑζ are splitted into three tokens: { i , ╤Γ. , ╨┤. }, { ╨┤╨░ , ╤Γ. , ╨┐. }.
  • Email addresses, URLs, and tweet-style names are treated as single tokens: {no@mail.ru}, {https://github.com}, {@anna_li}

The Belarusian UD treebank does not contain multiword tokens.
