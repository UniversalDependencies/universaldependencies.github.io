obl:agent: agent modifier

The relation obl:agent is used for agents of passive verbs. Agents of passive verbs are nominals followed by postposition xa “by”.

Petrub ge Marias xa ge mîǁamhe . \n Petru DECL Maria by PST be_offended .
nsubj:pass(mîǁamhe, Petrub)
obl:agend(mîǁamhe, Marias)
case(Marias, xa)
nsubj:pass(be_offended, Petru)
obl:agent(be_offended, Maria)
case(Maria, by)
