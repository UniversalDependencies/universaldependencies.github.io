aux: auxiliary

An auxiliary is a functional modifier of a verb that is tagged AUX. In Cantonese this relation is used for preverbal auxiliaries as well as post-verbal aspect markers. The passive auxiliary 被 bei6 used in the formal register, however, should use aux:pass.

Preverbal auxiliaries may precede the main verb immediately (1) or before other intervening material (2).
