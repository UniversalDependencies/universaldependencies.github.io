RelType: type of relative participle

This marks type of relative participle.

Abs: Absolutive

Examples

  • [abq] й-шв-тахъ-х-у [REL.ABS-2PL.IO-want-RE-PRS.NFIN] ‘[the thing] that you want’ Number[io]=Plur|Person[io]=2|RelType=Abs|Tense=Pres|VerbForm=NonFin

Erg: Ergative

Examples

  • [abq] с-а-з-ры-пхьа-ры-з [1SG.ABS-3SG.N.IO-REL.ERG-CAUS-study-FUT.NFIN-PST.NFIN] ‘[the person] who would teach me’ Caus=Yes|Gender[io]=Neut|Number[abs]=Sing|Number[io]=Sing|Person[abs]=1|Person[io]=3|RelType=Erg|Tense=Subj2|VerbForm=NonFin

Io: Indirect object

Examples

  • [abq] з-ла-чважва-уа [REL.IO-INS-speak-IPF] ‘[the language] that they speak’

Pr: Possessor

Examples

  • [abq] з-къвырман-у [REL.PR-sacrifice-PRS.NFIN] ‘whose sacrifice is …’ RelType=Pr|Tense=Pres|VerbForm=NonFin

Tmp: Temporal

Examples

  • [abq] д-ан-гIа-й-х [3SG.H.ABS-REL.TMP-CSL-come-RE] ‘when (s)he came back’ Gender[abs]=Com|Number[abs]=Sing|Person[abs]=3|RelType=Tmp|Tense=Aor|VerbForm=NonFin

Rsn: Reason

Examples

  • [abq] й-зы-л-гвал-и-щд [3SG.N.ABS-REL.RSN-3SG.F.IO-hope-QN-EMP] ‘why she wanted it’ Gender[abs]=Neut|Gender[io]=Fem|Int=Yes|Number[abs]=Sing|Number[io]=Sing|Person[abs]=3|Person[io]=3|RelType=Rsn|VerbForm=NonFin

Mnr: Manner

Examples

  • [abq] й-ш-с-чпа-уа [3SG.N.ABS-REL.MNR-1SG.ERG-do-IPF] ‘how I do it’ Gender[abs]=Neut|Number[abs]=Sing|Number[erg]=Sing|Person[abs]=3|Person[erg]=1|RelType=Mnr

Loc: Locative

Examples

  • [abq] й-ъа-та-з [3PL.ABS-REL.LOC-reside-PST.NFIN] ‘where they lived’ Number[abs]=Plur|Person[abs]=3|RelType=Loc|Tense=Past|VerbForm=NonFin
