advcl:cmpr: comparative clause

The advcl:cmpr relation is used in the Polish PDB-UD treebank for comparative expressions realised as clauses. Comparative expressions can also be realised as phrases (obl:cmpr) in Polish. Comparative expressions are preceded by a comparative conjunction either niż (“than”) in comparatives of inequality or e.g. jak (“like”) in comparatives of equality.
