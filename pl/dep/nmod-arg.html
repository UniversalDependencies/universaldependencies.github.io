nmod:arg: required nominal dependent of a noun

The nmod:arg relation is used in the Polish PDB-UD and PUD treebanks for nominal dependents required by nouns. The governing nouns are often derived from verbs, but they are not gerunds, e.g. zmiana (“a change”) is derived from zmieniać (“to change”).
