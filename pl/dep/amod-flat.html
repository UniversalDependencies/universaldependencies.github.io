amod:flat: adjectival part of a named entity

The amod:flat relation is used in the Polish PDB-UD and PUD treebanks for adjectival parts (ADJ) of named entities, e.g. organisation names with clear syntactic structure, dates. These adjectival parts of named entities are not proper names (PROPN). Their combinations with other words (PROPN and non-PROPN) build named entities.
