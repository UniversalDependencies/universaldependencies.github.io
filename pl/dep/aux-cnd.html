aux:cnd: conditional marker auxiliaries

The aux:cnd relation is used in the Polish PDB-UD and PUD treebanks for the conditional particle by. Regardless of whether it is attached to a verb form (pseudo particles) or it stands alone, it depends on the verb form in a dependency tree.
