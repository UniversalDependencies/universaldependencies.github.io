Treebank Statistics (UD_Polish)

This feature is universal. It occurs with 5 different values: Fin, Ger, Inf, Part, Trans.

14919 tokens (18%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 7764 types (33%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 3318 lemmas (25%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm.
