Subord: subordinate

Values: Yes

In Tekó, Subord is marked with suffixes -nam/-nawe/-nate/-zepe.

-Nam/-nawe/-nate introduces temporal clauses, while the subordinate -nate seems to express a specific time. Additionally, the subordinate -nam (sometimes_-na) marks conditional clauses and is often used with the irrealis particle _-itʃe.

The concessive particle -zepe/-epe indicates that the propositional content contrasts with what is described in the main clause.

Yes: Yes

Examples

  • en92anam , Polorekojaram adʒu  “In 92, I became Paulo’s replacement.”
  • nanipamẽitʃe zadʒapɨakanam…  “If everyone thought so…”
  • 10heuresaʔenawe oker  “At 10, they went to sleep.”
  • munuʔaŋanate ohẽhem ohoŋ  “In the afternoon, they leave.”
  • sidʒeminanãzepe omãʔẽnẽ nõderehe  “Even if we hide, he still sees us.”
