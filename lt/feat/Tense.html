Treebank Statistics (UD_Lithuanian)

This feature is universal but the values PastIter, PastSimp, PresHab are language-specific. It occurs with 6 different values: Fut, Past, PastIter, PastSimp, Pres, PresHab.

618 tokens (12%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 445 types (19%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 281 lemmas (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense.
