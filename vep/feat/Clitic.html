Clitic: clitic

Language-specific feature identifying clitics attached to the word.

Se: -se

Definite article.

Examples

  • [vep] kaikse elo “this all life”

Ik: -ik

Expresses a question.

Examples

  • [vep] voinik minä “can I ?”

Ki: -ki

Expresses focus. Can often be translated into English as also.

Examples

  • [vep] venänki kelel “also in Russian”
