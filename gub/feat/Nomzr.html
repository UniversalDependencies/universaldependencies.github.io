Nomzr: nominalizer

Values: Circ Pas DevPass Ag Abs

Circ: Circumstance

The nominalizer of circumstance is expressed with the suffix “haw”

Examples

  • py-haw  “night (time of darkness)”
  • yryk-haw  “river (the way it flows)”

</tr> </table>

Ag: Agent

The nominalizer of circumstance is expressed with the suffix “har”

Examples

  • he-mu’e-har  “teacher (the person who teaches me)”

</tr> </table>

Pas: Passive

The nominalizer of deverbal passive is expressed with the suffix “pyr”

Examples

  • imuzàg-pyr  “what is to be produced”

</tr> </table>

DevPass: Passive Deverbal

The nominalizer of deverbal passive is expressed with the prefix “emi”

Examples

  • he-r-emi-apo kwer  “what I did”

</tr> </table>

Abs: Absolute

The nominalizer is expressed with the sufix “maʔe”

Examples

  • u-ze-apo-maʔe kwer  “what had happened”

</tr> </table>
