cc: coordination

cc is the relation between a conjunct and a preceding CCONJ (coordinating conjunction). CCONJ is a word that links words or larger constituents without syntactically subordinating one to the other and expresses a semantic relationship between them. In Pomak, as CCONJ are marked i “and”, ta “and”, íli “or”, as well as the adversatives ála “but”, pak “but” and am “but”:


magáreso je stáro i jáko
"the donkey is old and strong" 
cc (jáko, i)     
conj (stáro, jáko)    

hódih ta go pýtah 
"I went and asked him"
cc (pýtah, ta)
conj (hódih, pýtah)  

magáreso je stáro íli  jáko 
"the donkey is (either) old or strong"                                      
cc (jáko, íli)     
conj (stáro, jáko) 

magáreso je stáro alá  jáko
"the donkey is old but strong"
cc (jáko, ála)
conj (stáro, jáko)  

magáreso je stáro pak  jáko
"the donkey is old but strong"   
cc (jáko, pak)
conj (stáro, jáko)  

am ty, kaná só razhóždaš nacýj-nasám?
lit: but you, what yourself stroll that way - this way?
"but you, why do you stroll back and forth?"
cc (razhóždaš, am)
