Person[psor]: person of possessor

Number[psor] feature captures the possessor’s person. Predmetning uch shaxsdan biriga tegishli ekanligini bildirgan kategoriyaga egalik qo’shimchasi deyiladi. Ish-harakatning soʻzlovchi nuqtai nazaridan uning bajaruvchisiga aloqadorligini ifodalovchi, oʻzaro qarama-qarshi shakllar tizimidan iborat grammatik kategoriya o‘zbek tili grammatikasida shaxs-son deb tushuniladi. Shaxs shakllari ish-harakatning soʻzlovchiga (1-Sh.), tinglovchiga (2-Sh.) va suhbatda ishtirok etmovchi oʻzga shaxsga (3-Sh.) qarashliligini biddiradi.

Shaxs Birlik Ko‘plik
Unlidan so‘ng Undoshdan so‘ng Unlidan so‘ng Undoshdan so‘ng
I -m -im -miz -imiz
II -ng -ing -ngiz -ingiz
III -si -i -lari

1: first person/birinchi shaxs (birlik-ko‘plik)

Examples

  • maktabim “my school”
  • maktablarim “my schools”
  • maktabimiz “our school”
  • maktablarimiz “our schools

2: second person

Examples

  • maktabing “your school”
  • maktablaring “your schools”
  • maktabingiz “your school”
  • maktablaringiz “your schools”

3: third person

Examples

  • maktabi “his/her school””
  • maktablari “his/her schools””
  • maktabi “their school””
  • maktablari “their schools”
